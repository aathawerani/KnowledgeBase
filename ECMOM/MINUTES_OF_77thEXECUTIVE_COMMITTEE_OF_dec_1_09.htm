MINUTES OF THE 77th MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON TUESDAY, DECEMBER 1, 2009

 

The 77th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Tuesday, December 1, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA, Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.   Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Associate Dean (Acting Director)

2.   Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar

3.   Mr. Ali Taha                                        Director Finance

4.   Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                             Director ICT

5.   Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director HR

6.   Mr. Shameem Ahmed                         Director HEC Projects

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1     To consider and approve (a) IBA Staff Welfare Fund, and (b) Pre-employment Medical Examination.

 

a)         IBA Staff Welfare Fund:

 

1.1              The Director H.R. stated that the Rules & Regulations relating to IBA Staff Welfare Fund were emailed to the Executive Committee for their comments / approval. However, she said that no comments were received. She requested the Executive Committee to consider the rules and regulations and accord approval.

 

1.2              Making clarification of clause (d) of the Eligibility criteria i.e. “Employee does not owe any previous loan to the Institute“, the Director H.R stated that those employees who have availed loan from Provident Fund or Welfare Fund will not be eligible for the Staff Welfare Loan until they repay / clear the loan already availed.

 

1.3              About clause (b) of the Procedure related to guarantor, Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri wanted to know as to how many applications an employee can be guarantor for at one time.     

 

1.4              The Director H.R. clarified that one regular / permanent employee who has at least three years remaining service to retire may be guarantor for one applicant only at a time.

 

DECISION

 

1.5              The Committee recommended the IBA Staff Welfare Fund as presented by the Director H.R. for approval by the A&FC.

 

ACTION

 

1.6              The Director H.R would present the recommended IBA Staff Welfare Fund to the A&FC for approval.

            b)         Pre-employment Medical Examination

 

1.7              The Director H.R. stated that the Rules & Regulations relating to Pre-employment of Medical Examination were emailed to the Executive Committee for their comments / approval. However, as no comments were received, the Executive Committee may discuss the rules and regulations or accord approval.

 

1.8              The Director Finance wanted to know as to what would be the impact of the medical test result if a candidate is found to be a diabetic patient.

 

1.9              The Director H.R. clarified that a candidate being a diabetic patient will not be rejected on this ground.

 

DECISION

 

1.10          The Committee approved the rules and regulations for Pre-employment Medical Examination as presented by the Director H.R.

 

ACTION

 

1.11          The Director H.R. would get the approved rules and regulations on Pre-employment Medical Examination implemented.

  

 

Item No. 2      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Location of Cafeteria Shed at City Campus.

 

2.1              The Registrar informed the Committee that the Coordinator, City Campus requested to shift the Cafeteria Shed at City Campus closer to the northern wall of the Campus. But he said that because of the tree over there, it is creating problem in the construction of the shed. He said that he had discussed the matter with the Director and got his approval for cutting the said tree. The Registrar clarified that the shed would be a temporary structure.

 

2.2              Mr. Shameem Ahmed pointed out that the cable and sewerage lines are passing through the proposed way.  He requested that while constructing the shed, the cables should not be disturbed as, according to him, the shifting of the services line will be an expensive, arduous and time consuming job.  He suggested that any digging required be done under on-the-spot supervision of someone who is fully aware of the routes of the services lines.

 

DECISION

 

2.3              The Committee decided that the existing separation of over 12 feet between the northern wall and the shed be reduced and the shed to be relocated as close as possible to the northern wall of the campus by cutting down the tree obstructing the cafeteria shed movement towards the northern wall. The Committee further decided that a tree be planted and nurtured in the lawn area in lieu of the tree to be cut.

2.4              The Committee also decided that the fibre optic and utility ducts / pits passing through the shed area be re-routed around the periphery of the shed without interruption of the services.

 

ACTION

 

2.5              The Registrar would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mr. Hanif Daud of M/s Icon for necessary action.

 

 

b)     Location of the Foundation Pillar of the overhead bridge at the City Campus.

 

2.6              The Registrar presented the pictures of the site being dug for the foundation pillar of the overhead bridge connecting the City Campus building and the APWA building. He said that the foundation pillar would be blocking the gate.  He said that there may be three options; one option is to re-design the 50 ton bridge but there is a possibility that it may not be done. The second option is to shift the gate to the right and the third option is that the size of the gate may be reduced.

 

2.7              Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that the space left after the construction of the foundation pillar is sufficient enough for the vehicles to pass through the gate.  He suggested that since it is a temporary arrangement – for three years or so – the foundation pillar be allowed to be raised where it is being proposed to be erected.

 

DECISION

 

2.8              The Committee agreed to the suggestion of Dr. Sayeed Ghani that the foundation pillar should remain where it is being erected.    

 

c)      Up-date on the Convocation 2009 arrangements.

 

2.9              The Registrar giving up-date on the Convocation arrangements stated that a total of 466 students were eligible for the degree out of which 314 have registered themselves for the Convocation. He said that out of these 314, forty four are fee defaulters. He said that the Director has desired that the parents of fee defaulters be written to clear the fee before the Convocation and that their wards may not be allowed to appear in the Convocation if they do not clear the dues.

 

2.10          The Registrar suggested that as all full-time faculty members are required to be part of the Academic Procession and owing to constraints of time and space associated with venue preparation as well as timely availability of personnel and their transportation, no classes be scheduled at both the campuses on convocation day i.e. Saturday, December 12, 2009. He requested the Acting Director and the Associate Dean to decide when the classes may be rescheduled.

 

DECISION

2.11          The Director Finance was requested to write to the parents of the fee defaulter students for clearing the dues.

 

2.12          The status of fee defaulters will be reviewed again in the next meeting of the Executive Committee.

 

2.13          Decision on rescheduling of classes will be taken in the next EC meeting on December 7, 2009.

 

 

d)     Number of Pre-qualified contractors for Maintenance

 

2.14          The Registrar informed the Committee that Mr. Moiz Hassanali, the then Head of Internal Audit of IBA had observed that the list of pre-qualified contractors for maintenance was very short and had advised to re-advertise for this.  The Registrar reported that according to the advice of the then Head of Internal Audit, it was re-advertised for pre-qualified contractors and out of 17 applications received, 14 have been selected / pre-qualified.

 

DECISION

 

2.15          Noted and approved by the Committee.

 

e)      Up-date on IBA Code Book.

 

2.16          Dr. Sayeed Ghani requested the Director H.R. to up-date the Committee on the IBA Code Book.

 

2.17          The Director H.R. stated that information from all have been received and Dr. Muhammad Nishat is working on his case.

 

DECISION

 

2.18          The up-date on the IBA Code Book given by the Director H.R. was noted by the Committee.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
