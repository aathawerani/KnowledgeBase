MINUTES OF THE 103RD MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON TUESDAY, MAY 25, 2010

 

The 103rd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Tuesday, May 25, 2010 at 9:30 a.m. at the Conference Room, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                           Associate Dean

  2. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar

  3. Mr. Mobin Khalili                             Controller of Examinations

  4. Mr. Ali Taha                                  Director Finance

  5. Mr. Shameem Ahmed                       Acting Director Projects

  6. Dr. Arshad. Siddiqi                          Director ICT

  7. Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                       Director CED

  8. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                       Director H.R.

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve Student Financial Guidelines.

 

1.1              The Director Finance presented the highlights of the Student Financial Guidelines and invited comments from the members.

 

DECISION

 

1.2              The Committee requested the Director Finance to send the Student Financial Guidelines (SFG) to Program Directors, Program Coordinators, Chairpersons, and Patrons of Societies for their comments and after incorporating the comments, if any, give the SFG to all students.

 

 

Item No. 2      To discuss the implementation of CMS on new ERP.

 

2.1              The Director Finance said that that they have to issue notice to the students for payment of semester fee.  He wanted to know if they should run ERP parallel with existing CMS or the student be asked to register on new ERP system only.

 

2.2              Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that the technical problem is that they are using on-line registration system in which students have to specify their courses which are allocated by the system on first-come-first-served basis; any time lag may change the queuing.

 

2.3              Dr. Arshad Siddiqi said that the CMS gets crashed because of overload but with ERP they would not have such problem.  He said that summer is ideal time for verifying the efficacy of the ERP system.  He also said that students would be trained about the usage of ERP.

 

DECISION

 

2.4              The Committee decided that on-line registration for Summer Semester should be done through new ERP system.

 

    

 

 

Item No. 3      To consider nominations for reward.

 

3.1              The Director H.R. stated that the following have been nominated for the Recognition / Reward as per approved Reward / Recognition Policy:

 

i)    Mr. M. Akmal Khan                     Administrative Officer of FCS

ii)   Mr. Abrar Ahmed                         Deputy Librarian

iii)  Mr. Muhammad Jawad Khan       Electrical Engineer

 

3.2              The Director H.R. also presented the filled in nomination forms which provided the level they were nominated for, nominees’ significant contributions, etc.  She said that all the three have been recommended for Level 4 i.e. cash reward of Rs.30,000/-.

 

DECISION

 

3.3              The Committee endorsed the nominations of the above three named persons for the Recognition / Reward according to Level 4.

 

ACTION

 

3.4              The Director H.R. would report the nominations of the above named three persons to the A&FC.

 

 

Item No. 4      To consider draft proposal for personal loan.

 

4.1              The Director H.R. presented the proposal for personal loan for Option ‘A’ employees.

 

4.2              The Director Finance said that since the Welfare Fund would soon be available which is more or less similar to the proposed personal loan, they should see the mechanism of the Fund. He suggested that the proposal for personal loan should be considered at a later stage. 

 

DECISION

 

4.3              The Committee deferred the proposal for personal loan.

    

 

Item No. 5      To consider manpower requirements.

 

5.1              The Director H.R presented details of the manpower requirements for 2010-11 for various departments. She said that the program offices restructuring have also been incorporated but there is no major structural change.

 

5.2              Mr. Mobin Khalili said that they did not have independent examination department. The existing staff work for the examination as well as program offices.  He said that in the new structure the staff in the program offices will be under the umbrella of the Examination Department; they would be working exclusively for the Examination Department but will provide help to the Program Offices.  He said that the proposed structure was not the one they had agreed upon. He also said that the EMBA and the Evening Program are two different entities and they should be separately mentioned and separate staff should be allocated for them.

5.3              While discussing the manpower requirements for IBA, Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah said that they need qualified people for the library. He suggested 15 new positions for the library.

 

5.4              Dr. Arshad Siddiqi presented a requirement of 20 positions out of which he said that 13 would be essentially needed for ERP. He added that the 20 would be in replacement of the existing 20 who have been suggested to be outsourced.

 

DECISION

 

5.5              The Committee decided that:

 

a)             The Director H.R. would circulate the Program Office structure among all Program Directors, Associate Deans, and the Controller of Examinations and would convene a separate meeting to finalise the Program Office Structure.

 

b)            The Chairman, Library Committee would provide the names of 5 proposed positions they would like to be filled in 2010-11.

 

5.6              The Committee further decided that the revised manpower requirements would be reviewed by the Director before incorporating them in the draft budget 2010-11.

 

 

Item No. 6      Monthly Progress Report of Projects Department.

 

6.1              Mr. Shameem Ahmed presented monthly progress report (upto April 2010) of Projects Department.

 

DECISION

 

6.2              The Committee noted the progress report as presented by Mr. Shameem Ahmed.

 

 

Item No. 7      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Review of draft budget 2010-11

 

7.1              The Director Finance presented the draft budget 2010-11 for review.  He said that ICT has proposed $25,000 for ICT staff training abroad which includes training fee and travelling and accommodation cost. He also presented the vehicles requirements.

 

7.2              Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested to see if the training can be arranged in the region i.e. gulf/middle east.

 

7.3              Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested to make provision for Educational Software. He said that Mr. Waseem Arain is working to obtain the estimate.

 

7.4       The Committee decided to delete the HCM requirements from the PeopleSoft.

 

 

 

DECISION

 

7.5       The Director Finance would incorporate the suggestions made above.  

 

b)      Fee for Fall 2010 semester.

 

7.6       The Director Finance said that as per past practice a 10% raise in the fee has been suggested.  However, he said that they have to collect the fee before the new fee is approved by the Board.  He requested the Executive Committee to approve 10% increase in fee to be charged from the students for Fall 2010 semester.

 

DECISION

 

7.7       The Committee keeping in view the past practice, allowed the Director Finance to charge the fee with 10% increase for Fall 2010 which may be adjusted depending upon the approval of the fee by the competent authority.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
