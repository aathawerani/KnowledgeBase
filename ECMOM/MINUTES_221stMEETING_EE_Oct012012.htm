MINUTES OF 221ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, OCTOBER  01, 2012

The 221st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 1, 2012 at 3:00 p.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

Mr. Mehmood Bughio, Senior Manager Projects, and M/s. Zayed Bilgrami and Ahsan of NBCL attended the meeting on special invitation. The following items were discussed:

The following items were discussed:

Item No.1       To consider and approve the final interior design of dining halls and the kitchen of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus.

1.1       The Director Projects said that the interior design of kitchen and dining halls of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus have been revised to accommodate four kitchens and the change in use of the first floor dining hall. Also the Architects had to present the final interior finishes of the dining hall ceilings. The Architects were therefore asked to present the details of the interiors finishes for the approval of the Executive Committee. 

1.2       Mr. Zayed Bilgrami while presenting the lay-out of the roof ceiling and kitchen said that due to budget constraints of the interior budget  they have  redesigned the lay out. He said that they have removed the false ceiling of atrium, dining hall as well as gymnasium maintaining the natural and consistent feeling. He said that they have provided seating space within and outside the atrium and have used red carpet in the students’ lounge. 

1.3       The Dean & Director said that the carpet gets spoiled very soon because of heavy circulation. He suggested using maintainable flooring instead of carpet in the Students’ Lounge. He further suggested finding some cheaper solution. Mr. Zayd said that he would find some alternate including terracotta tiles in place of carpet.

 1.4       The Registrar enquired about the seating arrangements in the Atrium. Mr. Zayd informed that there are fixed wooden benches placed in the atrium. The maintenance of water body was also questioned by the Director ICT and it was also asked as to how it would be operated.

1.5       The Registrar pointed out that it is very difficult to maintain the upholstered sofa seats and suggested that there should be some fabric which should last longer and is easy to maintain. To a question from the Registrar as to who will be responsible for providing LCD. etc. in the dining hall as portrayed in the design, Mr. Zayed said that they have not included LCD. or ICT equipment in the BoQ.

1.6       The Dean & Director said that sofa’s fabric should be such that it is easily washable or cleanable. Mr. Zafar Siddiqui also suggested that the space allocated for the study tables may not be used in the lounge. He suggested that there should be benches so that the seating capacity is increased. 

1.7       About the badminton court, the Dean & Director said that it should be upto the standard specifications. Mr. Zayd said that the badminton court is similar to that of the Aga Khan. The Dean & Director said that if it is comparable with Aga Khan then it is O.K. The Registrar questioned about the maintaining and cleaning of the court area ceiling. The type of ceiling light in the badminton court was also inquired from the NBCL.

1.8       Mr. Zayed said that originally they had designed for one kitchen but as per EC’s instruction, they have divided it into four and they would be without false ceiling; the ducting would be exposed.   

1.9       The Director Projects mentioned that the chairs in the dining hall are different from the chairs as provided by NBCL in the existing dining area of Aman CED.

1.10     Mr. Zayd said that they have not prepared the visual of the kiosk as the vendors will give their own requirements of equipment layouts and utilities requirements. The Dean & Director said that he would arrange a meeting of the Registrar with Mr. Rafiq Rangoonwala so that the Registrar may obtain Rangoonwala feedback on the kitchen layouts and communicate to the Consultant. The Dean & Director also asked the Registrar to talk to Students Biryani, and Chachaji.

1.11     About paintings as shown in the design of the dining hall, the Dean & Director said that he would arrange a painting competition among the students of Arts Society of IBA. The selected paintings would be placed in the dining hall and other places. About the AC system at the Centre, which was reported that it would be Package Unit, the Dean & Director said that we should have energy efficient consumption system as one of the objectives and asked the Consultant to be very cautious as far as energy consumption is concerned.  Mr. Zayed confirmed that the system is energy efficient. 

1.12     The Director H.R. suggested that one of the counters should be of juices because students want healthy food.

1.13    The Committee discussed various choices for inviting 4 vendors for the 4 kitchens.      The Dean & Director asked the Director Projects to get feedback from the students what they want.

DECISION 

1.14     The Committee noted and approved the interior design of dining halls and the kitchen of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus along with the observations noted above.     

ACTION

1.15     The Registrar would obtain feedback from Mr. Rafiq Rangoonwala and also from Students’ Biryani, and Chachajee about the kitchen requirements.

1.16     The Director Projects would get feedback from the students what they want about the food stuff.

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
