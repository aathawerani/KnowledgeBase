Item No.2       Overtime issue of IBA employees.

 

2.1       Mr. Fahad Rehman, Head of Internal Audit presented the details of overtime claimed/done by the IBA employees. He also presented statistics of the overtime in terms of percentage of salary, number of hours, department etc. He said that on an average Rs.1.9 million to Rs.2.3 million per month is paid on account of over time but through proper monitoring it can be cut down to around 60 to 70%. He said that the Internal Audit Department had made a surprise check and found that some persons were not present but later they claimed for the overtime for that day and time.

 

2.2       The Dean & Director said that the amount of overtime in terms of total salary is not very significant but we have to curb the misuse or excessive abuse of this facility. He said that those HoDs who are approving overtime without verification are equally responsible for the misuse. He said that there are some departments, facilities management for example, whose staff have to be available to resolve any problem that arises at any time. The Dean & Director asked Mr. Fahad Rehman to circulate to the respective HoDs the list of those employees who did not meet the physical scrutiny test. The respective HoDs would assign the duty to an independent person to check and verify the presence of those employees.

 

2.3       To a suggestion that there should be some ceiling on overtime, the Dean & Director said that it should be flexible because some events; convocation for example, require a lot of work and longer hours to work. 

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.4       The Internal Audit Department would circulate to the respective HoDs the list of those employees who did not meet the physical scrutiny test. The respective HoDs would assign the duty to an independent staff of his department to check and verify the presence of the employee claiming overtime but reported to be missing.

 

Item No.3       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Use of LMS and Lecture Recording System.

 

3.1       The Dean & Director said that some faculty members are not aware of the lecture recording system. He said that we have to give orientation and training. Mr. Abdul Wajed suggested that on sample basis the recording link can be circulated among the faculty so that they may get to know of the facilities available at IBA and how they can be used.

 

3.2       The Dean & Director referring to use of LMS specially by FBA visiting faculty requested Dr. Nishat to ask the Department Assistants to help the faculty members in using LMS. It was suggested that it should be made compulsory for every TA to scan the chapter and upload. Dr. Nishat said that some TAs are doing it but this practice should be made mandatory for every TA.
