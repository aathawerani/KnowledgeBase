The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       To consider request from IBA employees for Eid Bonus.

 

1.1   The Committee considered the requests from IBA employees for Eid Bonus as announced in The Current of 2011 as well as the suggestion of the Dean & Director to grant one time 60th Anniversary bonus to all employees in Grades 1 to 15 and their equivalent IBA scale employees as a token of appreciation for the services they have rendered to the Institute.

 

1.2   Mr. Adnan Hameed suggested that since this bonus is given as a token of appreciation to employees, it should not be linked with employees’ basic pay and also should not be given to employees in BPS 1-15 and equivalent IBA scales only.  He suggested that instead of Basic Pay, bonus at a flat rate of Rs.10,000/- should be given to all non-teaching staff of IBA irrespective of their pay scale or their equivalent grade.  He added that the financial impact of both these schemes will not have any significant difference.

 

DECISION

 

1.3       Based on the opinion of the majority of Committee members, the EC agreed to the suggestion of the Dean & Director to grant one-time bonus as a token of appreciation on account of 60th Anniversary bonus. 

 

1.4       The bonus scheme proposed by the Director Finance was also approved as one basic salary to all BPS employees in Grades 1 to 15; while the bonus for IBA Scale employees will be paid on the basis of average basic salaries of the equivalent BPS grades employees.

 

ACTION

 

1.5       The Director Finance would ensure that the payment is made before Eid.

 

1.6       The Head of HR would convey the decision to all concerned. 

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
