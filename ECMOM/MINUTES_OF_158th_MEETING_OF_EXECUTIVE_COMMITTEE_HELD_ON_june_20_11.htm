1.2              The Director ICT said that Dr. Shakeel Khoja wants to know as to why IBA should go for the certification.

 

1.3              Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that it has already been decided that they would not go for certification.

 

1.4       The Director Projects said that the paper has been submitted just for the information of the EC.  It should first be sent to the Technical Evaluation Committee - Projects (TEC-Projects) and the Technical Evaluation Committee – ICT (TEC-ICT), who should evaluate and forward their recommendations to the Central Phase Committee (CPC). The recommendation of the CPC would then be presented to the EC.  He further said that the Data Centre consists of two parts; one is that of services like air-conditioning, fire suppression system, fire fighting, CCTV system, access control system, electrical system, dehumidification etc., while the second part is that of hardware installations. He said that the design of hardware was done by the ICT Department and the hardware has been purchased accordingly. Projects department got the design and tender documents completed of the data centre services by ICON and their Consulting team under their existing contract as per the required criteria of Tier 3 as desired by ICT department. The designs and the tender documents were duly scrutinized by the ICT and were duly approved. (The Director ICT did not agree to this). In a meeting of Executive Committee there was a dispute on the quality of the design and tender documents by the ICT department because of which the Dean & Director had suggested to hire an independent Data Centre Consultant.

 

1.5       Dr. Sayeed Ghani observed that the consultancy fee for Tier 3 Data Centre quoted by the new consultant is also very high. The Director ICT informed that ICT is working on the quotation with them.

 

1.6       The Director ICT informed the Committee that H.K. Shah is the only Consultant who qualified the bid; another one was technically disqualified.

 

1.7       Mr. Zafar Siddiqui wanted to know if any amount for consultancy and tier 3 consultancy has been included in the budget. 

 

1.8       The Director Projects replied that the original design fee is part of the budget but the fee of the new consultants is not. He stated that such a high fee was never expected.

 

1.9       The Director Projects reported that PTA is willing to offer their system to IBA and in this connection they want to meet the Dean & Director. Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that using PTA system is not advisable. He suggested to know as to how much consultants charge for similar projects in Pakistan. The Director ICT corrected that it is PTCL and not PTA.

 

1.10     The Registrar wanted to know on what basis the data centre equipments were ordered.  The Director ICT replied that the data centre equipments were ordered without any consultancy. He reported that TIA-924 Certification includes 2600 points which may be available on the net.

 

1.11     The Registrar said that if 2600 points required for TIA 294 Certification are available on the net then they should use that as a guide and do it internally.

 

1.12     The Director ICT said that he along with the Director Projects can do this.  He also said that he would not like to spend huge money on hiring consultant if it can be done internally, however with the limited technical expertise in this area it is not possible.

 

1.13     Mr Zafar Siddiqi suggested that the original designer should also be consulted in this regard.

 

DECISION

 

1.14     In view of the high cost of the Consultant, the Committee suggested to rework the proposal.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
