MINUTES OF 233RD MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, JANUARY 14, 2013


The 234th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, January 21, 2013 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                       Dean & Director
2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                Associate Dean, FBA
3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                      Associate Dean, FCS
4. Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN       Registrar
5. Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                 Director CED
6. Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                     Director ICT
7. Mr. Moeid Sultan                      Acting Director Finance

The following items were discussed:


Item No. 1 To consider an application from Ms. Haniya S. Channah, a student of EMBA, for make-up exam.

1.1 Ms. Haniya, a student of EMBA in her application reported that she could not sit for the Accounts Paper on December 9, 2012 due to severe back pain and inflammation. She had attached medical certificate from the Aga Khan University Hospital in support of her statement. She requested for a make-up examination for the paper in which she could not appear due to hospitalization.

DECISION

1.2 The Committee requested Dr. Mohammad Nishat to look into the case and if he is satisfied with the claim of Ms. Haniya, take action accordingly and inform the EC of the action taken by him.


Item No. 2 Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

a) To consider request from Ms. Priya Bhojwani, student of BBA for putting her Fall Semester 2012 on hold.

2.1 Ms. Priya Bhojwani, student of BBA-I requested for putting her Fall Semester 2012 on hold for the reason that her father had a surgery for CA colon cancer at South City, Karachi, in 2011 due to which his health has been fluctuating for past month. She further reported that her mother has also not been well and the doctor has recommended her bed rest. She reported that her studies were being affected due to her preoccupation with her parents’ health. She stated that currently there is no one old enough in her family to look after her parents so she would have to go back to Nawabshah immediately.


DECISION

2.2 The Committee approved the request of Ms. Priya to put her fall semester 2012 on hold. However, the Committee decided that Ms. Priya will have to repeat the whole courses of Fall Semester 2012 when she rejoins.

ACTION

2.3 The Registrar would convey the decision to Ms. Priya as well as the Program Office.


There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
