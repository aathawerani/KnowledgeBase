d) Direct purchase of cables for electrical infrastructure works at IBA Main Campus.

 

4.24     The Director Projects reported that as per the quotation from market the ex-factory cost of total cable given in the BOQ for electrical infrastructure works at IBA Main Campus, prepared by the consultants came to Rs.12.95 million. The quotation was given by Pakistan Cables to a bidder who is participating in the tender competition. (The cost is without transportation and overhead and profit of the contractors. The bids are due in first week of March 2012.)  He said that the Dean and Director was requested to ask Pakistan Cable Management to give a special discount to IBA. Accordingly, the Dean and Director contacted the Pakistan Cables management, who in turn sent their rates offering 21.5% rebate on ex-factory price, quoting a total amount as Rs.10.173 million. He said that based on the quoted price of Pakistan Cable following analysis was done by Projects Department to arrive at the savings if the cable is directly purchased from Pakistan Cables:

 

Cables by contractor in tender                                  If cable Purchased directly by IBA

 

Cost of cable = Rs.12.95 including 16% GST              Cable cost=Rs.10.17including 16% GST

 

Overhead Profit =30% of 12.95=3.9 million                Overhead and Profit=Nil

say Rs.4 million

           

Any wastage is on contractors’ account                   Minimum 5% wastage on IBA account

                                                                        ie. Rs. 0.5 million

 

Total cost will be =16.95 say Rs.17 million                Total cost=10.673 say Rs.10.7 million

 

4.25     The Director Projects said that by comparison of above two costs, we can save an amount of Rs.6.3 million in case of direct purchase. However, he said that the final calculations of savings can only be done after receiving the bids from the contractors and the pricing at the time when direct order is placed on Pakistan Cables which is subject to certain conditions which include 50% advance payment also.

 

DECISION

 

4.26     The Committee considering that Pakistan Cable is the single source and there is a saving of around Rs.6 million, approved the direct purchase from Pakistan Cable.
