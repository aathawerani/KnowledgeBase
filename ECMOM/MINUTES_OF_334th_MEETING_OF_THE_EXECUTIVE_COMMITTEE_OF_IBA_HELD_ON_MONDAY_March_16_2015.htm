The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       To consider the proposal for the purchase of a Solar Car for IBA.

 

1.1       The Registrar said that the last five years have seen substantial development of buildings and facilities at the Main Campus. He said that as of now we have 9 premises sprawling an area of about 5 acres which we proudly show to all invited guests and visitors. A typical campus visit starts and ends at the Fauji Building; the round trip is about 2 km – excluding the distance which the visitors travel while touring the individual buildings / facilities. It is also for fact that barring the ring road which is black top, two way all weather motor-able, bulk of the pathways are paver finished and designed for pedestrian traffic; thus  plying of vehicles (often heavy VIP vehicles such as pajeros) is not advisable.

               

1.2       In the light of the above and with a view to promote better presentation of our facilities, make the visit less cumbersome for the visitors; the Registrar recommended that approval may be given to purchase one solar car / electric car similar to the one in use at the AKU and other large facilities. He said that while Mr. Mushtaq Ahmed was in Islamabad to attend a workshop, he came across with this car.

 

1.3       Mr. Mushtaq Ahmed said that the Manufacturer has already sold this car to Lahore Golf Club and now they are in the process of supplying the car to Islamabad Navy HQ.  He said that there is no operational cost except for battery which is replaced after 3 year; and the maintenance cost is also very nominal because of absence of any engine.

 

1.4       The Dean & Director said that as the campus is not so big and all buildings are in close proximity there is no need of having a car for the visitors to show them the buildings and facilities around the campus.  Moreover, the number of visitors who could be shown the buildings and facilities is not so significant. He further said that since the whole area would be paved, nobody would be allowed to move on car.

 

DECISION

 

1.5       The Committee did not feel the need to buy a Solar Car for the purposes as identified as such the proposal was not accepted.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)                  Input for Program Announcement – Fee Structure.

 

2.1       The Dean & Director requested the EC members to send their required input for the Program Announcement to the Registrar.  Mr. Moeid Sultan said that he wanted to discuss the fee structure before giving it for the Program Announcement. The Dean & Director said that they have to reduce the fee differentials between BS and BBA programs. He said that there is no need to subsidise the fee of BS programs of IBA in which the number of applicants is rising. He said that if any student cannot pay the fee, he / she has the option to apply for the scholarship.

 

2.2       The Dean & Director said that the existing students will continue to pay the same fee which they had paid on their admission plus the normal annual increase which is usually around 10% with Board’s approval. However, for the new entrants of BS Programs the fee should be higher than the existing fee and with the gradual increase in fee over a period of four years it should be the same as that of BBA program.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.3       The Committee decided to recommend to the BoG to gradually increase the fee of BS Programs to be charged from the new entrants so that the fees of BS as well as BBA programs are equalized over a period of four years.

 

b)                  TAs Monitoring and compliance of Policy.

 

2.4       The Dean & Director said that TAs salary and their number are increasing but the feedback on tutorial is not so encouraging.  He said that they are paying to MS and PhD students even then the number of TAs and RAs is increasing  He requested the Associate Deans to monitor them and use program office to check if tutorials are being given or not.

 

2.5       Mr. Moeid Sultan said that the policies compliance should also be checked which is not being done in its true spirit. The Dean & Director said that Mr. Fahad Rahman, the Head of Internal Audit could be asked to check their SOP and actual outcome and find where the weaknesses are. However, he said that before the end of this semester the Associate Deans should get the information as to how many TAs are there and how they are being utilized; how many of them are giving tutorials. He advised the Associate Deans to strengthen internal control and SOP before the Internal Audit would be asked to look into it.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.6       The Associate Deans would get information as to how many TAs are there and how they are being utilized; how many of them are giving tutorials, and strengthen the internal control and SOP.

 

c)                  Research output.

 

2.7       The Dean & Director said that the progress towards research and publication is not so encouraging and the IBA’s score on this dimension in the HEC ranking may not be high again. He requested the Associate Deans to review comment and implement in conjunction with the Director CBER the proposal he had sent to the Research and Publication Committee through email. Dr. Sayeed Ghani reported that on every Friday morning they have a slot from 10 a.m. to 12 noon on research presentation.  The Dean & Director appreciated this initiative.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.8       The Associate Deans would monitor the research work of their respective faculties.  

 

d)                  Shift in Focus of Executive Committee.

 

2.9       The Dean & Director said that since the projects are almost complete, now the focus of EC will be on control on operational expenditures instead of approval of expenditure on projects. He said that the EC has to focus on cost savings and improvement of weak areas for which no proper agenda would be required.

 

2.10     Mr. Moeid Sultan said that to this end the A&FC has suggested taking and noting meter reading on daily basis on a log book along with the daily usage details / reasons for comparison purposes. Mr. Moeid Sultan said that Building Incharge would be assigned this duty.  The Dean & Director said that Mr. Zahid Bashir has also suggested that in his office they switch on the air conditioners one hour after opening hour and shut off the air conditioners one hour before closing time. In this manner they have been able to contain their energy cost without any great inconvenience. The Dean & Director suggested that the respective Buildings Incharge should be asked to follow it.

 

2.11     The Registrar suggested that since there are lesser agenda items for the EC meetings, the EC meeting should be held fortnightly instead of weekly.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.12     The Registrar would instruct the respective building incharge to take and note meter readings on daily basis on a log book of their respective buildings. 

 

2.13     The EC meetings henceforth would be held fortnightly at Aman Tower, IBA City Campus. 

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
