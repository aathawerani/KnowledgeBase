MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON JUNE 9, 2008

A meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, June 9, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus.  The following were present:

  1. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Professor
  2. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Chairman, MIS & CS
  3. Mr. Inayat N. Din                              Registrar
  4. Mr. Mobin Khalili                                Controller of Examinations
  5. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                        H.R. Manager
  6. Mr. Moeid Sultan                               Acting Head of Finance & Accounts

Dr. Nasir Tauheed and Mr. Azam Sardar, Manager IT attended the meeting by special invitation.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      PABX Installation Project at IBA City Campus.

Mr. Azam Sardar, Manager I.T. in his note of June 4, 2008 sought approval with regard to placing of PABX/hardware either at the Server Room or in the Operator Room, placement of MDF, Operator Console, management of PABX Hardware by I.T. Department, Imparting training of I.T. support staff for future manageability / troubleshooting of PABX, Dial plan (extensions/user list with rights features), extension in timeline given for project deployment etc.     

DECISION:

1.         The Committee decided that the PABX should be placed in the Server Room and the I.T. Department will be entirely responsible for its upkeep. Mr. Azam Sardar will provide staffing requirements for its management and maintenance (one for the morning hours and one for the evening shift would be desirable).

2.         The Committee would recommend additional cost if the Vendor justifies the additional cost for cabling etc.

3.         The Purchase Department should send the draft tender documents to the concerned department for review / approval.

Item No. 2      Number of Courses Offered to Visiting Faculty in the Summer 2008.

Mr. Inayat N. Din pointed out that out of 39 courses being offered in Summer 2008, 14 are being conducted by the Visiting Faculty. He wanted to know as to why part-time faculty has been engaged while full time faculty is available. He said that one part-time faculty has been assigned three courses in the Summer Semester. He said that some faculty members just to avoid allocation of any course in the Summer, take leave for 10 to 15 days towards the start of the Summer semester and thus do nothing till the Fall Semester starts.

Dr. Sayeed Ghani said part-time faculty is hired when full-time faculty is not available and the courses are allocated to those faculty members who do not do any research in the Summer semester.

DECISION:

It was decided that a guideline will be prepared by Dr. Muhammad Nishat by the end of June 2008 on this issue and presented to the Committee for approval.

Item No. 3      Proposal for Starting Afternoon Sections of BBA at the Main Campus.

Prof. Dr. Nasir Touheed in his note of April 3, 2008 stated that although there is an MBA (Evening Program) yet there is no BBA evening program for the deserving and brilliant students who are refused admission only because of the limited space available in the morning BBA program. He proposed that the IBA Main Campus facilities be used for conducting classes of two BBA sections (each of 60 students) from 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. and may be called “Afternoon Program”. He proposed that the teachers engaged for this program may be paid on hourly basis. He also presented the proposed fee structure, recurring charges, and payment to faculty and staff.     

DECISION:

The Committee appreciated the idea of introducing Afternoon Sections of BBA at the Main Campus presented by Dr. Nasir Touheed. However, due to shortage of quality faculty, the Committee felt that the first priority should be faculty development and thus deferred the proposal.

There being no other business the meeting terminated.
