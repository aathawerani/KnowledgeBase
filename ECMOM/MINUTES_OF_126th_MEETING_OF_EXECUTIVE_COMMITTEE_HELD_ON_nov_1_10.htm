Item No. 4      To consider and approve appointment of Projects Managers for Detailed Supervision and Project Management services of new Academic Block at Main Campus.

 

4.1       The Director Projects reported that a meeting of the Technical Evaluation Committee was held on October 27, 2010 to discuss and decide the appointment of Project Managers for Detailed Supervision and Project Management Services of New Academic Block at IBA Main Campus.  He said that he briefed about the methodology adopted for scoring of the Project Managers. Eight Project Managers submitted their Technical Proposals.  Out of 100, the points scored by the eight Project Managers are mentioned as under:

 

a)      M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited          92 Points         

b)      M/s. Consult-Tech International                  89 Points         

c)      M/s. Shahzad Associates                            72 Points         

d)      M/s. MM Pakistan (Private) Limited               92 Points         

e)      M/s. NESPAK                                            91 Points         

f)        M/s. INTEG                                             88 Points         

g)      M/s. Arif Shah Associates                           76 Points         

h)      M/s. Shankland COX Limited                        85 Points         

 

4.2       The Director Projects reported that after the evaluation of their Technical Proposals, the Financial Proposals were opened in the presence of the Technical Evaluation Committee. He said that the financial proposal of M/s. Shahzad Associates was not opened as they scored less than 75 Points and were not able to pass the minimum technical criteria. The rates quoted by the seven Project Managers were as under:

 

a)      M/s. AAA Partnership (Pvt.) Ltd.       3.33% of the total cost of Project         

b)      M/s. Consult-Tech International        3.68% of the total cost of Project

c)      M/s. MM Pakistan (Pvt.) Ltd.            5.06% of the total cost of Project         

d)      M/s. NESPAK                                  3.50% of the total cost of Project         

e)      M/s. INTEG                                    3.00% of the total cost of Project

f)        M/s. Arif Shah Associates               3.75% of the total cost of Project

g)      M/s. Shankland COX Limited             14.48% of the total cost of Project

        

4.3       The Director Projects stated that at the stage of final evaluation, the options given by M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited & M/s. MM Pakistan (Private) Limited were not considered in order to have a true comparison of all the bids as per the requirement of RFP.  However, he said that in order to save time, they asked the Project Managers to send them the quotations in lump sum amount for the review of Tender Drawings, Tender Documents including Specifications, Conditions of Contract and BOQ as well. The Executive Committee in its 122nd meeting held on 4th October, 2010 approved the recommendation of the Director Projects for award of work of tender drawings, tender documents including specifications, conditions of contract and BOQ of Detailed Supervision and Project Management of New Academic Block at IBA Main Campus to M/s. Consult-Tech International being the lowest at the amount of Rs.375,000/- or 0.17% of the total cost of the Project.  This amount i.e. 0.17% has been deducted from each financial proposal.

 

4.4       The Director Projects stated that as per the Comparative Statement, M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited have a combined valuation of 92 points and obtained 1st Rank. He recommended that M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited be considered for award of work at a rate of 3.16% of the total cost of the Project.  He said that on approval by the EC, it would be presented to the CPC.

 

DECISION

 

4.5       The Committee approved the recommendation for appointment of M/s. AAA Partnership (Pvt.) Limited as Projects Managers for Detailed Supervision and Project Management services of new Academic Block at Main Campus at a rate of 3.16%.

 

ACTION

 

4.6       The Director Projects would present the proposal to the CPC for approval.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
