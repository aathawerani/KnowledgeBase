MINUTES OF THE 128TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, NOVEMBER 15, 2010

The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider Status Reports on EC decisions:

 

1.1              The Committee considered the status reports on the EC decisions presented by the following:

 

DIRECTOR FINANCE:

 

1.2              Up-date on the Task Force on student loan: The Director Finance reported that there was no progress as the matter is pending between the State Bank of Pakistan and the HEC.

ASSOCIATE DEAN, FCS:

CONTROLLER OF EXAMINATIONS:

DECISION

Item No. 2      Follow up on the decisions taken at the Director IBA’s meetings with IBA Staff.

 

2.1       The Committee reviewed the status of the decisions taken at the Director IBA meetings and also considered the following status reports sent by Mr. Aman U. Saiyed, Program Director BBA, and Dr. Zaheeruddin Asif, Program Director, Computer Science.  

 

2.2       Program Office and Exam Department will support each other in their peak seasonal works: Done. The Program Directors reported that their staff is already helping the examination department during the conduct of examination. 

 

2.3       Program Office Staff will be transferred to their respective campuses after the completion of registration process. Program Directors will organize training for their Program Office staff with the assistance of H.R. Department. Partially done.  Dr. Zaheeruddin Asif reported that a request for transfer of Mr. Mohammad Ayaz (Qazi) is pending with H.R.  Mr. Aman U. Saiyed reported that they would have to wait for the training until they move to the new premises.

 

            The Director H.R. stated that the announcement of new designations has not been done.  After announcement of new designations, individuals would move to concerned offices. The case of Mr. Mohammad Ayaz (Qazi) would be resolved after the announcement of designations.    

 

2.4       Program Offices to develop SOPs: Partially done. Dr. Zaheeruddin Asif reported that they are collecting data for SOPs.  Mr. Aman U. Saiyed reported that since they have undergone a complete reshuffle of the Program Office, it would take a little while to write SOPs and figure out the response time to queries.     

 

2.5       All queries / requests of students will be dealt by Program Office and students will not be referred to other departments:  Done. Dr. Zaheerudin Asif reported that they are already practicing it at FCS.  Mr. Aman U. Saiyed reported that the Program Office is answering and resolving students’ queries. However, there are matters pertaining to students records, teachers’ comments, etc. which they refer to specific departments and individuals.

 

2.6       Administrative rights to amend the schedules in the ERP to be given to respective program office only: Done.  Mr. Aman U. Saiyed reported that Mr. Imran Batada allowed access to the Program Officers to change class schedules and time tables with his (Mr. Aman’s) prior permission.   

 

2.7       Evening students will be given preference in Electives registration for the evening courses and in case seats are available students of morning graduating class will be given preference:  Partially done.  Mr. Aman U. Saiyed reported that they do not allow morning BBA students to take preference over Evening students. So far, there is no mechanical process to stop that but he is working with Mr. Abdul Wajed to resolve this issue. 

 

2.8       Program Office to hold departmental meetings on regular basis: Done.  Weekly meetings have been institutionalized.

 

DECISION:

 

2.9        The up-dates along with comments given above were noted and approved.

            b)  Enrolment of graduates for IBA Convocation.

 

4.5       The Registrar reported that 390 students are to be conferred upon the IBA degrees in the Convocation being held on November 30, 2010. However, he said that only 169 were enrolled till the last date of enrolment.  He requested the EC to extend the date for enrolment till November 22, 2010.

 

DECISION

 

4.6       The Committee extended the date for enrolment for Convocation till November 22, 2010.

 

c)      ERP Password.

   

4.7       The Director ICT stated that a few faculty members have shared their ERP Password with their Teaching Assistants whereas the Controller of Examinations has suggested that the faculty should not share their passwords with their Teaching Assistants.

 

4.8       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that part of the responsibility of the Teaching Assistants is to enter grades.

 

DECISION

 

4.9       After a detailed discussion, the Committee agreed that the faculty members should not share the password with their Teaching Assistants.  The Committee decided that the Teaching Assistant should be given separate password for the papers for which he / she would be required to enter grades.

 

ACTION

 

4.10     The Director ICT would get separate password issued to the Teaching Assistant for the paper for which he / she would be required to enter grades.

 

4.11     The Associate Deans would discuss this issue with their respective faculty.

 

d)     Request for Assistance for Participation in Global Sustainable Leadership Forum.

 

4.12     Mr. Aazar Ali Shad, a student of IBA in his email addressed to the Director IBA, stated that he was selected by the Global Sustainable Leaders Form (China) to be one of the delegates as the “Leaders of Tomorrow” at the forum. He stated that GSLF is an international public private partnership platform on sustainability with the aim to make sustainable changes for the Earth. It is a platform where 600 youth sustainable champions meet 600 global business, civic society and government leaders at one conference. The application and selection process was a rigorous one and his being selected alone is an honour to the IBA. The Leaders of Tomorrow are entitled the privilege to join sustainable leaders network, and are invited to Global Sustainable Leaders Forum every year as well as get opportunity to be involved in high level network.  He mentioned in his email that although it is his desire to be a part of Global Sustainable Leaders Forum 2010 and bring honour to the IBA, yet, to participate in the Forum, he would be needing financial assistance of Rs. 50,000 which is required for Registration fee, travel and accommodation in China.

 

4.13     Mr. Zafar Siddiqui suggested to obtain further details from Mr. Aazar Ali whether he has been invited only to attend the Forum or he has been asked to present any paper.

 

DECISION

 

4.14     The Committee authorized Dr. Mohammad Nishat and Dr. Sayeed Ghani to obtain further details from Mr. Aazar Ali and see how it can be possible to provide financial assistance to him and also suggest a criteria for financing for such events in future.

           

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
