MINUTES OF 317TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, OCTOBER 20, 2014

The 317th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 20, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Board Room, Third floor, Fouji Foundation Building (Admin. Building), IBA Main Campus. The following were present: were present:

1. Dr. Ishrat Husain Dean & Director
2. Dr. Mohammad Nishat Associate Dean, FBA
3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani Associate Dean, FCS
4. Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN Registrar
5. Mr. Moeid Sultan Director Finance
6. Engr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz Director Projects
7. Mr. Imran Batada Head of ICT Department
8. Mr. Adnan Hameed Head of HR

 

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Permission to open Kiosk at Students’ Center Cafeteria by M/s. Hobnob.

 

1.1       The Registrar reported that despite their best efforts they have not been able to get suitable parties for the cafeteria.  He said that tender for the cafeteria was floated three times but there was no response. He said that he thought an option of bringing confectionary on trial basis.  He reported that Hobnob have expressed their interest and they can be allowed to open their kiosk at Students Centre on trial basis for 1½ months.

 

1.2       The Dean & Director suggested that in order to provide a large business volume to the intended bidders we should club all catering facilities on the campus including the large events conducted during the year. He suggested that entrepreneurship incubating firms should also be asked to participate in the competition.

 

1.3       It was stated that some faculty and staff members at City Campus go to Café Mubarak for a dine.  The Director Projects said that the quality of food at Café Mubarak is good and the price is also reasonable. The Dean & Director suggested that Café Mubarak should also be asked to participate in the competition.  He said that as IBA would be providing its premises, it should reduce its rate and increase the variety of food.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.4       The Committee allowed opening of a Kiosk by M/s. Hobnob at Students’ Centre on trial basis for 1½ months.

 

1.5       The Registrar would invite Entrepreneurship incubating firms, Café Mubarak and other vendors to participate in the competition.

 

Item No. 2      Request from students for make up exams.

 

2.1       The Registrar reported that two students; Ms. Safa Habib of BBA-5 and Syed Anns Ali of BS CS-1, have requested for make-up exams. The reason as stated was the sudden death of grandfather of Ms. Safa Habib on the morning of exam day.  Syed Anns Ali had stated that her grandfather expired on September 24 in USA and being the only son of his family he had to accompany his mother. The Registrar said that the Controller of Examinations has forwarded their applications with the following comments:

 

“As per the policy of “Make-up Exams under Extraordinary Circumstances” stated in the Program Announcement 2014-15, they do not qualify for retake of their missed exams due to the death of their grandfather.”

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.2       The Committee decided to follow the laid down policy which does not allow make-up in such circumstances.  The Registrar would convey the decision to the students. 

 

Item No.3       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Review of progress.

 

3.1       The Dean & Director was very much annoyed at the slow progress and not meeting the deadline given earlier by the Contractors to complete the Fouji Foundation Building (Admin. Building) and the Library.  The Director Projects said that as the labours left for their hometown three days before Eid-ul-Azha and after five days of Eid they did not come, the work could not progress as planned.  The Director Projects said that there were some problems with the DBs which were not of the required standard. The new DBs would be fixed in 10 to 12 days.  He said that he has asked Nespak to suggest contractual remedy for not completing the work on time by the Contractor. The Dean & Director said that he wanted to get things done and the results according to the deadlines and commitments made. These were duly recorded in the minutes of the EC.

 

3.2       The Dean & Director asked as to why there has been only 2% progress in the Library. The Director Projects said that as soon as the testing and commissioning of HVAC is done the work will move speedily.  He further said that there were some small payments of library which were not being made due to cash flow problem. However, he hoped that the Library would be completed before the Convocation. The Dean & Director said that there is a cash flow problem; Rs.65 million from Aman Foundation and Rs.30 million from Sindh Government were to be received but have not yet been received.

 

3.3       The Dean & Director said that on November 1, 2014 he would visit the Admin. Building and the Library.  He said that by that time the electricity should be available in the Library, testing and commissioning of HVAC should be completed, façade is complete and DBs are operational in the Fouji Foundation Building and the pavers are fixed.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
