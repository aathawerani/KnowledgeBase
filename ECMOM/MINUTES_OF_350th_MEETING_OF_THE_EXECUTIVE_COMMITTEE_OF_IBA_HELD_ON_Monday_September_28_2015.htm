MINUTES OF 350TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 2015

The 350th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, September 28, 2015 at 9:30 a.m. at Dining Hall, 5th floor, Aman Tower, IBA City Campus. The following were present:   

1.

Dr. Sayeed Ghani

 Associate Dean, FCS

2.

3.

4.

5.

 Controller of Examinations

 

Before considering the agenda item, Dr. Sayeed Ghani congratulated Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer and his team, and the projects team on completing the work and making the Dining Room of 5th floor of Aman Tower ready for the meeting in a very short span of time.

 

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       To review the preparation for Sadequain Exhibition, and IBA Convocation.

 

1.1              The Registrar briefed the members about the arrangement for the exhibition of paintings by Sadequain.  He said that the exhibition is one of the series of events being held to commemorate 60th Anniversary of IBA.  He said that the exhibition of the poetic works of Pakistan’s Icon Sadequain would be inaugurated on Sunday, October 4, 2015 at 5:00 p.m. on the 5th floor of Aman Tower, IBA City Campus. He informed that the inauguration of the exhibition would be preceded by recital of rubai and some speeches by notables on works of Sadequain in the adjoining Jehangir Siddiqui Auditorium.  He said that the exhibition would continue to remain open from 11:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. throughout the week till Sunday, October 11, 2015.

 

1.2              The Registrar also briefed the members about the arrangements in-hand for Convocation 2015 to be held on December 5, 2015 in which Ms. Sharmeen Obaid Chinoy, winner of Oscar Award, will be the Guest of Honour. 

 

1.3              The Registrar informed the members that inauguration and dedication of facilities have also been scheduled at Main Campus the details of which are as under:

 

·         Inauguration of Mian Abdullah Library on Monday, October 12, 2015 at 10:30 a.m.

·         Inauguration of Fauji Foundation Building on Thursday, October 15, 2015, at 10:30 a.m.

·         Inauguration of OBS Courtyard on Friday, October 16, 2015 at 10:30 a.m.

 

DECISION

 

1.4       The Committee noted the up-dates on the preparations for exhibition, inaugurations, and convocation 2015. 

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
