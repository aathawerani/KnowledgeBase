MINUTES OF 204TH MINUTES OF 204TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, MAY 21, 2012

The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No.1       To consider and approve the construction of pavilion for cricket ground in the sports arena.

 

1.1       The Director Projects reported that the Executive Committee in its196th meeting held on 26th March 2012 had advised that the design of conventional type pavilion be done so that the cost could be minimized. He said that the Architects have now informed that they simplified the design into conventional type pavilion and reached the conclusion that its minimum cost would be Rs.16.5 million, having the same capacity and facilities as were envisaged in the original design. He said that the cost of the original design was Rs.25.00 million but they carried out value engineering to bring down the cost of the original design to Rs.16.5 million (6600 sft @ Rs.2,500/-per sft.). The Director Projects further reported that they have retained the original design to ensure the harmony with the original master plan and provided the same seating capacity and facilities. 

 

1.2       The Director Projects said that as the budget for the next financial year is being prepared, they needed approval of Executive Committee in principle for this reduced cost for inclusion in the budget of 2012-13.  He added that the seating platform is already being built under the current contract having the seating capacity for 440 persons. The pavilion will have necessary facilities and seating capacity for 320 persons.

 

1.3       The Director Projects sought approval of the Executive Committee so that the project could be started in the financial year 2012-13 to avoid cost escalations if its construction is further postponed.

 

1.4       The Registrar suggested to duplicate the Karachi University’s pavilion in order to bring the cost down.  He proposed that the maximum that can be allocated in the budget should be Rs.10 million.

 

 

DECISION

 

1.5       The Committee agreed with the Registrar and approved the allocation of Rs.10 million in the budget 2012-13 for the construction of pavilion for cricket ground in the sports arena.

 

   

  

Item No. 2       Progress Report on Data Centre Project.

 

2.1       The Director ICT said that he had a meeting with the Dean & Director and apprised him that the Consultant had suggested that the cost of the Data Centre could be reduced by changing the specifications. However, he reported that the Dean & Director did not agree to make a compromise on the specifications. The Director ICT said that he was trying to reduce the cost and would inform the EC about the final cost.

 

DECISION

 

2.2       The Committee noted the up-date.

 

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Up-date on Program Announcement.

 

3.1       The Registrar giving up-date on the Program Announcement said that they have incorporated Programs Menu in one page i.e. a summarised information. He said that they have also added in the Program Announcement Financial Assistance Modalities, and BS Economics & Finance. He said that the Program Announcement will be ready by 13th of June 2012 – one month behind the schedule.  

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
