MINUTES OF 313TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 15, 2014

The 313th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, September 15, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Ishrat Hussain Dean & Director
2. Dr. Mohammad Nishat Associate Dean, FBA
3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani Associate Dean, FCS
4. Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN Registrar
5. Mr. Moeid Sultan Director Finance
6. Engr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz Director Projects
7. Mr. Imran Batada Head of ICT Department
8. Mr. Adnan Hameed Head of HR

 

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Status Reports from:

 

Director Projects:

 

1.1       Transport Facilities Building (to be completed within Rs.5 million):  The Director Projects reported that the revised design from the Architect has not been received although they are constantly following it.

 

The Dean & Director advised the Director Projects to concentrate on the projects / work which are of high priority. He said that the work on clinic, shed, transport will go on as usual but the projects which are to be completed before Convocation 2014 should be focused and given top priority. These include gate, ring road, parking, monument garden, court yard, pavers etc.   

 

1.2       Portion of the existing Canteen at City Campus to be equipped with some fitness/body building equipment - the Director Projects to provide specs and drawings to the Registrar who will get it done internally: The Director Projects reported that the layout has not yet been received.

 

1.3       Courtyard Wall at New Girls’ Hostel at Staff Town: The Director Projects reported that Courtyard Wall would be completed by 25th September, 2014. He said that it was mistakenly reported that it would be completed within two weeks period i.e. by August 25, 2014.

 

1.4       The abandoned office of the Contractor to be demolished and the debris to be cleaned: The Director Projects reported that the Contractor’s office would be demolished by September 25, 2014. He reported that the debris has been removed.

 

1.5       Incomplete façade of the Admin. Building to be completed by end of August 2014: The Director Projects reported that the structure has been completed.  One intermediate slab for roof stair is being demolished. On other related activities work has been started. This area will be completed by end of September 2014.

 

1.6       Gate # 4: The Director Projects reported that the design of the gate has not yet been given by the Architect.  He said that due to delay in the revised design, it would not be completed before Convocation.

 

The Dean & Director was extremely annoyed at the delay and asked the Director Projects not to start the work if it cannot be completed before December 6, 2014.  He asked the Registrar to do painting of the existing gate.  The Registrar said that now they would use Gate No.3 instead of Gate No.4.

 

Registrar:

 

1.7       Recommendation for ‘I’ (Incomplete) grade for students Ms. Seher Khan and Ms. Mah Noor – the Registrar to obtain comments from the Controller of Examinations: The Registrar reported that comments from the Controller of Examinations have been received in which he (the CoE) has referred to the decision of 159th meeting of Executive Committee held on June 27, 2011. The decision was, “there will be no make-up exam if any student forgetfully misses the term or final exam.”  As such the recommendation for ‘I’ was not acceptable.

 

DECISION

 

1.8       The Committee noted the update along with the comments /observations made. 

 

Item No. 2      Proposal for placing of distinguished alumni portraits at the Students   Centre.

 

2.1       The Registrar said that the Dean & Director had suggested that we should have a portrait gallery at the Student Centre displaying the pictures of our distinguished alumni. Accordingly, he said that he has received a list of distinguished alumni whose portraits (8x10 inch) are planned to be displayed at the Student Center. The list includes Alumni forming part of batches up to 1996. He said that the Manager Alumni along with Manager Student Center has surveyed the building and selected the prominent locations at which the said portraits will be displayed. The Registrar said that a sample portrait would be presented for review by members at the next meeting of the EC. Bulk order is intended to be placed through tender later in the month to enable the portraits to be in position by early November, 2014.

 

2.2       The Committee reviewed the list. The Dean & Director suggested that the list should include those alumni who have really excelled, attained great achievement, and have made significant contribution to the society/country.

 

2.3       It was pointed out that the names of some prominent IBA alumni were missing from the list. Some of the names identified during the meeting were:

 

Mr. Shahid J. Hashmi – a great entrepreneur, Mr. Wajahat Husain, President UBL, Mr. Towfiq Husain, ex-Deputy Governor, SBP, Mr. Shoaib A. Shaikh of Axact, Mr. Adnan Siddiqui, ex-Country General Manager, IBM Pakistan & Afghanistan,  Mr. Qaiser Jamal, MD & CEO, National Refinery, Mr. Irfanullah Marwat, former provincial minister, Mr. Shahid Zaki, and Mr. Kamil Shahbazkar, ex-MD, Philips, Mr. Razi Ansari, Ex-MD, Pfizer Laboratories, Mr. Kamil Hasan of Intel.

 

2.4       The Dean & Director suggested that the list of distinguished alumni should also include those who are no more alive. 

 

DECISION

 

2.5       The Registrar would get a revised list of distinguished alumni prepared in consultation with Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui and give it to the Dean & Director who would also involve Mr. Shahid Shafiq in identifying distinguished alumni of IBA.

 

Item No.3       To consider and approve thrust boring for connection of Sui Gas Line for       IBA Aman Tower.

 

3.1       The Director Projects reported that advertisement was floated in daily Dawn, Jang and Aas for the bids to quote the rates for thrust boring at 3 road crossings for laying of Sui Gas line for IBA Aman Tower, City Campus. The three contractors submitted their bid on September 01, 2014, which were opened in front IBA officials. Following were the quoted prices announced at the time of bid opening:

1)      M/s Al-Nazar Builder                Rs.750,000/-   (100 meter @ Rs.7500/ per Meter)

2)      M/s Noor Khan                       Rs.800,000/-   (100Meter @ Rs.8000/ per Meter)

3)      M/s Noor Brothers                  Rs.850,000/-   (100 Meter @ Rs.8500/- per Meter)

3.2       The Director Projects reported that later the lowest bidder M/s Al-Nazar Builder offered voluntary discount to work at a cost of Rs.5800/- per meter.

 

3.3       The Director Projects stated that the Procurement Committee in its meeting held on 13th September 2014 approved the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s Al-Nazar at a cost of Rs.750,000/- at the discounted rate of Rs.5800/- per meter.

 

3.4       The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to consider and approve the award of work of thrust boring to the lowest evaluated bidder @ Rs.5800/- per meter.  He said that the payment to the contractor shall be made at the actual measured lengths executed at site @ Rs.5800/- per meter.

 

DECISION

 

3.5       The Committee approved the award of work of thrust boring to the lowest evaluated bidder @ Rs.5800/- per meter for the actual measured lengths. 

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
