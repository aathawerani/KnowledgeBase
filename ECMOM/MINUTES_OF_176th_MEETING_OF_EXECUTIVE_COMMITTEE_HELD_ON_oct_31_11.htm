MINUTES OF 176TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON

MONDAY, OCTOBER 31, 2011

 

The 176th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 31, 2011 at 9:30 a.m. at Conference Room, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                                         Associate Dean, FBA

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                               Associate Dean, FCS

3.      Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN                                Registrar

4.      Mr. Mobin Khalili                                                 Controller of Examinations

5.      Mr. Ali Taha                                                      Director Finance

6.      Mr. Zafar Siddiqui                                               Director CED

7.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                               Director ICT

8.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                                           Director H.R.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects attended on special invitation.

 

The following agenda items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve commencement of works at Aman Tower.

 

1.1       As the item was to be briefed by Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia who could not attend the meeting, this item was deferred.

 

 

Item No. 2      To consider the issues of AC failure at City Campus Admin Block UPS Room.

 

2.1       This item was withdrawn by the Director Projects who was to present this case.

 

 

Item No. 3      Progress Report on Data Centre Project.

 

3.1       The Director ICT reported that the final design documents of data centre of City Campus with technical specifications was finalized and sent to the Principal in U.K. on October 25, 2011 for internal vetting, response to which was received on October 27, 2011.  He said that due to holiday on October 24, the submission of final document was delayed for two days.  The final tender document was submitted to ICT on October 29, 2011 for final approval before forwarding it to the Contractors in replacement of the older tender document.

 

DECISION

 

3.2       The Committee noted the progress on the Data Centre Project.

 

 

 

Item No. 4      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)                  Proposal for New Permanent Site for Generators.

 

4.1       Cdr (R) Syed Fahim Uddin, Manager (Gen Maintenance / Security) submitted a proposal for a new site for Generators to be re-located on permanent basis.  He said that the matter of blocking only traffic way from main gate towards OMI gate will be clocked if the Generator drawing is turned horizontally. This would not only create inside parking space but will also cease movement of light trucks and browsers from one side to another. Keeping in view the above he requested that Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects may please be convinced and requested to turn proposed layout 90 degrees to avoid future re-locations as the present layout will not only be hazardous for traffic management but will also restrict movement of Fire Tender or browsers in case of emergency as it has less than 10 feet of space.

 

4.2       Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that the space left in between the Academic Block and the site of generator is exactly 10 feet.  He further said that browsers, trucks and tankers are not supposed to pass through the Main Gate; if needed they will come from other gates. On the proposal of turning the drawing at 90 degree Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that it would take much time.  Further he said that it is a temporary arrangement, and when the Aman Tower is ready all generators will be shifted to the basement of the Aman Tower.

 

DECISION

 

4.3       In view of the clarifications given by Mr. Shameem Ahmed the Committee did not approve of the new permanent site for generators as proposed by Cdr. (R) Fahim Uddin.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
