The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       To review the preparation of inauguration of Mian Abdullah Library.

 

1.1       The Head Librarian showed some photographs of the old Library and the photographs taken after its renovation. The Dean & Director suggested that students and faculty should know what facilities and resources are now available.  He suggested to arrange an orientation for them. The Registrar suggested to prepare a small presentation for all CRs, office bearers of societies, and the faculty at the Training & Instructions Room of the Library.  He said that he would invite professional photographer on October 4 to take photographs of the library for presentation purposes.

 

1.2       After the meeting the members took a round of the Library and also visited the places where the final touch was in progress. The Dean & Director instructed to expedite and complete the remaining work by October 12, 2015.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)                  To consider and approve tagline for Convocation 2015.

 

2.1       The Registrar said that the following taglines have been proposed for the Convocation 2015:

 

                  "Sixty years of harmonizing dreams with reality"

 

                  "60 years of thinking and building enlightened minds" 

 

                  "Commemorating 60 years of progress in pursuit of Excellence"

 

                  "Celebrating 60 years of dedicated service"

 

                  "60 years of providing exceptional learning opportunities"

 

                  "Celebrating a year of landmark achievements"

 

                  "Year of Landmark achievements"

 

2.2       The Registrar said that they have decided that every graduating student would be called   on the dais and would be given scroll for the degree. He said that the previous practice of making the batches stand before the dais for conferment of degree would be eliminated in order to adjust the time.

 

DECISION

 

2.3       The Committee decided that the tagline for Convocation 2015 would be, "Celebrating 60             years of progress in pursuit of Excellence".

 

2.4       The Committee noted the decision with regard to conferment of degree as recorded at 2.2   above.

 

b)                 Plan to hold Computer Science Classes at City Campus.

 

2.5       The Dean & Director said that they have to plan to hold Computer Science classes at City Campus from next semester. The Registrar said that it can be but the only issue is that the lab at Main Campus is much larger than that of City Campus. The Dean & Director said the lab classes can be shifted at Main Campus.  He wanted to know if there is any space at City Campus where we can expand the lab. The Registrar said that the faculty lounge at the Academic Block of City Campus can be converted into a lab by shifting the faculty lounge at Aman Tower.

 

DECISION

 

2.6       The Committee requested the Registrar to thoroughly look into the feasibility of shifting faculty lounge to Aman Tower and converting the existing faculty lounge located at Academic Block into a computer lab.

 

c)                  Entering attendance of TAs in ERP.

 

2.7       The Dean & Director said that there is attendance issue of TAs in ERP. He asked Mr. Adnan Hameed to tell the tutors that if they do not enter attendance, they would not get payment.

 

2.8       Mr. Adnan Hameed asked as to how bottom 30% would be decided.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested that it should be decided after first mid-term; before that it should be left to the Instructor to assign.  The Dean & Director agreed with Dr. Sayeed Ghani and said that after one month of the start of the semester i.e. from October 1, 2015 the Instructor should assign bottom 30% for tutorials.

 

DECISIONS AND ACTION

 

2.9       Mr. Adnan Hameed would inform the TAs to enter the attendance in ERP of the students being given tutorials failing which they would not get any payment.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
