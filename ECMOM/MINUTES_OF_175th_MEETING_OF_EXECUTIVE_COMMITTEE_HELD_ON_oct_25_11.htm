Item No. 2      To consider and approve proposed revision in Employee Attendance and Flexible Timings rules.

 

2.1       The Director HR said that she would like to propose inclusion of following flexible working arrangements in the existing flexible timings rules of IBA for non-teaching management staff.  She said that the objective of these proposed revisions are to facilitate employees to cope with their special circumstances such as pre & post maternity related condition; temporary disability, fit in a dependents care arrangements and any other circumstances that do not allow employee to regularly attend office or work for complete working hours.

 

A)        Staggered hours/half day working:

2.2       Employment terms can be re-drawn based on certain specified number of working hours to be put in by the employee on weekly/monthly basis, which will include adjustment of employee’s salary and other related benefits for the period s/he goes on staggered hours / half day working. 

 

Duration of such arrangement cannot exceed more than 3 months.  Arrangement up to 2 weeks can be authorized with the approval of the departmental head, while any period above 2 weeks will require approval of the Dean & Director IBA.

 

B)        Working from home or other flexible location

 

2.3       Requests for home working will be considered in relation to job role that may be carried out equally on site or from home.  This could involve carrying out specific tasks, which require a high level of concentration and/or minimum interruptions, at home or any other location. Maximum period for such work can be consecutive one week, which will require approval from the Dean & Director IBA. However, occasional home working may be allowed with the approval of the Departmental Head up to two consecutive days.

 

DECISION

 

2.4       After thoroughly discussing the proposal and also obtaining comments from Mr. A.K. Modi, Incharge Leave Record on the proposal, the Committee agreed to the first part of the proposal i.e. Staggered hours/half day working.  The Committee did not approve the second part of the proposal which was on “Working from home or other flexible location”.

 

ACTION

 

2.5       The Director H.R. would report the approved proposal to the Board.
