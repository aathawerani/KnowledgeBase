Item No. 5      To consider and approve re-organization of I.T. Department

5.1       The Director I.T. stated that in the course of restructuring of administrative infrastructure at IBA, the IT department has also been shuffled. Previously, IT and MIS were two distinct sections and were part of a common department, CCS. Recently major changes were made in administrative infrastructure of IBA and departmental directors were hired. Likewise Department of Information Technology is now headed by Director IT.

5.2       The Director I.T. suggested that keeping in view major transformations in different sectors at IBA, it is imperative that some responsibilities within the IT department should be disseminated to ensure providing prompt and efficient I.T. related services to faculty, students and staff at IBA. Further the IT and MIS sections, under the umbrella of DoIT, should also be renamed to better reflect their responsibilities and services provided by them. He suggested that IT section be renamed as ICT Infrastructure and Operations, whereas MIS section should be renamed as Information System and the Director I.T. should be re-designated as Director ICT (Information and Communication Technology). He said that there would be no change in the functionality of his department.

DECISION

5.3       The Committee approved the recommendation of renaming the I.T. section as “ICT Infrastructure and Operations”, and the MIS section as “Information System” and the Department to be named as “ICT Department”.

ACTION

5.4              The Director H.R. would issue a notification for the change of name of the sections as well as the Department.

Item No. 6      Extension / completion of  Server Room at IBA Main Campus.

6.1       The Director I.T. informed the Committee that IBA will very soon experience major changes regarding IT infrastructure. He said that up-gradation of email and network infrastructure requires a fully equipped data centre (main site) and a fully redundant DR (Disaster Recovery) site. He reported that initially it was planned to make City Campus as main site and Main Campus as DR site. But due to unavailability of space for extension at City Campus, it is now planned to make Main Campus as Main Site till such time multi storey, twin towers are in place at City Campus.

6.2       The Director I.T. said that making Main Campus as main data centre will require huge quantity of hardware which will be procured after two months. Recently completed Server Room at Main Campus is much less in space and will not accommodate the hardware required for data centre, an extension of which is already planned. He said that if hardware for data centre is purchased before Server Room is ready, they will have no space available to securely store it. He requested the Executive Committee to approve immediate expansion of the server room at Main Campus so that it is ready before hardware for data centre is procured and ready to be deployed.

6.3              The Director Projects stated that the design for expansion has been incorporated and it was planned to be started in January 2010. However, he said that since this is urgently needed he will take it up.  He added that it may take three months time to complete it but he said that he cannot give any firm date unless he sees the extent of the work.

DECISION

6.4              The Committee requested the Director Projects to start the process and try to complete the expansion of server room at the Main Campus as soon as possible.
