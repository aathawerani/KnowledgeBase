MINUTES OF 152ND MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON

MONDAY, MAY 9, 2011

 

The 152nd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, May 9, 2011 at 10:00 a.m. at IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                             Associate Dean

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                   Associate Dean

3.      Mr. Ali Taha                                          Director Finance

4.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                       Director Projects

5.      Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                               Director CED

6.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                   Director ICT

7.      Ms. Ayesha A. Minai                                Director H.R.

 

The following agenda items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      To  consider and approve conversion of offices into class rooms in Aman –CED Building at Main Campus.

 

1.1       The Director Projects stated that to discuss various issues related to the class rooms, a meeting was held with Dr. Shahid Qureshi, Associate Director, CED IBA, along with the representatives of M/s. Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative (Pvt.) Ltd. The meeting was attended by Mr. Zayd Bilgrami, and Mr. Shabih Haider from M/s. NBCL, the Director Projects and Engr. Mahmood Bughio, Projects Department IBA.

 

1.2       In this meeting, the Director Projects reported that, Dr. Shahid Qureshi showed the seating arrangements and class rooms of Babson College which in his opinion ought to be followed while designing the CED Building. Dr. Qureshi emphasized that at least two more seminar type bigger class rooms of 60 students capacity each should be provided in addition to the one seminar type class room for 60 students.

                                                                                                 

1.3       The Director Projects informed that the Architects advised that there was a possibility for conversion of the four offices on the 1st floor and four offices on the 2nd floor into 2 seminar type class rooms.  If needed, 2-3 small classrooms can be converted into offices to make up the deficiency of the offices. This will involve some cost adjustment pertaining to the service drawings.  

 

1.4       The Director Projects further said that Dr. Shahid Qureshi also advised that the capacity of smaller class rooms (30 seats) is too less and that the class rooms be tiered so that the teacher could see the name tags of the students. The Director Projects reported that the Architects would look into this requirement and bring in the proposal.

 

1.5       The Director Projects reported that Dr. Qureshi has strongly recommended the use of black board or the green board instead of white board currently being used. The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to approve the suggested changes.

 

1.6       Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that there are 33 offices many of them will remain vacant which could be used as Syndicate Room. As such there will be no need to create offices out of the class rooms.  He suggested that instead of fixed chairs, there should be movable chairs for which some moving space should be provided.  About the suggestion of black board, he said that it would be decided later. However, he said that whatever the Board may be, it should not be located in front of multimedia.

 

1.7       The Director ICT said that he could not find the Incubation Centre in the drawing. Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that the Incubation Centre is there but it would be in a big hall which would be separated through partition.

 

1.8       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that there is a difference between Incubation Centre and CED as such the Incubation Centre should be separate.   

 

1.9       Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that for the Interior Decoration of CED, if its cost is around Rs....../- (..% of Rs..... million) then he would request Aman Foundation to pay for that and he hoped that Aman Foundation would do that.

 

1.10     Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that they have Rs.... million for SAP Project which they had planned for FCS but since the Director has suggested that it should be integrated with CED; they would require two rooms in CED; one for SAP and one for Industrial Projects.

 

1.11     The Director ICT said that he would require one room at the CED to convert it into Video Conferencing Room.   

 

DECISION

 

1.12     The Committee approved the suggested changes and requested the Director Projects to ask the Architects to include the suggested changes in their drawings and present it to the EC.

 

1.13     The Committee decided that the cost related to SAP would be met from the amount already budgeted under this head.

 

1.14     The issue of black board or any other board would be decided later by Mr. Zafar Siddiqui.

 

ACTION

 

1.15     The Director ICT would give the details / requirements of Video Conferencing Room to the Director Projects.  

 

1.16     Dr. Sayeed Ghani would request Mr. Imran Khan to provide the details / requirements for a room for SAP to the Director Projects.

 

1.17     The Director Projects would bring the proposal and cost for Interior Decoration to the EC for approval.

 

Item No. 2                  To consider and approve revision in Fee Concession Policy for IBA employees and eligible dependents.

 

2.1       The Director H.R. presenting the existing policy on Fee Concession for IBA employees and eligible dependents said that in view of the fact that one year has passed since this policy was approved, the EC may now review and discuss the possible need to revise, add clarity to, or modify the same. She particularly referred to the monthly gross pay range and the fee concession schedule of the existing policy according to which the employee or his / her dependent admitted into under-graduate or graduate program of IBA is to pay the fee. She wanted to know whether any fee should be charged from the employee or his / her dependent or not.

 

2.2       It was suggested that since the employees who get admission in IBA Programs would be improving their education and in order to encourage them to upgrade themselves, they should not be charged any fee.  However, for the dependents the Fee Concession Schedule should apply.

 

DECISION

 

2.3       The Committee decided to study the fee concession policy at LUMS and requested the Director Finance to obtain the fee concession policy of LUMS and present it to the EC along with the IBA’s existing policy.

 

ACTION

 

2.4       The Director Finance would be obtain the fee concession policy of LUMS and present it to the EC.

 

 

Item No. 3      To consider and approve payment for first quarter for the Post Production Support of ERP System.

 

3.1       The Director ICT reported that the matter of Post Production Support of ERP system was discussed in the EC meeting held on January 10, 2011 and the EC had decided as under:

 

o   The Committee approved the payment of Rs.125,000/- for the 2 months support contract for Nov-Dec 2010.

 

o   In case other universities do not continue with HEC, the Director ICT would negotiate with Techlogix for the revised cost of the contract for January to December 2011 including HRM and Finance modules and report to the EC for approval.

 

o   The Director ICT would obtain separate approval from EC for January to December 2011.

 

3.2       The Director ICT reported that M/s. Techlogix have sent them first quarter invoice on the old rates.  He requested the EC to approve the payment of around Rs.187,000/-for the first quarter at the old rate.

 

DECISION

 

3.3       The Committee approved the payment of Rs.187,000/- to M/s. Techlogix for Post Production Support of ERP for first quarter.

 

 

Item No. 4      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Request from Director Finance for providing facts and figures for preparing budget 2011-12.

 

4.1       The Director Finance requested the EC members specially the Associate Deans to provide the facts and figures for the budget 2011-12.

 

4.2       The Associate Deans assured that they would finalize the facts and figures and send them to the Finance Department the next day.

 

 

b)     Report of the Grievance Committee.

 

4.3       The Director Projects said that he has felt that the motivation level of the employees in his department is getting low, which is probably due to the delay in the decisions on the employees various requests. He requested that the Grievance Redressal Committee be advised to send its recommendations to the Executive Committee as early as possible.   

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
