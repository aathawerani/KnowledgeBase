MINUTES OF THE 61ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON AUGUST 17, 2009

The 61st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, August 17, 2009 at 10:00 a.m. at the Chairman, KPT’s Hut, Manora, Karachi. The following were present:

1. Dr. Ishrat Husain Director
2. 2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat Associate Dean
3. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN Registrar
4. Mr. Ali Taha Director Finance
5. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz Director Projects
6. Mr. Zafar A. Sidiqui Director CED
7. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi Director I.C.T

 

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No. 1      Implementation Reports – Up-date on the Executive Committee decisions (48th to 55th meetings).

 

Up-date from the Registrar

 

1.1       Request from Mr. Muhammad Ameen for facilitating him during entry test and other examinations during the program.  The Registrar reported that the decision of the Executive Committee had been communicated to Mr. Muhammad Ameen. However, he informed the Committee that Mr. Muhammad Ameen has taken the matter to Ansar Burney Trust to whom a reply had been sent in writing.    

 

1.2              Repair of seepage at APWA Building: The Registrar reported that upto 75% of the seepage repair work has been completed within the acceptable limit for occupation.

 

1.3              Construction of Pedestrian Bridge between APWA and City Campus Buildings: The Registrar reported that APWA has some reservations on the construction of Pedestrian Bridge. He said that Ms. Laila, Chairman APWA wishes to discuss this matter with the Director IBA. Dr. Ishrat Husain said that he would talk to Ms. Laila of APWA.

 

1.4              Requirements for renovation work of auditorium at Main Campus:  The Registrar reported that he has provided the details to Director Projects for preparing a fresh BOQ for the renovation work of auditorium of Main Campus.

 

Up-date from the Director ICT

 

1.5              The Director ICT to provide draft of Technology Partner Agreement to the Head of Internal Audit for his comments: The Director ICT reported that he had provided a copy of the draft agreement to the Head of Internal Audit and it has been completed.

1.6              The Director ICT to get the existing IBA Code Book scanned and circulated among the EC members: The Director ICT reported that the existing IBA Code Book has been scanned to send to all EC members.  

 

The Director requested the EC members to provide up-date on the Code Book to the Director H.R. by August 25, 2009 as he said that he would present it to the Board after discussing it in the EC.

 

1.7       Final notice from the Director ICT to SAP for compliance by certain dates failing which the arrangements would be cancelled: The Director ICT reported that final notice was given to SAP. However, he said that as the IBA is going with HEC and the tender date has expired, so the ERP with SAP is closed.

 

1.8       Revisit of manpower requirements: The Director ICT reported that the process of procurement has been delayed and they are still in the process as such no progress towards manpower requirements has been made.

 

1.9       The Director ICT to get notice for cancellation of ERP Tender placed in the newspapers after obtaining approval from A&FC:  The Director ICT reported that since the tender has expired, there is no need to cancel the tender.

 

1.10          The Director ICT and the Head of Internal Audit would jointly develop the criteria and relative weightage for various attributes: The Director ICT reported that all ERP related items have been completed with the help of Head of Internal Audit. The items were evaluated by the Evaluation Committee and approved.

 

1.11          The Director ICT to provide multimedia projector with screen for MBA (Banking and Financial Services) classes: The Director ICT reported that the multimedia projector and the screen have been arranged and they will be installed after the false ceiling is ready. 

 

1.12          Dr. Arshad Siddiqi and Mr. Zafar Siddiqui to provide documents related to Code of Conduct available with them to the Director H.R.: Dr. Arshad Siddiqi stated that he has provided some documents and there are some additional documents under preparation which he would provide to the Director H.R. as soon as possible. 

 

Up-date from the Director Projects:

 

1.13          Pantry for ground floor temporary offices:  The Director Projects reported that refrigerator has been received, tea kettle has been bought and the oven is to be received.

 

The Director wanted to know as to when the pantry will be functional to which the Director Projects assured that it would be functional by August 26, 2009.

 

1.14          The Director Projects to get the facts from HEJ on power generation: The Director Projects reported that he visited HEJ power plant and met the concerned engineers. He informed the Committee that HEJ has six Gas engines of 370 KVA capacity each, one gas engine of 340 KVA capacity and 4 Diesel Engine sets of 350 KVA each.  At present they are using only 3 gas engine sets and the diesel engine sets as standby.  He stated that at present they do not have enough gas to run even the three engines. They have applied for additional gas connection on the basis that they would use it for co-gen purpose. For this they have already installed a 250 TR capacity absorption chiller. They are in the process of procuring a Waste Heat Recovery Boiler (WHRB). 

 

The Director Projects reported that the HEJ is going to fully utilize their generation capacity. He said that there is no possibility of importing power from them because of long distance and the power losses.

 

1.15          Claim from Insurance Company: The Director Projects reported that the Insurance Company has verbally informed Finance Department that they have rejected the claim of the boundary wall. They have not yet sent any written confirmation. He reported that the file is not traceable as the person who was dealing with that in his office has absconded.  He said that he would go to NIC office personally as soon as possible and pursue this case and will give a report next week.    

 

1.16     Temporary shed for the students at the Main Campus:  The Director Projects reported that order has been placed and it would be installed and made operational by August 30, 2009.

 

1.17     Report on technical and economic viability of the AC plant: The Director Projects reported that the HVAC Consultant had inspected the plant attached to the auditorium of the City Campus and categorically declared it as unserviceable and beyond economical repairs.

 

The Registrar reported that he has included the above referred AC plant in the disposal list.

 

The Director asked the Director Projects to obtain a certificate and an inspection report from the HVAC Consultant and provide it to the Registrar.

 

The Director Projects informed the Committee that the certificate has already been obtained.

 

1.18     Tender for the design and manufacturing of furniture for the existing Academic Block at Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that procurement is complete.  He said that teachers’ tables & Chairs, rostrums, and furniture of eight breakout rooms will be received by August 20, 2009.  He further said that furniture for all four auditorium classes, faculty lounge and student lounge will be received by September 15, 2009. 

 

1.19   Tender for the development of training facility for EMBA (Banking & Financial Services) in APWA building:  The Director Projects reported that the procurement process has been completed. Furniture will be received by August 25, 2009.

 

The Director Projects reported that another tender was floated for interior decoration including false ceiling, wall panelling and flooring which is in the procurement process.

 

1.20          Renovation of Girls’ Hostel A&B:  The Director Projects reported that work on renovation is 95% complete.

 

1.21          Pathway for multi-purpose building: The Director Projects reported that 80% of the pathway is complete.

 

1.22          Bidding documents for the Auditorium at the Main Campus: The Director Projects said that the architect has not yet provided the documents and is behind schedule.  He said that he expects to get the documents by the first week of September, 2009.

 

1.23          Design of the Students’ canteen at City Campus: The Director Projects reported that the architect has not sent any drawing, tender although he has been following him almost on daily basis.          

 

Up-date from the Director Finance

 

1.24          Assigning a person the responsibility of handling and resolving project related payments and documentation issues:  The Director Finance reported that candidates have been short listed and will be finalized in next two weeks time.

 

The Director said that the person to be assigned should be of Assistant Manager level but well versed in processing project related bills.  He further said that since the Director Projects is the user, he should not have any complaint about the delays in payments to the Contractors.

 

1.25          Postponing the installation of ATM Machine at IBA:  The Director Finance reported that the decision of the EC has been conveyed to Standard Chartered Bank.

 

The Director advised the Director Projects to make provision for ATM in the future plan.

 

1.26          Agreement on student loan:  The Director Finance reported that a request has been sent to State Bank of Pakistan for introducing and implementing the Student Loan Scheme.

 

The Director asked the Director Finance to prepare a proposal on Student Loan and send it to the State Bank.

 

1.27     Approval from UBL for transferring Rs.5 million to Development Fund of IBA: The Director Finance reported that approval from UBL has been obtained for transferring the fund to Development Fund of IBA.

 

1.28     Double duty allowance to Mr. Imran Batada:  The Director Finance reported that the information has been communicated to Director H.R. and the payment has also been effected.

 

Up-date from the Director H.R.

 

1.29          Re-hiring of Mr. Hizabbar Husain, Head Messenger:  The Director H.R. reported that Mr. Hizabbar Husain has been appointed as Messenger on contract for one year after his retirement.

1.30          Medical reimbursement to Mr. Akbar H. Kazmi, Finance Executive:  The Director H.R. reported that the medical bills submitted by Mr. Akbar Kazmi have been reimbursed as per policy.

 

1.31          Claim of Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri, ex-Audit Officer (Systems): The Director H.R reported that the decision of the Executive Committee has been conveyed to Mr. Ehsan Qadri.

 

1.32          The Director H.R. and two Associate Deans to revise staff orientation and staff handbook and ensure that all relevant information is included in that: The Director H.R. reported that feedback was received and incorporated in the draft of Code of Conduct. The staff and faculty handbooks are yet to be produced.

 

Up-date from the Director CED

 

1.33          Insertion of a clause in the contract with regard to addition / deletion in the documentary by BBCL: Mr. Zafar Siddiqui reported that he is negotiating with M/s. BBCL but so far they have not agreed to it.

 

Up-date from the Controller of Examinations

Up-date from Dr. Muhammad Nishat

 

1.35          Memo on handling degree and non-degree programs for approval by the Academic Board:  Dr. Muhammad Nishat reported that the memo is ready and will be presented to the Academic Board.

           

DECISION

 

1.36     The up-dates given above by the individual members were noted by the Executive Committee.

 

 

Item No. 2      To discuss and approve Computer Donation Policy prepared by Director ICT.

 

2.1              The Director ICT presented the computer donation policy.  He suggested that the computers should be fully depreciated in three years period as computers have three years warranty period and after that large amount is to be paid for its maintenance. He also presented a list of schools where he proposed to donate the computers.

 

DECISION

 

2.2              The Committee agreed to the suggestion of the Director ICT for fully depreciating the computers in three years time.

 

2.3       The Committee decided that the preference in donation of used computers would be given to the colleges situated in the backward areas of Sindh and Balochistan as requested by the Provincial Minister for IT of Sindh.

 

2.4       The Director ICT would prepare a revised Computer Donation Policy for consideration and approval by the Audit & Finance Committee of IBA.

 

ACTION

 

2.5       The Director ICT would prepare a revised Computer Donation Policy incorporating the decisions taken above and present it to the A&FC for approval.

 

 

Item No. 3      To consider note from Director (HEC Projects) regarding construction of new Girls’ Hostel at the IBA Staff Town.

 

3.1              Mr. Shameem Ahmad, Director HEC Projects, in his note stated that construction of the New Girls’ Hostel is a part of the RFP (or Terms of Reference) that they had issued to pre-qualified architectural and engineering consultants in respect of the New Boys’ Hostel.

 

3.2       Mr. Shameem Ahmad mentioned that subsequent to Executive Committee and A&FC’s approval of the conceptual design submitted by M/s. Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative (Pvt.) Ltd. (NBCL) on June 15 and 16, 2009, respectively, the terms and conditions for appointment of M/s. NBCL as our consultant were negotiated and finalized, as a result of which a contract agreement with M/s. NBCL has been signed on July 31, 2009. The consultant has started the designing work of the New Boys’ Hostel.

 

3.3       Mr. Shameed Ahmad further stated in his note that construction of the New Girls’ Hostel is a part of the same project (irrespective of funding). He requested the Executive Committee to give approval for appointing M/s. Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative (Pvt.) Ltd. (NBCL) as our consultant for the New Girls’ Hostel, too. If approved, M/s. NBCL would be asked to prepare and present before the Executive Committee a conceptual design of the Hostel.  He also presented the funding position.

 

 

DECISION

 

3.4              The Director did not agree to the proposal given by Mr. Shameem Ahmad and asked the Director Projects to obtain proposals from all short-listed architects and arrange competition among them.

 

ACTION

 

3.5              The Director Projects would obtain proposals from the short-listed architects and arrange competition for the designs for new Girls’ Hostel.

  

 

Item No. 4      To consider request from Mr. Ahmed Ali Sirohey, Charge d’ Affairs a.i., the Embassy of Pakistan in Republic of Yemen regarding affiliation of Pakistan School Sanaa with IBA, Karachi.

 

4.1              The Charge d’ Affairs a.i. in his request of August 9, 2009 stated that the Embassy of Pakistan on the advice of the higher education minister Republic of Yemen has initiated the process of establishing Institutes of IT, English language and business administration at Pakistan School Sanaa. The institute would be offering bachelors’ and masters courses to the Yemeni and expatriate students in the evening.  They requested for affiliation with IBA, Karachi. They stated that giving affiliation would not only introduce IBA, Karachi in Yemen but would also help in creating goodwill for the country.

 

DECISION

 

4.2              The Committee requested Dr. Arshad Siddiqi and Dr. Muhammad Nishat to assess the Pakistan School Sanaa in accordance with the affiliation criteria and give its assessment for consideration by the Affiliation Committee / Academic Board through Executive Committee.   

 

ACTION

 

4.3              Dr. Arshad Siddiqi and Dr. Muhammad Nishat will assess the Pakistan School Sanaa in accordance with the affiliation criteria (available with the Affiliation Department) and present its assessment before the Executive Committee.

 

4.4              The Affiliation Department would provide the Affiliation Criteria to Dr. Muhammad Nishat. 

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
