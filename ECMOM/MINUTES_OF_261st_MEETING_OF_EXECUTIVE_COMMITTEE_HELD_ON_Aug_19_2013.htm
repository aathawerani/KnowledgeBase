MINUTES OF 261ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD

ON MONDAY, AUGUST 19, 2013

 

The 261st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, August 19, 2013 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                                                       Acting Dean & Director

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                                             Associate Dean, FCS

3.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                                          Registrar

4.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                              Director Finance

5.      Engr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz                                                   Director Projects

6.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                                                         Director H.R.

7.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                              Acting Head of ICT

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects, attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No. 1      Status Reports from:

 

Director HEC Projects:

 

1.1       Extension in completion time of Visiting Faculty Hostel project till August 15, 2013 failing which 8½ months penalty to be imposed on the Contractor: Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that the Project Manager Consult-Tech International has advised that 95% of the work has been completed by the given date i.e. August 15, 2013, and therefore, a substantial completion certificate can be issued to the Contractor.  He said that he did not agree with the Project Manager and has asked the Project Manager to determine the time required by the Contractor for completion of the Punch list items. He said that there is a lot of physical work to be done. He said that the penalty would be imposed on completion of the project.

 

The Registrar asked Mr. Shameem Ahmed to send photographs of the Visiting Faculty Hostel to the EC so that EC members can have an idea as to what extent the work has been completed.

 

Registrar:

 

1.2       The Registrar to consider the proposal at the Admission Committee of giving provisional admission and to coincide IBA test with LUMS: The Registrar said that there are a lot of practical problems involved on it. He said that at LUMS there is a completely independent Admission Office; faculty and support staff are also dedicated to admission process but at IBA we are dependent on our faculty and infrastructure availability.  He said that at LUMS there is no interview at undergraduate level.  He said that the Admission Committee has put it on hold and would review it after rethinking about the admission to be given or not in Spring semester.   

 

Mr. Imran Batada said that on-line arrangement for admission is almost ready and can be offered.

 

1.3       Kiosk at Tabba Building: The Registrar reported that he has deferred this for the time being because the construction work is still going on. He said that once the Students’ Centre and the Sports Complex are completed, flow pattern of students would be determined and the Kiosk would be prepared accordingly so that it may serve multipurpose.

 

1.4       Transport Policy: The Registrar reported that he would present the Transport Policy in the next EC meeting.

 

Acting Head of ICT:

 

1.5       Outsourcing of vacant ICT positions: Mr. Imran Batada reported that the SOP has been developed for classroom support and forwarded the same to HR Department for review and circulation.  He further said that meeting with City Campus Administrator has also been conducted. He said that the lab staff would be providing classroom support. The process will be implemented from upcoming semester (Fall 2013).

 

Director H.R.

 

1.6       The Director H.R. to develop a SOP for ICT Staff: The Director H.R. reported that tender notice for outsourcing of support services (including IT support) has been published in newspapers on August 8, 2013. She further reported that SOP was developed by IT and reviewed by H.R. Now it has been sent to the Registrar for his input. The Registrar said that the review is in process.

 

DECISION

 

1.7       The Committee noted and approved the updates.

 

ACTION

 

1.8       Mr. Shameem Ahmed would send photographs of the Visiting Faculty Hostel to the EC so that EC members can have an idea as to what extent the work has been completed. 

 

Item No. 2      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a) Moving of fibre media from existing data centre to new Tier-III Data Centre.

 

2.1       Mr. Imran Batada reported that Copper / Fibre / Internet backbone has been moved to Tier-III data centre. He reported that we will move equipment in two phases, next week we will move all network equipment i.e.  IPT / Wi-Fi / Network security and by the mid of September remaining servers i.e. EMC Storage / IBM servers would be moved to Tier-III data center.

 

DECISION

 

2.2       The Committee noted the shifting of existing data centre to new Tier-III Data Centre with satisfaction.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
