MINUTES OF 300TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON
MONDAY, JUNE 9, 2014

The 300th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, June 9, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Conference Room, Tabba Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Ishrat Husain Dean & Director
2. Dr. Mohammad Nishat Associate Dean, FBA
3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani Associate Dean, FCS
4. Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN Registrar
5. Mr. Moeid Sultan Director Finance
6. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz Director Projects
7. Mr. Imran Batada Head of ICT Department
8. Mr. Adnan Hameed Head of H.R

Mr. M. Asif, Senior Executive, Mr. Musa Ali, Executive, and Mr. M. Ather Rana, Executive-I attended the meeting on special invitation.

The following items were discussed:


Item No.1 Status Report from the Director Projects.

1.1 Construction of boundary wall and ramp road on the southern side of the under-construction Auditorium at City Campus through Al-Shafi Enterprises at a lump sum cost of Rs.3 million: The Director Projects reported that the boundary wall has been substantially completed. Five per cent work involves painting. EA Architects are designing it a fresh as they did not agree to the design of HFA. Their promise is that they will submit the design by 14th June 2014.

The Dean & Director wanted to know as to when the construction work will be completed. The Director Projects replied that by September 2014 it will be completed and they would issue Variation Order to Paragon.

1.2 The Director Projects would prepare and advertise the RFP for the sewerage of Staff Town: The Director Projects reported that RFP has been prepared and circulated to all concerned. It will be finalized on 20th June after receipt of the comments of the users. He said that they have to assess the future housing. After the survey they would come to know what the requirements are.

The Dean & Director said that three new blocks of faculty apartments will be eventually added. The Registrar said that in the available space new apartments can be constructed without disturbing the existing houses. He said that the three blocks should be independently constructed not by demolishing the existing houses. He suggested to improve the drainage of the existing houses. The Director Projects said that his idea was to develop a good quality master plan including road, and sewerage system for the staff town. The Dean & Director said that we have to give immediate relief to the residents of the staff town, visiting faculty hostel, and the girls hostel. He suggested to postpone the Master Plan at the moment and priority to be given to the sewerage system of the colony.

1.3 The Director Projects would seek advice from Adaptive Technologies for using solar heated geysers: The Director Projects said that he could not complete the task being busy on other priority jobs. However, he said that he would take up this month.

1.4 The Director Projects would prepare a package of interior decoration work and place tender for it: The Director Projects reported that Interior design of 1st floor has been done. Details of Ground Floor Server Room civil works have been received on 5th June 2014. It will take another 10 days to prepare the BOQ.

The Registrar suggested that first the wall should be broken, ground floor to be completed, and the false ceiling should be done when electrical and mechanical points are identified. The Dean & Director suggested to first complete the civil work. Mr. Imran Batada and Mr. Mushtaque should prepare BoQ and issue tender for the mechanical and electrical work in coordinate with Projects Department.

1.5 Construction of Boundary Wall of courtyard at New Girls’ Hostel at Staff Town: The Director Projects reported that the bidding has been completed and it has now been submitted to Procurement Committee. The Registrar said that it is not boundary wall; it is courtyard wall.

1.6 Engagement of Single Contractor to do balance miscellaneous works of Library at Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that M/s Total have been given the job. They have commenced mobilization. They have been given three months to complete the job.

1.7 Sports Facilities at City Campus: The Director Projects said that the Architect is expected to submit his design this month. The Registrar suggested that half of the cafeteria should be converted for the sports facilities and half should continue to function as cafeteria for the lower staff.

1.8 Dr. Sayeed Ghani pointed out that the value of contract (as per letter of award) as mentioned in the attachment is Rs.137.54 million whereas in the note the cost of the project of Electrical Infrastructure Work is Rs.109 million: The Director Projects after verification confirmed that the correct figure is Rs.137.0 million.

1.9 Bursting of bulk water supply pipeline at Boys Hostel: Repair job was completed in 3 days without any cost to IBA. Valves design completed. The cost is being estimated. It will be done through a Variation Order to the External Development works.

DECISION

1.10 The up-dates given above along with the comments were noted and approved.


Item No. 2 Presentation on HCM Implementation.

2.1 Mr. Imran Batada reported that they have finalized the following modules of HCM and accordingly made a presentation of those modules:

  • Publication Module
  • Unit system Module
  • KPI System for Faculty
  • Personal Information Management System
  • Time Management System
  • Leave Management System
  • Recruitment, Selection Module
  • Training and Development Module.

2.2 Mr. Imran Batada explained the features of the following modules one by one:

Publication System:

  • Added the module in the existing ERP System
  • Already implemented in the Spring 2014 Semester for Annual Report

Unit System:

  • Added the module in the existing ERP System
  • This will be used for Faculty Calcom as well.
  • It has the features to display complete report of individual faculty members like:
        
         • Faculty Development Objectives
         • Faculty KPI
         • No. of Courses taught
         • No. of Publications
         • Faculty Evaluation
         • Status of Result Posted
         • Status of Faculty Presentation

KPI for Faculty:

  • Added the KPI module in the ERP System
  • Units claimed workflow system has been added as well
  • Faculty member can claim units and chairperson can approve the same.
  • This will be useful at the end of the year where the comparison can be made between the units claimed and the units earned.

Personal Information Management System:

  • Managing personal details, contact details, emergency contacts, dependents, immigration information, previous job, qualification etc.
  • Complete organogram can be managed by this module.
  • This is fully integrated with the ERP System

Time Management System:

  • This module is integrated with our biometric system
  • Overtime calculation can also be made by the System
  • In the existing system we are not able to calculate or monitor overtime because of the manual process
  • Head of Department can login and also check the report of individual/department
  • Attendance Reward can also be generated from the System.

Leave Management System:

  • This module has the complete workflow.
  • Employee can apply for leave via the System. The request will go directly to his / her supervisor.
  • Supervisor can accept or reject
  • No paper will be used in the entire process
  • Leave Administrator can review the activity
  • It will automatically deduct leaves after approval.

Recruitment Module:

  • Vacancy can be announced by the System
  • Candidates can apply via online system
  • HR Administrator can short list the candidates
  • Align interviewer via the system
  • Entire history can be managed by the system for future use.

Performance Appraisal:

  • KPI can be defined of an individual employee.
  • This can be utilized at the time of Calcom
  • This will help when performing quantitative analysis
  • We can give number based on the task completed.

2.3 The following comments / suggestions were made:

  • Overtime calculation which is a part of the Time Management System will be done on the basis of punch in and punch out timing and it will be live from July 1, 2014.

  • ERP and Payroll should have the same Universal ID Number.

  • The Registrar when asked as to how the lower staff would operate the Leave Management System, Mr. Imran Batada replied that the lower staff can have access to the system through their supervisors.

  • It was suggested that employees should be trained in HCM module and particularly how to operate Leave Management System.

  • The Dean & Director said that all information is dependent on Business Intelligence. Mr. Imran Batada said that HEC was to provide Business Intelligence Module but they are taking time. Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested that IBA should purchase the BI Module from its own resources. Mr. Imran Batada that they can purchase it as it is not so costly. However, he said that he would get the up-date from HEC and take action accordingly.


DECISION

2.4 The Committee appreciated the work done by Mr. Imran Batada and congratulated him on HCM implementation.

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
