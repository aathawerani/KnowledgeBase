MINUTES OF THE 51st MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON JUNE 8, 2009

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                 Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Associate Dean
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Associate Dean
  4. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar
  5.  Mr. Mobin Khalili                              Controller of Examinations
  6.  Mr. Ali Taha                                      Director Finance
  7.  Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                         Director CED
  8. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                 Director Projects
  9. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                          Director I.C.T
  10. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director H.R.

  11. Ms. Erum Hafeez, Manager Communication, attended by special invitation

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      Presentation of IBA Documentary.

 

1.1       Before giving presentation of IBA Documentary prepared by M/s. BBCL, Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that this is for the first time that a 16 minute documentary has been prepared. He said that they would have different versions of the documentary of various durations. He requested the members to view the documentary and give their comments on it. The members made certain observations which were noted for incorporation.

 

1.2              At the end of the presentation the Director wanted to know if any changes can be made in the documentary over the period.  Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that he would talk to BBCL in this regard.

 

1.3              The Committee suggested that an item in the contract should be included that additions / deletions can be made in future in the documentary by BBCL at the advice of the Competent Authority of IBA.

 

DECISION

 

1.4              The Committee approved the documentary subject to the incorporation of the comments / observations made by the members present.

 

1.5              The Committee also thanked Mr. Zafar Siddiqui and his team members Ms. Erum Hafeez and Mr. Raza for getting the documentary prepared.

 

ACTION

 

1.6              Ms. Erum Hafeez would ensure that the comments / observations made are incorporated in the documentary.

1.7       Mr. Zafar Siddiqui would ensure that a clause is inserted in the contract with regard to additions / deletions in the documentary by BBCL at the advice of the Competent Authority of IBA.     

 

 

Item No. 2      To consider request from Mr. Akbar H. Kazmi, Finance Executive for Medical Reimbursement.

 

2.1       Mr. Akbar H. Kazmi, Finance Executive in his application for medical reimbursement stated that he joined IBA as Finance Executive on March 9, 2009. During his final interview he stated that the HR and Finance panel assured him that he would be entitled medical facility and leaves from the very first day of his joining as IBA wanted him to join IBA on urgent basis. However, he said that in his appointment letter, medical and leaves facilities were not allowed to him for first six months. He stated that he enjoyed full medical coverage at his previous employer. He further stated that his wife got hospitalized due to delivery after his joining IBA.  He requested the Executive Committee to allow him medical entitlement from the date of his joining IBA and to allow reimbursement of actual expenses incurred by him on IBA benchmark rates on account of hospitalization of his wife.

 

2.2              The Director HR stated that as the reference of Mr. Akbar Kazmi could not be verified at that time, he was appointed on six months contract with no medical and leaves facilities. She further stated that according to IBA medical policy, only 14 hospitals are on the panel. Mr. Akbar Kazmi’s wife was treated at a hospital which is not in our panel. However, she suggested that since Mr. Kazmi’s wife was registered prior to Mr. Kazmi’s joining of IBA, his requested may be considered as a special case and may be allowed reimbursement at LNH’s rate. 

 

DECISION

 

2.3              The Committee agreeing to the suggestion of the Director H.R., allowed reimbursement of medical bills to Mr. Akbar Kazmi on account of hospitalization of his wife at the rate approved for LNH.

 

2.4              As a policy the Committee decided that all employees will be provided medical, leaves and other associated perks from the date of joining.

 

ACTION

 

2.5              The Director H.R. will ensure that the medical bills submitted by Mr. Akbar Kazmi is reimbursed as per policy and he be allowed applicable leaves.

 

2.6              The Director H.R. will further ensure that all employees are provided medical cover and leaves as per policy from the date they join IBA.

 

 

Item No. 3      To consider request from Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri, ex-Audit Officer (Systems) for settlement of outstanding balance.

 

3.1       Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri, ex-Audit Officer (Systems) at IBA in his request to the Director IBA stated that he discharged additional responsibility of acting head of internal audit from December 20, 2005 to July 8, 2007 for which he was promised an allowance of Rs.5,000/- per month and which was subsequently recommended / approved. He also claimed increment arrears (performance component) for the year 2005.

 

3.2       The Manager H.R. clarified that initially it was recommended that Mr. Ehsan Qadri be given officiating allowance of Rs.5,000/- and the performance based increment for 2005. However, before the recommendation could get approval, Mr. Ehsan Qadri tendered his resignation with proper notice. Thus in accordance with the decision of the Internal Committee that “those who are on long leave, have left, resigned or are serving notice period will not be considered for any increment”, the name of Mr. Ehsan Qadri was dropped from the final recommendation.

 

3.3              The Manager H.R. further stated that the approved increment of Dr. Awais Tipu was withheld on the basis of the Internal Committee decision but later the Executive Committee allowed the approved increment to him on the basis of satisfactory performance for the period he served and clearance before leaving the IBA.  However, he said that in case of Mr. Ehsan Qadri, there is no approval from the Board for the increment and payment for additional responsibilities.

 

DECISION

 

3.4              The Committee requested the Director H.R. to examine the case and come up with her findings.

      

 

Item No. 4      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Issuance of Transcript to Ms. Faiza Izhar

 

4.1              Dr. Sayeed Ghani stated that Ms. Faiza Izhar got provisional admission in MBA (MIS) in Fall 2008.  As per IBA policy, admissions to MBA(MIS) were offered with a requirement of 16 years of academic education. Ms. Faiza had done B.Sc (Pass- 2 year) and Advance Diploma in S.E. (3-year).  She was required to obtain equivalency of her three years Advance Diploma in S.E. which she confirmed in writing that she was approaching HEC for equivalency.  Later she replied that her Advance Diploma is not recognized by HEC.  Therefore, her admission was cancelled vide letter of June 1, 2009. Now she has requested for issuance of transcript for the courses she had done during the two semesters.

 

4.2              The Director said that he requested the Controller of Examinations to establish the fact by asking the HEC directly. Accordingly the Controller of Examinations wrote to the HEC. The HEC replied that APTECH Computer Education, Karachi, is not a chartered degree awarding institution, therefore, certificates held from this Institute cannot be considered as academic qualifications.

 

DECISION

 

4.3              The Committee decided that she may be given certificate for the courses she has done. Names of the courses, grades / marks obtained may be mentioned in the certificate but no credit would be given for such courses if she gets admission in IBA’s undergraduate or graduate programs in future.

 

4.4       The Committee further decided that in future Program Coordinators will resolve such issues.

 

b)    Evaluation Committee

 

4.5       The Director Projects referring to the formation of Evaluation Committee of Technical Proposals and Designs of New Projects approved by the EC in its 46th meeting held on May 4, 2009 suggested that in addition to the Director Projects, the Director I.CT., and the Registrar, the Project Director HEC, and the Director Finance should also be included in the Evaluation Committee and the quorum should be fixed at 3 members.

 

DECISION

 

4.6       The Committee agreed to the suggestion for inclusion of the Project Director HEC, and the Director Finance in the Evaluation Committee. The Committee also agreed that it should have 3 members quorum. 

 

c)      Payment to Mr. Shahab Ghani

 

4.7              The Director Projects reported that Mr. Shahab Ghani has agreed to charge Rs.50,000/- as lump sum fee instead of hourly rate for the first assignment. He said that as per TAP, approval for payment to Consultants rests with the Director IBA.

 

DECISION

 

4.8              The Committee approved the payment of Rs.50,000/- to Mr. Shahab Ghani and that future fee be decided on negotiation basis.

 

d)     Availability of signed / authenticated documents on matters presented before the EC.

 

4.9              The H.R. Manager stated that because of the introduction of paperless meeting (matters for discussion are circulated through email and presented on computer), there are certain documents which are received unsigned and as such it will have no authenticity if presented to the audit as supporting document. He suggested that since the all related original papers lie with the department / person initiating the issue, it should be the responsibility of the concerned department / individual to satisfy the audit as and when needed.

 

DECISION

4.10          The Director agreed with the H.R. Manager and instructed him to request EC members to send signed papers to the Secretary, Executive Committee also for record purposes.

 

ACTION

 

4.11          The Members Executive Committee will ensure that a signed copy of the documents presented before the EC is sent to the Secretary, Executive Committee for record.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
