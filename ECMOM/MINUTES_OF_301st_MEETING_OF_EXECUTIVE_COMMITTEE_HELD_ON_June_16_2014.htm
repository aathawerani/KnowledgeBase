MINUTES OF 301ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD
ON MONDAY, JUNE 16, 2014

The 301st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, June 16, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Conference Room, Tabba Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Mohammad Nishat Associate Dean, FBA
2. Dr. Shakeel Khoja Associate Dean, FCS
3. Mr. Moeid Sultan Director Finance
4. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz Director Projects
5. Mr. Imran Batada Head of ICT Department
6. Mr. Adnan Hameed Head of H.R

Mr. Abdul Wajed, Assistant Professor attended the meeting on special invitation.

The following items were discussed:

Item No.1 To consider an application of a student Ms. Shahrukh Samad Siddiqui.

1.1 Dr. Nishat said Ms. Shahrukh Samad is a final semester student of BBA. He said that because of transition last year the Summer semester classes were offered in the morning. In that particular summer semester students got themselves registered in more than two courses. He said that normally approval is required for taking more than two courses in summer. However, because of transition Ms. Shahrukh took the following five courses and missed verification / approval. In final term she got head injury which was operated upon at Aga Khan University Hospital and thus missed the final term. Medical certificate from Aga Khan University Hospital was presented by Ms. Shahrukh:

      1. Calculus 1
      2. Principles of Account 2
      3. Statistical Inference
      4. Services Marketing
      5. Financial Management

1.2 Dr. Nishat said that this case remained pending for quite some time and then he discussed this with the Dean & Director who on compassionate ground advised to get this case processed through Executive Committee. Dr. Nishat said that there are two issues: (a) condoning non-approval for five courses, and (b) make up exams for the courses she missed. He said that there is a precedent and students have taken more than two courses in that Summer semester. He said that if Ms. Shahrukh had appeared in the final exam, there would have been no issue but as she got injured and remained hospitalized from August 11, 2013 to August 15, 2013 she missed the final and this issue was raised. He suggested that she may be given ‘I’ on medical ground and as per policy she be allowed to take the final exam in Summer semester 2014 and if those courses are not offered in summer, a make up exam should be arranged for her.

1.3 Dr. Shakeel Khoja suggested that in Summer not more than two courses should be allowed in future. It was suggested that the Registrar should be requested to get the medical certificate submitted by Ms. Shahrukh verified.

DECISON

1.4 The Committee condoned the non-approval for registration in five courses, and subject to medical verification, ‘I’ would be granted in the five courses and accordingly, the Controller of exams would arrange final / make up exams for those five courses she missed.

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
