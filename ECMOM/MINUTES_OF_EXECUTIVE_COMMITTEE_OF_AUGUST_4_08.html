MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON AUGUST 4, 2008 (SEVENTH MEETING)

 The seventh meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, August 4, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                               Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                         Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Mobin Khalili                                 Controller of Examinations
  5. Mr. Moeid Sultan                                Acting Head of Finance & Accounts
  6. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                         H.R. Manager

 

Prof. Inayat N. Din, Registrar, could not attend the meeting due to indisposition.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider request from Ms. Sarah Iftikhar student of MBA (Direct) at Main Campus for exemption from doing foundation courses and to allow her 5-month project instead of foundation courses.

1.1       Ms. Sarah Iftikhar in her application stated that she completed her BBA from the IBA City Campus in May 2008. To avail concessional fee of IBA Main Campus which is allowed to top 50% successful candidates, she appeared in the admission test for MBA(Direct) instead of automatically getting admission to MBA after BBA and she ranked in top 50%. 

1.2       Ms. Sarah has applied for exemption from foundation courses which she has already done at BBA level at the IBA and instead has requested for approval for 5-month project.

DECISION

1.3       The Executive Committee approved the request for exemption from foundation courses of MBA(Direct) which Ms. Sarah Iftikhar had already done in her BBA and allowed her to do 5-month project instead of first semester courses of MBA(Direct).

ACTION

1.4       Mr. Imranul Haq, Manager Alumni and Placement will arrange 5-month project for Ms. Sarah Iftikhar in coordination with the concerned Program Office.

Item No. 2      To consider note from Dr. Sayeed Ghani regarding advancement in existing Campus Management System.

2.1       Dr. Sayeed Ghani in his note of August 2, 2008 stated that as per the requirement of Program Offices, customization is required in the existing Campus Management System. He stated that since the implementation of ERP Project may take time, therefore, the required changes were an immediate requirement of Program Offices. For making the requisite changes, M/s. Tower Technologies has quoted Rs.100,000/- whereas the allocated budget on account of Software Operating Expense is around Rs.1 million.  

2.2       The Director wanted to have some performance guarantee from M/s. Tower Technologies to the effect that if there is any break down in the system or anything goes wrong, they would be responsible to fix it free of cost.

DECISION:

2.3       Dr. Sayeed Ghani will ask M/s. Tower Technologies to provide performance guarantee. Subject to performance guarantee, the job may be awarded.

ACTION

2.4            Dr. Sayeed Ghani will take necessary action.

Item No. 3      Freezing appointment at various levels.

3.1       A status report of the vacant positions was presented to the Director on August 2, 2008.  The Director in consultation with the Registrar, Acting Head of Finance & Accounts and representatives of H.R. decided to freeze certain positions mostly at the lower level. The status of the positions in each department along with the decision to freeze certain positions was presented to the Executive Committee.

3.2      The Director said that due to switching over to computerization the manual work will be minimized to a great extent. Thus the existing strength of people at lower level would not be required after computerization. He said that appointments on junior level positions, if needed, may be made on daily wages basis. He further said that students may be hired for suitable jobs on hourly basis. He said that for reception desk two students may be engaged on rotation basis.

  DECISION:

3.3       The Committee noted the decision and decided to give any input based on their discussion with their respective departments.

 Item No. 4      Creation of positions / manning requirements.

4.1       The Director said that in future respective departments would be required to submit their manpower requirements to the H.R. department first. The requirements would include justification for the positions, their job specifications and job descriptions.  The requisitions which must be submitted before March each year would first be discussed in the Executive Committee and, if needed, the heads of the respective departments initiating the requisition would be invited to give justification for the requisitioned positions. The Executive Committee will authorize each department to put in their requisitions in the budget.

ACTION

4.2              The H.R. department will obtain requisitions for new vacancies from department heads with justifications, including job description, and job specifications and present them before the Executive Committee for discussion.

 Item No. 5      Re-designation of the head of Centre for Executive Education.

 

5.1       The Committee decided to re-designate the head of Centre for Executive Education from Chairman, CEE to Director, CEE.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
