Item No. 4      Steel Staircases at the Academic Block, at City Campus - Human Safety Measures

 

4.1       The Director Projects stated that at the time of designing, during and after the construction of the staircases, the Architects did not specify any engineering or Architectural solutions for application on the treads of the steel staircases in the Academic Block, even after repeated reminders.  He said that a number of complaints were received regarding "slippery" nature of staircase steps of Academic Block, City Campus. He said that on the suggestion of the IBA Projects Department the Architects and Project Manager prepared proposals for the following four alternative materials  for the treads:

 

·        Marble

·        Granite

·        Wood

·        Heavy duty Rubberized vinyl sheets

 

4.2       The Director Projects reported that after conducting a market research following comparison emerged:

 

Material       Cost per tread            Cost of installation on

                                                87 steps (4 staircases)             

 

Marble         Rs.3100/-.                        Rs.269,700/-

 

Granite         Rs.3650/-                         Rs.317,550/-

 

Wood           Rs.3200/-                         Rs.278,400/-

 

R/Vinyl          Rs.2445/-                         Rs.212,715/-

 

4.3       Giving technical details of each material the Director Projects said that Marble is a material which requires high maintenance and is susceptible to breakage. Moreover, it is a harder material and is not safe on stairs especially during rains. So it is not recommended.

 

Granite is costly. It is hard and is susceptible to breakage. It is not safe and, therefore, not recommended.

 

Wood is the ideal solution but is used in interior staircases. It is susceptible to damage by rain and termite. So it is also not recommended.

 

R/Vinyl sheet is weather resistant, softer material and maintenance free. It is the safest and the cheapest solution. Its life is 5-7 years and is recommended for installation.

 

4.4       The Director Projects stated that the cost of the rubberized vinyl flooring for the four staircases of Academic Block, City Campus is based on the quotation obtained from the only supplier M/s. Sarina Corporation in Pakistan who have also applied the same material in the Auditorium staircase. He said that the abrasive nature of these rubber sheets will offer suitable resistance/friction against any slipping and tripping possibilities. Laying of this flooring can be an important step towards improving safety aspects for passing students, faculty and staff who use these stairs extensively on a daily basis.

 

4.5       The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to approve Rs.212,715/- on the basis of single quote considering the reasons mentioned in 4.4 above.

 

DECISION

 

4.6       The Committee approved the recommendation for rubberized vinyl flooring for the four staircases of Academic Block, City Campus to be supplied by M/s. Sarina Corporation at a cost of Rs.212,715/-
