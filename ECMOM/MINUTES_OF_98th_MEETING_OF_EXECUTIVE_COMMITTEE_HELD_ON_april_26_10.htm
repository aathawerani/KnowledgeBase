MINUTES OF THE 98TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, APRIL 26, 2010

 

The 98th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, April 26, 2010 at 9:30 a.m. at the Conference Room of IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Associate Dean

  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                         Associate Dean

  3. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN            Registrar

  4. Mr. Ali Taha                                      Director Finance

  5. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                   Director Projects

  6. Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                           Director CED

  7. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                          Director H.R.

  8. Mr. M. Faisal Zia                                Senior Manager IT (Nominee of Director ICT)

 

                                               

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve ICT Cell phone & communication policy.

 

1.1       Mr. Faisal Zia presented details of proposed Company Maintained Cell Phones & Cell phone communication expenditures along with their limits for ICT department. He said that the proposed limits and entitlements are according to approved Cell Phones & Communication Expenditures Rules of IBA. He stated that there would be a one time cost of Rs.44,995/- and monthly recurring expense would be Rs.26,000/-  which would be charged to ERP project.

 

1.2              Mr. Zafar Siddiqui said that if the facility is extended to all ICT team then employees of other departments will also claim this facility.

 

1.3              Mr. Faisal Zia said that this facility is proposed to be offered to those staff who have the requirement according to the operations. He also informed EC that infrastructure has been expanded and no hiring has been done yet. The existing staff is already overloaded that’s why they have proposed for giving this facility to only concerned persons

 

1.4              The Director H.R. said that the Executive Committee had decided that only those whose services are essentially required would be provided this facility.

 

1.5              Dr. Muhammad Nishat suggested that the proposed policy should match with the existing policy of IBA.

 

1.6              Mr.Faisal Zia said that this requirement has already been fulfilled and the presented policy is matched by IAS department and forwarded to EC.

 

1.7              Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that it appears inappropriate to incur regular cost for occasional requirements. He asked Mr. Faisal Zia to define job responsibilities of all those persons who are recommended for the proposed facilities.

 

1.8              The Registrar suggested to reduce the number of persons recommended for this facility by at least 50%.

 

DECISION

 

1.9              The Committee deferred this item and decided that the Director ICT would present the policy again before the Executive Committee by rationalizing the requirements.

 

   

Item No. 2      To discuss and approve layout plan of Administration Building, Main Campus.

 

2.1              The Director Projects presented the layout plan of Administration Building, Main Campus and requested the members to suggest changes according to their requirements. He said that he has taken care of HR requirements and has also reserved space for CDC.

 

2.2              The members suggested changes according to their requirements in the layout plan.

 

DECISION

 

2.3              Mr. Hanif Daud of Icon would resubmit the layout plan by incorporating the changes suggested by the members.

   

 

Item No. 3      To consider and approve the Notification of Sindh Government on declaring every Saturday as Public Holiday.

 

3.1              The Registrar informed the Committee that the Government of Sindh has issued a Notification declaring every Saturday as public holiday.

 

3.2              Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know if program offices can reschedule the Saturday classes.

 

3.3              It was also suggested that the program offices may remain open and other offices may be closed on Saturdays.

 

DECISION

 

3.4              The Committee in general agreed to comply with the Government of Sindh’s Notification for observing Saturdays as holidays. 

 

3.5              The Committee requested the Registrar to gather data for Saturday classes.

 

 

Item No. 4      To consider implementation plan of faculty evaluation.

 

4.1       Mr. Imran Batada stated that there were some functions which were not included in ERP; Faculty Evaluation System is one of them. He said that they floated RFP and M/s. Techlogix who are the single source offered the solution at a total one time cost of Rs.681,600/-.

 

4.2              Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know if additions and deletions can be made in the system to which Mr. Batada replied in the affirmative.

 

4.3              Mr. Batada said that there are some limitations in the existing system which can be removed in the proposed system. He also said that both course evaluation and faculty evaluation can be done in the proposed system and the ID will not be disclosed. However, he said that if two teachers are teaching one course jointly, their separate evaluation cannot be done. He said that he would get the per course multiple evaluation incorporated in the system. He said that employees evaluation can also be done but at a later stage.

 

4.4              Dr. Muhammad Nishat suggested that provision for evaluation of teaching fellows, and teaching assistants should also be made.

 

DECISION

 

4.5              The Committee granted conceptual approval for the implementation plan of Faculty Evaluation incorporating the above suggestions and asked Mr. Imran Batada to present the proposal to the Central Purchase Committee.

 

ACTION

 

4.6              Mr. Imran Batada would present the implementation plan of faculty evaluation to the CPC for approval.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
