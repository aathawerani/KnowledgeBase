Item No.1       Presentation on Landscaping:

Item No.2       Review of Reward Nominations (under IBA’s Rules on Recognizing and Rewarding Employees at IBA).

 

2.1       Mr. Adnan Hameed presented the names and reward levels of the employees nominated for cash reward; he requested the departmental heads to present their justifications for nominating their employees to the Executive Committee.

 

2.2       Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know if there was any policy specifying the number of years after which an employee can be reconsidered for the reward and recognition.  Mr. Adnan Hameed replied that there is no restriction in the reward policy.  However, the Dean & Director said that a time limit can be fixed that if an employee has been given the reward / recognition, he / she would be nominated and be eligible for the reward / recognition after three years of the previous reward. It was decided that from next year (2016 onwards) rewards and recognitions will be given only to those employees who are eligible and the eligibility criteria would be that the employee has not received any reward and recognition during the last three years and the performance period for the reward and recognition would be the previous years.

 

2.3       Dr. Nasir Touheed suggested that along with other details, details of overtime payment should also be included and considered while considering nominations for reward and recognition.  It was decided that employees who are drawing more than 50% of their salary as overtime will not be eligible for the reward and recognition.  However, Dr. Nasir Afghan said that it would have been fair to inform the HoDs of such decisions in advance.  The Dean & Director said that it is the prerogative of the HoDs to nominate anyone but the EC is to take a decision and set or change the criteria.

 

DECISION

 

2.4       The Committee decided that:

 

a)      from next year (2016 onward) rewards and recognitions will be given only to those employees who are eligible i.e. have not received any reward and recognition during the last three years, and the performance period for the reward and recognition would also be the previous 3 years.

 

b)      employees who are drawing more than 50% of their annual salary as overtime will not be eligible for the reward and recognition.

Item No.3       Review of Promotion Recommendations.

He also presented the promotion ladder for IBA Scale as well as BPS Scale Employees.

 

3.2       Mr. Adnan Hameed said that some positions were created which were filled internally by the following four employees who were kept on probation for almost 1½ years.  He said that although their BPS grades remained the same, they were temporarily re-designated according to the position they were placed on after interview with the concerned HOD.  The purpose was to fill these positions internally by giving opportunity to support staff members who have good performance record and are also enhancing their academic qualification. He also mentioned that it was clearly mentioned in the transfer letters that in case of unsatisfactory feedback, they may be returned back to their original positions.
