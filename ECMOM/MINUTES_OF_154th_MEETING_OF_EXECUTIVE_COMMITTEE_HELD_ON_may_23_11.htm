MINUTES OF 154TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON
MONDAY, MAY 23, 2011

 

The 154th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, May 23, 2011 at 9:30 a.m. at IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                    Director

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                    Associate Dean

3.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                              Associate Dean

4.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                 Registrar

5.      Mr. Ali Taha                                           Director Finance

6.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                        Director Projects

7.      Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                                Director CED

8.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                    Director ICT

9.      Ms. Ayesha A. Minai                                 Director H.R.

 

The following agenda items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      Status Reports from:

 

Dr. Sayeed Ghani:

 

1.1       List of interested faculty and Ph.D students from other institutions such as NED, AERC to be prepared and they be invited to the Research Seminar / workshops arranged at IBA. Dr. Sayeed Ghani reported that a monthly Research Seminar series has been initiated in particular for the FCS faculty and PhD students. The first session is planned for the month of June and is expected to be repeated every month. He reported that a list of academicians and industry experts by discipline would be maintained who would be invited regularly.

 

1.2       The Chairman Academic Committee to notify that credit of one unit would be given to MBA faculty only if the concerned faculty teaches cases according to the course outlines approved by the Academic Committee: Dr. Sayeed Ghani reported that all faculty have been informed and requested to submit Case Method based course outlines to AC for presentations.

 

Dr. Muhammad Nishat:

                                                                                                 

1.3       Dr. Mohammad Nishat and Dr. Sayeed Ghani to get a list of interested faculty and Ph.D students from other institutions such as NED, AERC prepared and invite them to the Research Seminar / workshops arranged at IBA: Dr. Nishat reported that series of graduate seminars have been initiated by Dr. Naeem-uz-Zafar.  He reported that Dr. Kamran Siddiqui from IBA and Dr. Javed Iqbal from Karachi University will share their research on May 25, 2001.

 

1.4       Dr. Muhammad Nishat will get the budget allocated for Case Unit at IBA: Dr. Nishat reported that it has been implemented. 

DECISION

 

1.5       The Committee noted and approved the up-dates given by Dr. Sayeed Ghani and Dr. Muhammad Nishat.

 

 

Item No. 2                  To consider and approve a proposal for “Hacking Audit”.

 

2.1       The Director ICT proposed that he wanted to have a Hacking Audit from an external expert. He said that one of his friends is top expert whose services are hired by large organisations incognito. He said that he would like to engage him to do an audit for the safety and integrity of IBA’s system and especially ERP data base.  He requested the EC to approve the Hacking Audit and the nominal expenses.  He said that subject to approval by the EC he would get the estimate from him.

 

2.2       The Director asked the Director ICT to invite that expert for hacking audit and pay for the return air ticket.

 

DECISION

 

2.3       The Committee agreed with the Director that Dr. Arshad Siddiqi should request his (Director ICT’s) friend who is an expert in this field to do Hacking Audit at IBA without any remuneration and IBA would pay only for his return air ticket.

·         Approval of Extensions in completion periods.

3.5       The Director Projects mentioned of the major reasons because of which extensions in completion targets were allowed and which were beyond the control of Contractors & Project Managers:

 

·         Un anticipated changes in Structural elements and construction of additional structures.

·         Un anticipated repairs and strengthening of existing structures

·         Delayed release of areas to the contractors.

·         Layout changes due to site conditions

·         Intermittent Stoppage of work on request of faculty & Staff.

·         Law and Order situation of the city

 

3.6       The Director Projects reported that the services of Project Managers were continued during these extended periods and the deployment of their staff was as per their contract.

 

B.        EXTENSION OF SERVICES OF PROJECT MANAGERS DUE TO EXTENSION IN TIME OF THE PROJECT COMPLETION ALONG WITH THE FEE FOR THE EXTENDED PERIOD.

 

3.7       The Director Projects said that because of the extensions in the duration of the projects, services of the Project Managers had to be extended and as per contract IBA had to compensate them on mutually agreed terms. He reported that the case was presented in Executive Committee meeting held on December 30, 2010 in which the EC had advised to negotiate the fee of extended period from Project Managers M/s Integ.

 

3.8       The Director Projects reported that a series of negotiation meetings were held with the Project Managers and after detailed discussions the project Manager M/S Integ agreed to reduce around 46% (Rs.4, 347,708) from their total claimed amount of Rs.9,419,062/- Thus he said that they are entitled for an amount of Rs.3,463,463/- after deducting Rs.1,607,891/- already paid to them.

 

3.9       The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to accord approval of (a)  extensions in completion periods, and (b) extension of services of Project Managers M/s. Integ along with their fee of Rs.3,463,463/-

 

 3.10    The Director asked the Director Projects to send the documents to Mr. Jehanzeb to examine them.

 

DECISION

 

3.11     The Committee recommended the extensions in completion periods of the projects listed in 3.4 above and also extension of services of Project Managers M/s. Integ along with their fee of Rs.3,463,463/-

 

ACTION

 

3.12     The Director Projects would send the documents to Mr. Jehanzeb, Project Accountant of IBA for scrutiny.

 

 

Item No. 4      Monthly Progress Report.

 

4.1       The Director Projects who was to present the monthly progress report on the projects requested the EC to defer this item as he would present this item in the next meeting of the EC.

 

DECISION

 

4.2       At the request of the Director Projects, the Committee deferred this item.

 

 

Item No. 5      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)                  Draft IBA Budget 2011-12

 

5.1       The Director invited comments from the members on the draft IBA Budget 2011-12 that he had circulated to the EC members.

 

5.2       The Registrar proposed to reduce the amount allocated for external development program because all work would not be done in a year.

 

5.3       The Director said that they were not starting Visiting Faculty Hostel in 2011-12.  He asked to exclude it from the proposed budget.

 

5.4       The Director said that no expenditure would be incurred on Video Conferencing facility in 2011-12.  He said that for CED there would be a separate Video Conferencing facility but it would not be installed in 2011-12.    

 

DECISION
