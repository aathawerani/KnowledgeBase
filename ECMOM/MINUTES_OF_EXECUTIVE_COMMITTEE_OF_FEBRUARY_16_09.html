MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON FEBRUARY 16, 2009 (THIRTY FIFTH MEETING)

 

The 35th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, February 16, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                           Associate Dean & Acting Director
  2. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                 Associate Dean
  3. Mr. Ali Taha                                        Director Finance
  4. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                        Director Projects
  5. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                             Director I.T.
  6. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                            Director H.R.
  7. Mr. Zafar Siddiqui                                Director CED

 

Before the commencement of the meeting, Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer, the new Registrar IBA was introduced to the Executive Committee. Similarly the members Executive Committee introduced themselves to the new Registrar.The Committee welcomed Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer as Registrar of the IBA as well a member and Secretary of Executive Committee of IBA.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider email from Mr. Muhammad Shafi regarding list of holidays 2010 and Academic Calendar 2010.

1.1              Mr. Muhammad Shafi, Admin. Officer (Evening Program) in his email of January 14, 2009 addressed to Mr. Mobin Khalili stated that the whole area around City Campus is sealed by the law enforcement agencies on the occasions of 8th Moharram, 20th Safar (Chehlum), 21st of Ramazan on account of Yaom-e-Shahadat-e-Hazrat Ali (RA), and Jummatul Wida/Youm-ul-Qudus.  As a result only a few faculty members and a small number of students manage to reach the campus.  Thus only a few classes are held with very thin attendance.  He suggested that IBA may be closed on those occasions.

1.2              Mr. Mobin Khalili recommended the note of Mr. Shafi for consideration by Executive Committee.

DECISION

1.3              The Committee decided that students input should also be taken on this issue. Moreover, if four additional holidays can be managed or not will also have to be looked into.

1.4              The matter was deferred till next meeting of the Executive Committee.

Item No. 2      To consider and approve Terms of Reference for Admission Committee.

2.1              The Director H.R. proposed the following terms of reference for Admission Committee for approval by the Executive Committee:

·         Formulation of Admission Policy for 2009

·         To monitor and ensure the effective implementation of rules, regulations and policies related to all parameters governing admissions. 

·         To determine and propose requirements for admission, inclusive of cut off points of test scores.

·         To formulate and review admission policies of all programs of IBA

·         To promulgate the admission calendar.

·         To explore the possibility of having a common aptitude test for students applying to business schools in Pakistan.

2.2              The Director H.R. also suggested the constitution of the Admission Committee for consideration and approval by the Executive Committee.

DECISION

2.3              The Committee approved the terms of reference for the Admission Committee as presented by the Director H.R.The Committee further decided that the recommendations of the Admission Committee would be presented before the Executive Committee for approval.

2.4              The Committee constituted the Admission Committee comprising

i)                  The Registrar

ii)                 Head of Testing Services

iii)                Two Program Coordinators

ACTION

2.5       The Director H.R. would issue a notification about the constitution of the Admission Committee along with the ToRs for the Committee.

Item No. 3      Organization structure oversight and sanctioned strength of faculty and staff.

3.1       The Director H.R. presented the organization structure and the sanctioned strength of faculty and staff as approved by the Board of Governors of IBA.She requested the Executive Committee to give their comments, if any.

DECISION

3.2              The Committee requested that they be sent the organization charts and the sanctioned strength for detailed review.

ACTION

3.3       The Director H.R. would send the organization charts and the sanctioned strength to EC members for detailed review.

Item No. 4      To consider request from Mr. Muhammad Ameen, a student of Class XII who is interested to appear in IBA admission test but has severe eye disease (poor vision). 

4.1       Mr. Muhammad Ameen stated in his application that he is interested to take admission in IBA. But unfortunately he is suffering from a severe eye disease Macular Degeneration of both eyes and has a poor vision which cannot be corrected with glasses. He mentioned that he had cleared ‘O’ level examination from the British Council with distinction in Accounting and secured 74.36% marks in HSC part I from Board of Intermediate Education, Karachi. He stated that he was provided enlarged font question paper. The invigilators also helped him in reading the question paper. He wanted to know if IBA would facilitate him during the entry test and other examinations during the program.

DECISION

4.2              The matter was referred to the Admission Committee.

ACTION

4.3              The Registrar will consider the application of Mr. Muhammad Ameen in the Admission Committee and convey the decision to the applicant accordingly. 

Item No. 5      To consider note from Registrar regarding time bar for completion of course work for BBA students and time period allowed for 4-year BBA Graduate to join MBA.

5.1       Prof. Inayat N. Din stated in his note that the Program Announcement carries an information about time bar rules for IBA students regarding comprehensive exams and duration in which the evening students must complete their PGD & MBA course work. He stated that similar time bar rules needed to be decided about:

a)      The completion of course work for BBA students

b)      Time period allowed for 4 year BBA Graduates to join MBA for students other than having completed their project.

c)      The review of Gold Medal requirements for MBA & BBA graduates particularly with regard to interpretation of C grade because we now have grades C-, C & C+

DECISION

5.2              The Committee referred the issue to the Academic Committee.

ACTION

5.3              Dr. Muhammad Nishat will discuss the issues raised by Prof. Inayat N. Din in the meeting of the Academic Committee.

Item No. 6      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

            a)Medical Facilities to IBA students.

6.1       The Director Finance informed the Committee that the information obtained about the students who needed medical facilities in emergency last year suggest that only two students had availed medical facilities in emergency last year. He suggested that for such an insignificant number of students needing medical facilities in emergency, we should arrange medical facilities in emergency as and when needed as we do for our faculty and staff instead of making a security deposit of Rs.100,000/-.

DECISION

6.2       The Committee agreed with the Director Finance and decided that instead of making a deposit of Rs.100,000/-, students needing medical treatment in emergency would be referred to the approved hospitals as is done with the IBA employees.

6.3              The Committee further decided that arrangements with Karachi University Clinic for medical facilities may be finalized.

ACTION

6.4       The Registrar will finalize the arrangements of medical facilities with Karachi University’s clinic.

b)         UBL’sstall for Debit Card facilities to students.

6.5       The Director Finance informed the Committee that UBL has approached him for allowing them to offer debit card facility to the IBA students.

DECISION

6.6       Since it is in the interest of the students, the Committee allowed the UBL to offer debit card facility to IBA students at the Campus provided they maintain the decorum and the classes are not disturbed. 

ACTION

6.7       The Director Finance would convey the approval of the Executive Committee to the UBL for offering debit card facility to IBA students at the Campus.

            c)    Donation of US$10 million to IBA by Aman Foundation.

6.8              The Director Projects informed the Committee that Aman Foundation has handed over the cheque of US$10 million to IBA on Saturday, February 14, 2009 at the Governor’s House. He said that the Foundation has formed a Project Committee on which Mr. Zafar Siddiqi and he (Director Projects Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz) will represent the IBA.

6.9              The Committee passed a resolution of appreciation for Aman Foundation.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
