MINUTES OF THE 53rd MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON JUNE 22, 2009

The 53rd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, June 22, 2009 at 10:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                Director
  2. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar
  3. Mr. Ali Taha                                       Director Finance
  4. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                 Director Projects
  5. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                         Director I.C.T
  6. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director H.R.

 

Mr. Moiz Hassanali, Head of Internal Audit attended by special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider application from Mr. Mirza Noman Shah for pick and drop facility at subsidized rate.

 

1.1       Mirza Noman Shah, Program Officer, Morning Program, City Campus in his request to EC stated that last year he suffered a stroke on his right side and by the grace of God he recovered and was coming to office. He stated that his doctor advised him not to drive any vehicle for a year. He stated that in the beginning after recovery from the stroke, he used to hire taxi for reaching office and going back home but due to financial constraint he had to discontinue that and then started to come to the office by his motorcycle. He stated that within two weeks of using bike he met with an accident and suffered minor backbone injury. Due to this accident the doctor instructed him for a complete bed rest for two weeks and strictly prohibited him from driving and to be remained cautious during movement for about 6 weeks more.

 

1.2       Mr. Noman Shah stated that he had to commute by a taxi again but it has become extremely difficult for him to bear this expense on monthly basis. He stated that he cannot use public transport as embarking on and disembarking from a moving vehicle is not possible for him at this stage.

 

1.3       In view of the above facts Mirza Noman Shah requested the Executive Committee to arrange for him an official vehicle for pick and drop and charge from his monthly salary for the same, on concessional rates.

 

DECISION

 

1.4       The Committee considered the application of Mirza Noman Shah and regretted to provide pick and drop facility as logistically it was not possible.   

 

ACTION

 

1.5              The Registrar would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mirza Noman Shah.

Item No. 2      Presentation of the case of Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri, ex-Audit Officer (Systems).

 

2.1       The Director H.R. referring to the earlier decision of the Executive Committee in which she was advised to examine the claim of Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri for increments and officiating allowance, stated that although there is no written approval yet Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri is witness to the fact that the Internal Committee had decided an officiating allowance of Rs.5000/- along with increments for Mr. Qadri.

 

DECISION

 

2.2       The Director said that in government organization witnesses do not work, there has to be approval for everything. He regretted that in the absence of any approval, no payment can be made to Mr. Qadri. The Committee agreed with the Director.

 

ACTION

 

2.3       The Director H.R. would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mr. Ehsan-ul-Haq Qadri.

 

DECISION

 

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

           

a)                  Cancellation of ERP Tender

 

3.1              The Director ICT presented the draft of the notice for Cancellation of ERP Tender for approval by the Executive Committee.

 

3.2       The Head of Internal Audit stated that he has already suggested the ground for cancellation and the procedure for cancellation of the tender in the last EC meeting.

 

DECISION

 

3.3       The Committee decided that the Director ICT in consultation with the Head of Internal Audit will draft the language of the notice for cancellation of ERP tender. The Committee further decided that as suggested by the Head of Internal Audit, notice for cancellation would be advertised after seeking approval from A&FC through circulation resolution.

 

ACTION

 

3.4              The Director ICT will get notice for cancellation of ERP Tender placed in the newspapers after obtaining approval from A&FC.

     

b)                 To consider application from Mr. Inayatullah, Security Guard for appointing him on contract after his retirement in June 30, 2009.

 

3.5              Mr. Inayatullah, Security Guard, requested that he may be appointed on contract after his retirement on June 30, 2009.

 

DECISION

 

3.6              The Committee did not consider the application of Mr. Inayatullah and decided that as a policy retired employees would not be re-hired.

 

ACTION

 

3.7       The Director H.R. would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mr. Inayatullah.

 

c)                  To consider and approve evaluation criteria developed by the Director ICT for various items.

 

3.8       The Director ICT presented the evaluation criteria for items such as Servers and Services, Storage, Wi-Fi, Desktops laptops and printers, Network Infrastructure Solution, VOIP, Network Security Solution etc. for approval by the EC.

 

3.9              The Head of Internal Audit said that the evaluation criteria should be mentioned when the tender is given.  He further said that the relative points / weightage given to different criteria needs to be reviewed.

 

DECISION

 

3.10          The Committee authorized the Director ICT and the Head of Internal Audit to jointly develop the criteria and relative weightage to various attributes.

 

ACTION

 

3.11          The Director ICT and the Head of Internal Audit would jointly develop the criteria and relative weightage for various attributes.

 

d)                 Technical Evaluation Committee

 

3.12     The Director Projects said that the Head of Internal Audit has suggested that the Director Finance and the Registrar should also be members of the Technical Evaluation Committee.

 

DECISION

 

3.13     The Committee agreed to the suggestion of the Head of Internal Audit and decided that there would be a three member Technical Evaluation Committee comprising:

 

i)                    Director Finance

ii)                  Registrar

iii)                Director Projects / Concerned Director

 

e)                  Requirement of Chairs

 

3.14     The Registrar reported that as the furniture has been shifted to APWA, there is no furniture for the new Academic Block. He said that the Director Projects had asked the Consultants to provide drawings and those drawings are ready however, no samples are ready and no cost estimates are available so tender cannot be done without these.

 

3.15     He said that they went to the Interwood with the drawings but they regretted to meet our demand because of the shortage of time. 

 

DECISION

 

3.16     The Committee requested the Registrar to tender for the supply of chairs. The Committee further suggested that the exact specifications and delivery time of the chairs should be mentioned in the tender documents. 

 

ACTION

 

3.17          The Registrar would float a tender for the supply of chairs.

 

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
