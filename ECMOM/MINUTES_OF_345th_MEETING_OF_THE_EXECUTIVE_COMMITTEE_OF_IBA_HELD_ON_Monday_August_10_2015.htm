The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Placement of new fresh water supply line.

 

1.1       Syed Fahim Uddin, Manager General Maintenance stated that a study was made by Nespak on the water supply line when Mr. Shameem was handling such projects.  Nespak had prepared a drawing also but the matter was postponed and it was decided that initially VFR would be provided new water supply line.  However, due to pressure the old lines which are 40 years old got punctured and despite several repairs during the last two years it has become apparent that the pipe has been eroded and needs to be replaced. Recently the old line in front of security office at Staff Town got punctured and in Block ‘D the water was not reaching at all due to low pressure.  Alternate arrangement had to be made from girls hostel.  Under these circumstances the Registrar after visiting the site and inspecting the condition of the pipe instructed the Maintenance Department to get estimate for laying new water supply line in staff colony.

 

1.2       Syed Fahim Uddin reported that they requested M/s Al-Shafi to follow the design prepared by Nespak. However, Al-Shafi suggested that they could identify a contractor  to do this estimation as they themselves were no longer mobilized there. Mr. Fahim informed that the water will come from overhead tank and due to gravity flow no pumping would be required. The Dean & Director emphasized that it should be designed in such a way that water should flow on gravity instead of being pumped to minimize operating costs.   

 

1.3       Syed Fahim Uddin reported that he has obtained estimate for this work which amounts to is Rs.2.2 million including the cost of control valves, chambers also. He said that if the proposal is approved the Maintenance Department would prepare and float the tender on the basis of the available BoQ.

 

1.4       Dr. Sayeed Ghani asked if it includes road cutting.  Syed Fahim Uddin replied that minimum road cutting is involved; the pipe would be laid one foot down and then the road would be cemented.

 

1.5       Syed Fahim Uddin said that tendering process would take 1½ months.  Mr. Jehanzeb confirmed that budget is available for this project.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.6       The Committee approved the proposal of placement of new water supply line at IBA Staff Town.

 

1.7       The Maintenance Department would prepare tender documents and float it.  Syed Fahim Uddin would ensure that the work is completed as soon as possible.    

 

Item No.2       To consider and approve TOR for Permanent Grievance Committee.

       

2.1       Mr. Adnan Hameed stated that a Grievance Committee (GC) is proposed to be constituted at the request of IBA employees.  The GC would be a neutral forum where employees can share their grievances related to their employment at IBA. He presented the ToR and identified the scope of work of the GC, its composition and the grievances to be handled on first priority based on the papers submitted by the Employees. 

 

2.2       The Committee felt that only the permanent / full time faculty members should be on the GC. The visiting faculty members can be co-opted members. The Dean & Director said that he would appoint Dr. Faisal Iradat as the Coordinator for Staff matters reporting directly to him. He will not only act as the focal point of contact with the staff but    also serve as Member Secretary of the GC. Dr. Faisal Iradat would be assisted by a full time Executive for staff matters located in the Director’s Secretariat.

 

DECISION

 

2.3       The Executive Committee approved the formation of a Grievance Committee (GC) along with the ToR as presented. The composition of the GC as proposed by the employees of IBA and endorsed by EC would be as under:

 

1.      Dr. Nasir Touheed                                            Chairman
