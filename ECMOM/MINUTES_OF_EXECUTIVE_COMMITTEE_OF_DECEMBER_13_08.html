MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON DECEMBER 13, 2008 (TWENTY FIFTH MEETING)

The 25th meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Saturday, December 13, 2008 at 10:00 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                              Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                        Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                              Chairman CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Inayat N. Din                              Registrar
  5. Mr. Mobin Khalili                               Controller of Examinations
  6. Mr. Ali Taha                                     Director Finance
  7. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                    Director Projects
  8. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director H.R.
  9. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                          Director I.T.
  10. Mr. Moiz Hassanali                            Head of Internal Audit (by special invitation)

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider and approve IBA Transaction Authority Policy and Procedure (version 2)

1.1       Mr. Moiz Hassanali, Head of Internal Audit highlighted the salient features of the draft Transaction Authority Policy and Procedures.  He briefly explained the purpose of the Transaction Authority Policy, delegation levels, Role Players i.e. Transaction Authority, Approval Authority, and Payment Authority, Delegation Plan, Administration of Approval Path, Delegation in absence of Approval, Scope of the Policy, Related Party Transactions, Budget Availability Approval, Budget Re-appropriation, and the overall process for approval.  

DECISION

1.2       Although the Committee was informed of prior consultation with the members, it was decided that members of the Executive Committee should send their comments / observations, if any, to the Head of Internal Audit within a week by email.

1.3              The Committee further decided that the Head of Internal Audit would incorporate the comments, if any, made by the members Executive Committee and prepare a final draft for A&FC’s approval. The Head of Internal Audit would also attach a self-explanatory letter with the Policy for A&FC.

1.4              With regard to delegation of approval authority to another employee in the absence of an employee having delegated approval authority, the Committee decided that notification to this effect would be issued by the Head of the concerned department and copy would be marked to Director HR, Director Finance, Director IT, the Head of Internal Audit and other concerned heads.  In addition for absence of level three employees i.e. Director Finance, Director H.R., Registrar, a copy of the nomination would be sent to the Director.

1.5              The Committee requested Director Finance to prepare guidelines and rules for operating the imprest account and provide that to all concerned.

ACTION

1.6              The members of the Executive Committee would send their comments on the Transaction Authority Policy and Procedure, if any, to the Head of Internal Audit within one week by email.  

1.7              The Director H.R. would request the heads of respective departments to issue a notification for nomination of a person who would be the acting head in their respective departments in their absence and inform the Director and all concerned including the Director H.R, Director Finance, Head of Internal Audit.

Item No. 2     Organization Structure Oversight

2.1              The Director H.R. presented the proposed organization structure before the Executive Committee for review / approval.

DECISION

2.2              The Committee approved the proposed organizational structure with the following modification:

i)         The Project Planning & Development Department would be named as Projects & Facilities Management Department.

ii)        The City Campus Coordinator will be reporting to the Registrar and the job of Coordinator would be assigned to a person as an additional responsibility. 

iii)         The Coordinator City Campus would be seeking inputs and services from every support department but he / she would be responsible for the overall administration and smooth operation and functioning of the City Campus.

iv)         Profit centres such as CEE, CED would be charged for the services provided by the IBA. 

v)         The Director H.R. was advised to revisit the structure of H.R. Department.

vi)        The Director said that second in command at this stage is not needed. If the head of a department leaves, a new high powered person would be appointed. During such time a new person is appointed, one of the existing senior staff would be assigned additional responsibility. The reason for this he said was that he does not want to create more jobs in non-teaching department as he has to strengthen the academic side.   

vii)       The Associate Deans would present their proposed organizational chart to the Director H.R. by December 20, 2008. The Director H.R. would present the proposed organizational chart of H.R. Department before the Executive Committee.

viii)      The Director H.R. was advised to get and appoint an Assistant Controller of Examinations. She was also advised to try to find if internal candidate for this position is available.

ix)        The Controller of Examinations would provide job descriptions and job specifications for the position of Assistant Controller of Examinations to the Director H.R. 

Item No. 3      To consider email from Dr. Zeenat Ismail regarding policy on using semester break.

3.1       Dr. Zeenat Ismail in her email of December 11, 2008 stated that she has information that some faculty members were taking the month of December as semester leave and leaving Karachi without intimation. She wanted to know if after the semester final exams during semester break faculty members are free to go anywhere without applying for the accumulated leaves or should they apply for leaves and inform the respective heads.

3.2       Dr. Zeenat Ismail through an email was informed that faculty members must seek prior permission if they leave the station even for a day and that they are supposed to come to the IBA during semester break also.

3.3       Dr. Zeenat in her email of December 12 stated that either the faculty does not know or is trying to be ignorant.  She suggested that the information provided to her with regard to the status of semester break be notified to all faculty members and put on the IBA portal also.

3.4       The Registrar stated that he had issued such notification in the past and would re-issue the notification clarifying that during semester break faculty members are on duty and cannot leave the station without prior approval. If they want to avail leave they must apply for that.

DECISION

3.5       The Committee requested the Registrar to issue the notification again for the faculty and have it placed on the IBA portal also.

ACTION

3.6       The Registrar will re-issue the notification for faculty regarding leave during semester break and also get the notification placed on the IBA Portal.

Item No.  4     Clarity of next steps in the Performance Appraisal for non-teaching staff and related questions.

4.1       The Director H.R. wanted to know as to what would be the procedure for deciding the annual increments for the non-teaching employees.

4.2       The Manager H.R. said that in the previous years an Internal Committee comprising the Registrar, Head of Finance, Head of H.R, Controller of Examinations and Chairman, MIS & CS was formed to recommend annual increments / promotions to non-teaching employees.  He further said that in 2007 a 10% increase for inflation and a maximum of 7% for performance was given to the employees.  In addition to that, contract employees who had completed seven years of service as on November 30, 2007 were given additional increment equal to the amount given for performance.  Regular employees were given promotion, up-gradation and advance increments on case to case basis.

DECISION

4.3       The Director said that for future there would be a promotion policy which the Director H.R. has been requested to frame. However, for the increment to be effective from December 1, 2008 the Director suggested to follow the past practice.  He formed a Committee comprising (1) Director H.R., (2) Registrar, (3) Director Finance, and (4) Dr. Sayeed Ghani, Chairman, MIS & CS to recommend increments / promotion to non-teaching employees.    

4.4      The Committee further decided that the Director Finance would find the State Bank’s declared rate of inflation for increment purposes.

ACTION

4.5       The Director H.R. would convene a meeting constituted for recommending annual increments / promotion as soon as possible.

4.6       The Director Finance would obtain State Bank’s declared rate of inflation.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
