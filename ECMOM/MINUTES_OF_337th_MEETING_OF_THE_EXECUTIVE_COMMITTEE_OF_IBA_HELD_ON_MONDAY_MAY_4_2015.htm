Item No.1      To consider a proposal on “Landscaping of the Main Campus”.

                   

1.1     The Registrar said that the External Development Works are in three areas: associated with lighting, associated with fixing of pavers, and top black carpeting and another one is compound wall. He said that all the works would be done by Projects but those which involve horticulture would be done by IBA because the rates guven for the horticulture are exorbitant. He said that horticulture work in front of Fauji Foundation has been done by IBA.

1.3     Mr. Fahim Uddin said that although the cost estimate was given a year ago, they would try to bring 30% to 40% reduction in cost. He said the job would be completed in phases.  He said that first phase of work commenced, which include the pathway starting from round about upto Tabba main entrance left side only. On this space grafting of grass will commence this week. 

 

1.4     The Dean & Director said that there should clear demarcation of the work and asked Mr. Mahmood Ali, Mr. Fahim Uddin and Mr. Imran Mumtaz to sort out the area around the Courtyard and as to who is responsible for what – which area is being done by Mr. Imran Mumtaz and which area by Maintenance according to the Master Plan.

 

1.5     The Registrar said that from Tabba Building to Aman-CED all patches will be made green in phase one. The second phase will be pathway connecting the main line towards OBS Courtyard.  The Registrar said that there is construction material which has to be removed.  Mr. Mahmood Ali said that he would get it removed.  The Registrar said that by June 20, 2015 they will have green surface adjacent to Tabba building upto the approaches of Aman-CED. He said that the first phase would be completed by May 30, 2015 and the whole landscaping would be completed by end of December 2015. The Registrar said that he is getting the work done by breaking up the work; sweet earth from someone else, manure from someone else etc. Mr. Fahim said that by end of December 2015 entrance periphery that includes approaches to Tabba and Aman-CED between library and auditorium as well as these buildings upto football ground would be completed.  The Dean & Director suggested that the landscaping should be completed before convocation. The Registrar asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to get the foot of monument garden prepared.

 

          Mr. Mahmood Ali said that the pavers before the auditorium has to be replaced.  The Dean & Director said that it looks fine and advised him not to disturb it.

 

DECISION

 

1.6     Landscaping of first and second phases would be completed by end of December / before Convocation whichever is earlier.

 

1.7     Mr. Mahmood Ali, Mr. Fahim Uddin and Mr. Imran Mumtaz would sort it out as to who is responsible for what – which area is being done by Mr. Imran Mumtaz and which area by others and what is in the Master Plan.  

 

 Item No.2      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 
a)           Program Announcement 2015-16.

 

2.1     The Registrar presented the draft of Program Announcement 2015-16 and said that they would put it on the IBA website by May 10, 2015 as they did last year. He said that they have kept the same format as was of the previous year and continuing since 2012. The number of pages are also the same as was last year. He said that they have been able to reduce 15 pages by removing duplication and have enlarged the photographs of faculty.  He said that this was done by Mr. M. Ather Rana, Senior Executive and an Intern having Karachi Grammar background. He said that the theme of the cover page is that of old building and the new building, and a photograph of a faculty. He said that undergraduate and graduate programs have been explained in detail. He said that they have made composite statement of all undergraduate and graduate programs.

 

2.2     The Dean & Director suggested to place a student’s photograph on the cover page instead of the photograph of a faculty member.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested to obtain consent of the student before placing his/he photograph. It was also suggested to place Aman Tower instead of old building on the cover page. The Dean & Director suggested to include students services category including Students Societies, Internship, NTHP, STHP, CDC etc. 

 

DECISION

 

2.3     The Committee appreciated the efforts of the Registrar in reducing the duplication of information and the format of the Program Announcement.

 

2.4     The Registrar after incorporating the suggestions given during the presentation would circulate the Program Announcement to all concerned for their final comments.

 

 

          There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
