MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON SEPTEMBER 8, 2008 (TWELFTH MEETING)

The twelfth meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, September 8, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                          Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Inayat N. Din                                Registrar
  5. Mr. Ali Taha                                       Director Finance
  6. Mr. Moeid Sultan                                General Manager, Finance & Accounts
  7. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                         H.R. Manager

The following attended by special invitation:

1.  Mr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz                           Director Projects (would be)

2.  Mr. Shameem Ahmad                                Manager Projects

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider proposal from Manager Projects for creating faculty and staff rooms at the Main Campus.

1.1       The Manager Projects said that there is an acute shortage of faculty rooms and offices. He said that the construction of faculty rooms and offices at the ground floor of Administration Block according to Consultant’s design would require a longer period. He further said that the construction of the low-cost multi-purpose temporary building would also take at least six months from award of contract. He proposed that the quickest way for making faculty rooms and offices would be by erecting wooden partitions (laminated chipboard + flush doors), similar to the staff room of PP&D Department. He stated that the ground floor has a comfortable space of about 130 x 26 feet, leaving about 11 feet from each of the staircases. If we leave a six feet wide passage / corridor in the middle, we can create 11 rooms of about 11 x 11 feet on each side (north and south); altogether 22 rooms. The estimated cost for the entire work is Rs.500,000/- and it will take about a month to complete one side (11 rooms) which may be occupied while the work is in progress on the other side. He said that the budget has a provision for such miscellaneous work.

1.2       The Manager Projects said that the IBA would have to hire carpenters on daily wages or on assignment basis for this job but the material would be provided by the IBA.  He also said that almost 70 to 80% of the material may be reused.  Moreover, the rental cost, if offices are to be acquired on rent, would be saved and thus the pay out period will be very short.

1.3            Mr. Rehanul Ambia said that in order to make the offices secured from rain and fire, the exterior walls should be made of bricks.

DECISION

1.4            The Committee visited the site and decided that instead of erecting a wall of bricks, a boundary with one block / brick size on the ground floor would serve the purpose.  The Committee also decided that sliding window pane should be fixed on the rooms.

1.5       The Committee approved the proposal of the Manager Projects and asked for a revised estimate based on actual costs of the material, labour, bricks and windows.

ACTION

1.6       The Manager Projects would provide revised estimate and get the work completed as soon as possible.

Item No. 2      To consider note from Manager Projects regarding Pre-qualification of Electrical Consultants.

2.1       The Manager Projects in his note of August 30, 2008 stated that the Executive Committee had decided to hire an electrical consultant who may assess the present electrical infrastructure, its present power requirements and to plan for next 5 to 10 years needs. An advertisement for pre-qualification of electrical consultants was published on May 19, 2008. Twelve applications were received out of which five applications were not evaluated for technical reasons. The following 7 applications were evaluated and ranked in order or merit:

1.      Jafri and Associates

2.      S.K. Engineers

3.      W.J. Associates

4.      A.S. Consultants

5.      Advance Engineering Associates

6.      Associated Technical Consultants

7.      Matrix Consultants

2.2              The Manager Projects suggested that the top four applicants should be pre-qualified to enable them to submit their technical and financial proposals.

DECISION

2.3              The Committee pre-qualified the top four applicants as suggested and decided that they would be required to submit their technical and financial proposals separately. Financial proposal would be evaluated if the technical proposal is satisfactory.

2.4       The Committee further decided that the terms and reference would be prepared by Mr. Shameem Ahmed and Mr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz.

2.5       The Consultancy fee would be on piece or percentage of work basis.

ACTION

2.6      Mr. Shameem Ahmed and Mr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz would prepare TOR and send them to the four pre-qualified Consultants for further processing.

Item No. 3      To consider proposal from Ms. Maheen Ghauri, Faculty Member regarding placing leave information of faculty on the IBA Portal.

3.1       Ms. Maheen Ghauri suggested that like all other information that has been made available on the IBA Portal, a database containing leave information of faculty be also up-loaded on the IBA Portal.

DECISION:

3.2              The Committe agreed to the proposal and decided that along with leave details of faculty, leave details of staff of officer grades should also be placed on the IBA Portal.

ACTION

3.3       The Manager H.R. will ensure that the Leave Record Incharge provides leave details and monthly up-date for IBA Portal.

Item No. 4      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

            a)  Redesigning of the IBA Portal

4.1       The Director wanted the IBA portal and website to be redesigned in order to make it more effective.

DECISION

4.2              The Committee decided that an internal competition among the students may be organized for redesigning of the IBA Website as well as IBA Portal and the winner may be awarded cash prize.

4.3              The Committee further decided that before conducting the competition, a survey should be carried out on what are the requirements of faculty, students and staff.

ACTION

4.4              Mr. S. Mohsin Naqvi, Web Administrator will get the survey conducted.

4.5              Dr. Sayeed Ghani will assign a faculty member in his department to arrange competition on redesigning of IBA website and IBA Portal.

b)  Availability of a generator for Server Room

4.6       The Committee decided that a generator dedicated to Server Room only be arranged for both Main Campus and City Campus.

ACTION

4.7       The Registrar and Manager Projects will ensure that a generator is made available for the Server Room at the Main Campus and a generator for the Server Room at City Campus.

c)      Outsourcing the computerization of pay roll to Sidat Hyder

4.8       Mr. Moeid Sultan, G.M. Finance informed the Committee that due to high turn over of the employees in the Finance Department in general and the Pay Roll Section in particular, despite several attempts, the pay rolls could not be computerized.

DECISION

4.9       The Committee decided that instead of engaging the internal staff for computerization of pay roll, till such time the ERP is implemented, the function should be outsourced to M/s. Sidat Hyder who have provided the Financial software at the IBA.

ACTION

4.10     The G.M. Finance will obtain details from M/s. Sidat Hyder regarding outsourcing the computerization of pay roll.

d)     Formal reporting of information regarding progress and achievements to the Board and other concerned

4.11     The Director said that the Board is not being formally informed of the activities / development taking place at the IBA.  He mentioned a number of events / developments that took place recently such as his visit to Yale, visit of Dr. Shaukat Bra, Dean of LUMS to IBA, appointment of Director Finance and Head of Internal Audit, Endowment Chair Fund received from Mr. Toufeeq A. Chinoy, return of a faculty after completing Ph.D, admission status etc. etc.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
