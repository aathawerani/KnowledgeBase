MINUTES OF THE 74th MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, NOVEMBER 10, 2009  

The 74th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Tuesday, November 10, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA, Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                           Associate Dean (Acting Director)

  2. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar

  3. Mr. Mobin Khalili                             Controller of Examinations

  4. Mr. Ali Taha                                  Director Finance

  5. Dr. Arshad Siddiqui                         Director ICT

  6. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                       Director HR

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1     To consider application from the Class Representative of MBA (Tax Management) students regarding Eid-ul-Azha Holidays.

 

1.1              The Class Representative of the MBA(Tax Management) in his application of November 7, 2009 stated that there exists a deep concern amongst the students of MBA(Tax Management) Program about the number of holidays that are likely to be granted on the eve of Eid-ul-Azha. He stated that the expected two days holidays would be insufficient given the fact that they did not enjoy any holiday period even during the first semester.

 

1.2              The CR stated that the MBA(Tax Management) class consists of students hailing from all over the country and from the areas as far as Azad Jammu Kashmir and Northern areas of Pakistan and many of them are married and raising families. He requested for at least one week Eid Holidays and the Eid Holidays should be announced as early as possible so that they may make necessary travel arrangements.

 

DECISION

 

1.3              The Committee considered the application but regretted to accede to their request as IBA being a public sector institution has got to follow the official holidays. The Committee suggested that the students may avail the allowed absences for such occasions.

 

ACTION

 

1.4              Dr. Qazi Masood Ahmed will inform the CR of MBA(Tax Management) about the decision.

 

 

Item No. 2      To consider application from Mr. Farhad Khan, a student of  BBA-I for exemption from mandatory attendance due to illness.

 

2.1       Mr. Ajmal Khan, brother of Mr. Farhad Khan of BBA-I submitted an application on behalf of Mr. Farhad Khan stating that Mr. Farhad was critically ill with viral fever and requested for leave of absence from October 26 to October 31, 2009 and exemption from mandatory attendance during this period.  He also submitted medical certificate from Aga Khan University Hospital.

 

DECISION

 

2.2              The Committee decided that in view of well defined policy on absences not withstanding the circumstances mentioned by the applicant, it will not be possible to grant any exemption in mandatory attendance.

 

ACTION

 

2.3              Mr. Amanullah Saiyed, Program Coordinator will convey the decision to Mr. Farhad Khan.

 

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)         Request for allowing 5th absence to students participating in the Faculty of Computer Science Ad Campaign.

 

3.1              Dr. Sajjad Haider stated in his email addressed to Dr. Sayeed Ghani that some students have been selected by BBCL for the Faculty of Computer Science Ad Campaign and the final photo shoot would take place during the 2nd half of November 11, 2009.  He stated that as the selected students are from different classes, it is difficult to find a single 3-4 hours slot where everyone is available. He suggested that if any student has to miss a class then he / she be allowed a 5th absence in the corresponding course. He assured that the proposed option would be availed as a last resort and all efforts would be made to accommodate students’ classes by rescheduling the shooting sequence of different ads.

 

DECISION

 

3.2              The Committee decided that the students would be facilitated to complete the photo shoot and the FCS Ad Campaign. However, the Committee did not accept the proposal of allowing 5th absence.

 

ACTION

 

3.3              Dr. Sayeed Ghani will convey the decision of the Committee to Dr. Sajjad Haider.

   

b)                 IBA Convocation 2009 Document.

 

3.4              The Registrar presented the proposed brochure for the IBA Convocation to be held on December 12, 2009.  He said that the proposed brochure consists of 22 pages which include Core Values and description of IBA logo also. It also includes names of faculty and policy makers - Executive Committee, Academic Board, and Board of Governors. It also has an sketch of the Centre for Entrepreneurial Development.

 

DECISION

 

3.5              The Committee recorded its appreciation for improving and enhancing the look and contents of the brochure for IBA Convocation. 

 

3.6              The Committee decided that instead of black and white, coloured sketch of the Centre for Entrepreneurial Development be presented in the brochure and other projects should also be included by increasing the number of pages from 22 to 24.

 

ACTION

 

3.7              The Registrar would incorporate the suggestions made by the Committee.

     

 

c)                  To consider and approve IBA-FCS Logo.

 

3.8              Dr. Sayeed Ghani presented the proposed IBA-FCS Logo designed by BBCL.

 

DECISION

 

3.9              The Committee decided that BBCL should be asked to give a presentation to the Executive Committee on the concept of the proposed IBA-FCS Logo.

 

ACTION

 

3.10          Ms. Erum Hafeez, Manager Communication would request M/s. BBCL to give a presentation to the Executive Committee on the concept of the proposed IBA-FCS Logo.

 

d)                 Review of Security Arrangements

 

3.11          Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that we should review our security arrangements. He said that there are insufficient armed guard at the City Campus. 

 

3.12          The Registrar said that there are few armed guards at City Campus as well as at APWA building.

 

3.13          Mr. Mobin Khalili suggested that the boundary wall should be covered with barbed wires and the existing barriers should be strengthened by installing road blockers.

 

3.14          The Registrar said that installing the road blocker would take time and cost more and also create interruption.

 

DECISION

 

3.15          The Committee decided that the Registrar in coordination with the Security Officer will further tighten and strengthen the security arrangements.   

 

e)                  Allowance or allowed overtime to ERP Implementation Team

 

3.16          The Director ICT informed the Committee that Mr. Imran Batada wants to discuss the matter of allowance or overtime to ERP Implementation Team because the ERP Implementation Team has to put in extra hours and sometimes holidays too to complete their assignments. But due to overtime entitlement limit such persons are not getting any compensation.

 

3.17          Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that in the transition phase there should be some extra cost which has to be paid. He also agreed that those who are involved in the projects and are required to put in extra hours should be given some incentive.

 

3.18          The Registrar suggested that a separate team should work on ERP and instead of involving program office staff, it would be better to hire some extra people to work under ERP head. The Program Offices will be required to provide data to the ERP staff.  He said that one Data Entry Operator and one Supervisor will be enough but the verification of the data will have to be done by the Program Office.

 

3.19          Mr. Mobin Khalili said that the deadline of December 15, 2009 for completion of data entry and verification is not realistic because about 200 entries for one student are required.

 

DECISION

 

3.20          The Committee requested the Director ICT to ask Mr. Imran Batada to present his proposal with financial implications (to be worked out in coordination with the Director Finance) and a realistic deadline for completion of data entry work to the Executive Committee.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
