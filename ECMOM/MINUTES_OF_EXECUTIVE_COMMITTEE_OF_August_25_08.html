MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON AUGUST 25, 2008 (TENTH MEETING)

The tenth meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, August 25, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Professor
  2. Mr. Inayat N. Din                             Registrar
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Moeid Sultan                              Acting Head of Finance & Accounts
  5. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                       H.R. Manager

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      Revision of Testing Service Remuneration

1.1       Dr. Shahid Mir, Coordinator Testing Service had proposed that the remuneration to faculty and staff involved in testing service and centre rent and other charges for conducting tests outside Karachi were approved by the Board back in 2001.  Since then no revision was made in the remuneration / testing service rate. He proposed that keeping in view the inflation over this period and the nature of sensitivity of this job, the remuneration, centre rent and other charges need to be reviewed. He had submitted a proposal with the existing rate, justification for increasing the rate and the proposed rate.  The proposal was reviewed at the Finance Department in consultation with Coordinator Testing Service. The proposal was finally presented to the Executive Committee for consideration.

DECISION

1.2              The Committee thoroughly reviewed the proposal and agreed that the testing remuneration should be enhanced.   

1.3       The Committee wanted to know the total budgetary impact – overall percentage increase of expenditure due to revision; the saving or deficit due to the enhancement of testing remuneration and other charges.

ACTION

1.4       The General Manager Finance & Accounts will get the budgetary impact worked out and present it to the Executive Committee in its next meeting.

Item No. 2      To consider proposal from Dr. Sayeed Ghani for enhancement of internet bandwidth for City Campus.

2.1       Dr. Sayeed Ghani in his email addressed to Executive Committee stated that at present 2 mbps bandwidth is available for the City Campus and 2 mbps for the Main Campus. He said that there it is felt by the IBA user community that the present IBA internet bandwidth has become quite slow. He proposed that the bandwidth at the City Campus be expanded from 2 mbps to 6 mbps. He said that the charges for enhancing the bandwidth from 2 mbps to 6 mbps would be US$2650 + 15% FED per month for which the budget is also available.

DECISION

2.2              The Committee agreed to the proposal for enhancing the bandwidth from 2 Mbps to 6 Mbps for the City Campus at the charges of US$2650 + 15 FED per month.

ACTION

2.3      Dr. Sayeed Ghani will request Mr. Azam Sardar, I.T. Manager to get the bandwidth of the City Campus enhanced from 2 mbps to 6 mbps.

Item No. 3      To consider proposal for revision of hourly rate for lab sessions.

3.1       Referring to the decision of the Eighth meeting of the Executive Committee in which the Acting Head of Finance & Accounts was requested to work out the financial implications and budgetary impact as a result of applying the same rate for theory and practice Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that the estimated financial impact based on last year’s data has been submitted by the Acting Head of Finance & Accounts for consideration by the Executive Committee.

DECISION:

3.2       The Committee noted that the financial impact as a result of applying the same rate for theory period and lab session was within the budget.

3.3       The Committee agreed that the payment for lab sessions should be equal to teaching sessions if a teacher is involved in the lab session.

3.4       The Committee decided that the matter be referred to the Academic Board with the recommendation to treat lab sessions equal to teaching sessions and thus per session payment to faculty at the same rate.

ACTION

3.5              The Manager H.R. will ensure that the matter is included in the agenda of the meeting of the Academic Board.

Item No. 4      To consider purchase and installation of emergency lights at the City Campus.

4.1       Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Manager Projects proposed that there is a request of emergency lights at the City Campus as sometimes the ATS does not function properly or the generator may not be working.

DECISION

4.2              The Committee wanted details about the emergency lights with complete justification and the estimated cost.

ACTION

4.3              Mr. Shameem Ahmed will arrange to provide the required details to the Executive Committee.

 There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
