MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON AUGUST 11, 2008 (EIGHTH MEETING)

The eighth meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, August 11, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                          Professor
  3. Mr. Inayat N. Din                                Registrar
  4. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Chairman, CS & MIS
  5. Mr. Mobin Khalili                                 Controller of Examinations
  6. Mr. Moeid Sultan                                Acting Head of Finance & Accounts
  7. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                         H.R. Manager

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      Strategic Direction for IBA

1.1       The Director said that he has prepared the paper on five years’ Strategic Direction for IBA having discussed with the IBA alumni, faculty, and people from outside IBA. He said that he had not presented the implementation plan which he said he would prepare after incorporating the comments / suggestions of the faculty, and students and approval of the Board. As he had already emailed the paper to all members of the Executive Committee, he invited their comments on the paper.   

1.2       The Committee congratulated the Director on preparing such a comprehensive paper for the Strategic Direction for IBA. 

1.3       The Committee suggested certain minor amendments to the Strategic Direction which were accordingly incorporated. 

DECISION

1.4       The Director will have the paper for Strategic Direction for IBA placed on the IBA portal for comments from faculty and students as well as alumni. After incorporating the comments, the paper will be presented to the Board of Governors of the IBA for approval.

Item No. 2      Remedial courses

2.1       Dr. Nasir Touheed informed the Committee that as a result of the test given to the BBA students to determine their proficiency levels in English and Mathematics, there were 57 students who needed remedial courses in both English and Mathematics.

DECISION:

2.2       The Committee decided that students who need remedial courses both in English and Mathematics will have be doing six courses in the first semester.  Out of the six courses two will be remedial courses in English and Mathematics.  In the second semester they will have to take seven courses; six from regular work load and seventh course would be of English Grammar & Composition of first semester. In addition to these two semesters, such students will be required to complete their regular Business Maths course of first semester in Summer. 

2.3      The Committee decided to consider offering remedial courses in Accounting and Economics also if needed.

ACTION

2.4      Dr. Nasir Touheed and Dr. Zeenat Ismail will inform the respective students and their parents.

Item No. 3      To consider revision of Hourly Rate for Lab Sessions.

3.1       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that the hourly rate for lab session taken by faculty members is very low compared with the rate for taking theory classes although the lab session is also taken by the same faculty. He proposed that the rate for theory classes should apply for lab sessions also.

DECISION:

3.2       The Committee requested the Acting Head of Finance & Accounts to work out the financial implications and its budgetary impact as a result of applying the same rate for theory and practice.

Item No. 4      To consider rehiring Mr. Zahid Iqbal, retired Admin Officer, CCS for Testing Services.

4.1       Mr. Mobin Khalili said that he wants a person who should be trustworthy in testing department. He said that Mr. Zahid Iqbal is an honest, sincere and dedicated person and suggested that he should be hired for testing services.

4.2       Dr. Sayeed Ghani agreed with Mr. Khalili but added that according to his knowledge Mr. Zahid Iqbal has some reservations on working in the Testing Department.

DECISION

4.3       The Committee decided that Mr. Khalili should ask Mr. Zahid Iqbal as to what are his reservations.

Item No. 5      Any other item.

a)         Redesigning the IBA admission test.

5.1       Dr. Nasir Touheed suggested that we should strengthen our test and give more weightage to maths. 

DECISION

5.2       The Committee agreed to the suggestion and decided that requirements of strong mathematics background would be placed in the Program Announcement as well as IBA website. It was also decided that a circular would be sent to all schools from where we get the intake informing the potential candidates about the requirement.

5.3       The IBA should redesign its admission test and include comprehension also.

ACTION

5.4       Dr. Nasir Touheed and Dr. Zeenat Ismail will prepare a draft letter for the Director for sending to all schools.

5.5              Dr. Nasir Touheed and Dr. Zeenat Ismail will look into the test format of IBA as well as GRE, SAT etc. and suggest a suitable format for the IBA.

b)         Flexibility in taking elective courses.

5.6              The Committee allowed students the flexibility of deciding to take any elective course after audit within one week. In such case their attendance may be transferred to the elective course they finally decide to take. However, they will not be allowed to change the elective after one week. The Program Office will handle such cases on first come first serve basis.

c)                  Attempt to murder on Mr. Waseem Arain, faculty member.

5.7              Mr. Waseem Arain, faculty member CCS, was asked to appear before the executive committee and narrate the incident wherein an attempt to murder him had been made by the younger brother of a student of IBA. He apprised the committee that a case has been filed by the state following the arrest of the accused.

DECISION:

5.8       The Committee heard Mr. Waseem Arain and decided to hold the degree of the student till the case is pending before the court of law and until further academic investigations are completed.

d)         Aftab Associates Gold Medal.

5.9       Mr. Moeid Sultan informed the Committee that M/s. Aftab Associates want to give Gold Medal in Marketing to the IBA student. Mr. Moeid wanted to know if the IBA should give medal of pure gold or the medal should be gold plated.

DECISION

5.10     The Director advised Mr. Moeid Sultan to inform M/s. Aftab Associates of the cost of pure gold medal and symbolic gold medal. He also asked Mr. Moeid to provide details of interest amount that has been accumulated over the period on the endowment to M/s. Aftab Associates.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
