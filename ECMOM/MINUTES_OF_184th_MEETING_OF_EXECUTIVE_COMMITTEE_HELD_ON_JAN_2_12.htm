The following agenda items were discussed:

DECISION

 

1.2     The Executive Committee noted that as most of the work assigned to Mr. Jawad would be completed in January 2012, therefore Mr. Jawad would be asked to present the status report in the first week of February 2012.

 

Item No.2      To consider and approve recommendations for position up-gradation.

 

2.1     The Director H.R. said that every departmental heads was requested to send nominations for promotion / position up-gradation.   She said that a lot of queries were made by the department heads related to promotion and up-gradation policy.  She said that before considering the recommendations for promotion / up-gradation she would like to give a brief review of the approved Promotion Rules for non-teaching employees.

 

2.2     Mr. Adnan Hameed, Manager H.R. presented the salient features of the promotion policy which included types of promotions, promotion ladder which has been divided into managerial cadre and non-managerial cadre, and procedure for processing the recommendations.

 

2.3     The Dean & Director said that we have to make a distinction between various streams.  The jobs at Projects Department are time bound.  So we cannot bundle them on the same footing as for example H.R. which has a long career path. He said that engineers in IBA cannot compete for other jobs as they are specialists.

 

2.4     The Director Projects said that the positions at Projects Department are task oriented and there is no ladder for promotion. He said that normally three years period is considered a reasonable period for up-gradation of positions.

 

2.5     The Dean & Director agreed that the work load has expanded from six to 12 projects and the Projects Department has the same workforce to shoulder this work load.  But he added that project management job for all these projects has been outsourced instead of building in-house capacity.

 

2.6     Explaining the procedure, the Director H.R. said that the person nominating someone for promotion will not be part of the decision making.  However, the Dean & Director did not agree with the Director H.R. as the sponsoring Director is in a better position to justify the nomination and can respond to the questions from other members. The Director H.R. then proposed that instead of a Promotion Committee, the entire EC may consider promotion cases. The Committee agreed to this proposal that the entire EC will be on the Promotion Committee.

 

2.7     The Director ICT said that the “T-Scale” he had proposed for ICT staff has not been shown in the Job Band.

 

2.8     The Dean & Director asked the Director H.R. to include T-Scale parallel to J-Scale.

 

2.9     The Dean & Director suggested that on every grade, the title should also change and it should be specific to the department along with generic title.

 

2.10    The Committee decided that a sub-committee of Promotion Committee will interview candidates, short list the candidates and present its findings to the Executive Committee.  The EC further decided that the person nominating someone for promotion/up-gradation will also be invited to the Promotion Committee.  

 

2.11    After discussing the Promotion Policy, the Committee considered the recommendations for promotion received from the Career Development Centre, and the H.R. Department.

 

2.12    Mr. Adnan Hameed, Manager H.R. presented the justification for recommending up-gradation of the position of Senior H.R. Assistant to Executive (from equivalent grade BPS-9 to 12) along with the previous and new responsibilities being discharged by the incumbent. He said that if the EC approves the recommended up-gradation of the position, it would be internally advertised. 

 

2.13    The Director Projects suggested that the Recommendation Form should have a column of the position recommended for. 

 

2.14    The Committee approved the up-gradation of the position of Sr. H.R. Assistant to “Executive”.

 

2.15    Mr. Adnan Hameed then presented the justification for recommending up-gradation of the position of Executive to Senior Executive along with the previous and new responsibilities being discharged by the incumbent.

 

2.16    The Dean & Director said that under reorganization, the Alumni and CDC services have been merged to achieve synergy and have a single point of contact with the corporate sector. The Manager Alumni will now report to the two heads; the Director H.R. and the Director Alumni. He said that there are two positions; Executive Students’ Services, and Executive Corporate Services and the recommended one is Students’ Services side of the structure.

 

2.17    The Director H.R. said that there is an Executive in the CDC but this position is needed to be up-gradated as Senior Executive because in the last two years the CDC activities have increased many folds.

 

DECISION

 

2.18    The Committee approved the up-gradation of the two positions as recommended above in 2.12 and 2.15.

 

ACTION

 

2.19    The Director H.R. would include T-Scale provided by the Director ICT parallel to J-scale.

 

2.20    The H.R. would get an internal ad issued for the up-gradation of the two positions mentioned above.

 

2.21    The H.R. would insert a column of the recommended positions in the Recommendation Form.

 

Item No.3      To consider and approve the proposal for re-organization of Finance Department.

 

3.1     The Director Finance said that in the recent years various changes have taken place at all levels in IBA as a result all operational and financial activities have also been increased tremendously. Similarly, these changes have affected financial and accounting functions. He highlighted some of the major changes relating to Finance Department.

 

3.2     The Director Finance said that to re-organize the Finance Department and to fix the responsibilities of employees as required and to accomplish the overall tasks of the Finance Department from incurrence of a transaction till its reporting, we need to have fresh professional evaluation in accordance with leading practices. He said that in the light of these facts it is the time to re-evaluate the overall activities of the Finance Department.  He presented the complete scope of work and broad objectives for re-organization of Finance Department and requested the Executive Committee to accord conceptual approval for reorganization of Finance Department.

 

3.3     The Dean & Director said that the staff working in Finance Department are very capable but their talent is under-utilized. He asked the Director Finance to see where the weaknesses are, reorganize the department and give challenges to his staff along with reward and recognition.  He did not agree to the proposal of hiring professional evaluator.  He asked the Director H.R. to work on this assignment, interview the Finance Department staff to find out what changes are needed and ensure that their potential is extracted both for the individual aspirations and organizational goals. 

 

3.4     The Dean & Director asked the Director Finance to include in the terms of External Auditors, review of internal control system for which fee may be paid. He said that he would not favour increasing the head count of Finance Department. 

 

DECISION

 

3.5     The Committee agreed with the Dean & Director on his comments noted in 3.3 and 3.4.

 

 

Item No.4      Progress Report on Data Centre Project.

 

4.1     The Director ICT reported that a joint meeting was held between ICT and Project Departments to discuss ICT Data Center at City Campus with special reference to the budget shortfall. He said that the budget for the data centre is of Rs.55.82 million. He said that if we import items such as electrical ones we may save a lot of money. He said that external electrical work including diesel generator and other items are already incorporated in new SBD and if Projects Department allocates the external electrical budget part to ICT then in return we will get quick and efficient result independent of delays and conflict of communication between parties.  He informed the Committee that Mr. Umar Makhdumi agreed to request Director Projects that Rs.11.61 million be re- appropriated for ICT data center from City Campus External Development Work. He said that Rs.7.7 million is available for civil works of ICT data center at City Campus from the savings from civil works of City Campus Admin Block. He said that in the budget Rs.20 million has been allocated for ICT Data Center at City Campus. By adding Rs.11.61 million from External Development and Rs.7.7 million from civil works savings and Rs.20 million from the budget, the total estimated amount available at this stage would be Rs.39.31 million. So there would be a short fall of Rs.16.51 million.

 

4.2     The Director Projects said that M/s. H.K. Shah was requested to provide details of Rs.55.82 million. The Director ICT said that he would ask H.K. Shah to provide details of Rs.55.82 million.

 

4.3     The Dean & Director asked the Director Projects and the Director Finance to see which project is going slowly till June 2012 so that we may make appropriations for Rs.16.5 from that project and take it to the A&FC for approval. He asked the Director ICT to continue his work according to his time line and asked him to provide exact date of completion in the next EC meeting and also inform the EC if the data centre work is as per schedule or not. 

 

4.4     The Dean & Director wanted to know as to why we should have tier 3 at Main Campus.  He said that as per his understanding only back up will be needed for the Main Campus which will not cost so much.

 

4.5     The Director ICT said that the same tier 3 is required for both the campuses because if, for any reason, the date centre at any campus is not working then the centre at other campus will remain functional.

 

4.6     Mr. Zafar Siddiqui suggested to consult Engro’s IT Head who is IBA alumnus and is willing to get involved in our data centre. The Director ICT informed the EC that he has already discussed with the Engro IT Head and would again discuss with him if needed.

 

4.7     The Dean & Director asked the Director ICT to do an exercise on what should be the structure of data centre at Main Campus; tier 3, 2 or 1 and present it to the EC.

DECISION

 

4.8     The Committee noted the up-date given by the Director ICT.

 

ACTION

 

4.9     The Director Projects and the Director Finance would see which project is going slowly upto June 2012 for making appropriations for Rs.16.5 million from that project and taking it to the A&FC for approval.

 

4.10    The Director ICT would consult Engro’s IT Head on our data centre as needed.

 

4.11    The Director ICT would do an exercise on what should be the structure of data centre at Main Campus; tier 3, 2 or 1 and present it to the EC.
