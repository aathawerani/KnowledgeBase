The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Review of GC recommendations on Promotion Cases.

 

1.1              The Dean & Director asked Mr. Adnan Hameed to present only those cases where there is disagreement between the GC and the EC.  Accordingly, Mr. Adnan Hameed identified those cases on which GC had a different view.  He said that there were three cases which are to be sent to the Selection Board, and three non-management cases, out of which one has been withdrawn.

 

1.2              The Committee considered the comments given by the GC on such cases where it had differed, and the justifications provided by the HR in support of those cases.

 

DECISION

 

1.3              The Executive Committee endorsed the justifications given by the HR / EC’s earlier decision and asked Mr. Adnan Hameed to inform the GC accordingly.

 

Item No.2       To consider proposed policy framework for IBA non-teaching staff engagement in assignments / activities not directly related to their job.

 

2.1       Mr. Adnan Hameed wanted to discuss the proposed policy framework. However, the Dean & Director advised Mr. Adnan Hameed to first circulate the draft to the GC and obtain their views before bringing it to the EC for discussion.

 

Item No.3       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Briefing on DHA City.

 

3.1       The Registrar briefed the members about the 10 acres of land at DHA City situated at Education District of DCK that has been offered at Rs.500,000/- per acre (subsidized rate) plus External Civil Infrastructure Development charges at Rs.1,500,000/-  per acre. He said that an MoU with DHA will be signed on March 8, 2016.

 

3.2       About the utilization of the plot he said that they have envisaged various plans for utilization of this plot. The Architect would be engaged to prepare the plan. He said that there would be a common user block of 4 floors. Three floors will have seminar room, classroom and other facilities; ground floor will have offices. Another will be residential block with cafeteria. He said that the Centre for Executive Education can be accommodated there. National Talent Hunt program students can be provided accommodation over there. An Academic Block could also be constructed to cater to the future needs.

 

3.3       The Dean & Director said that we should consider building community college so that the students specially from Dadu, Jamshoro, Thatta, Hyderabad, New Karachi could be trained and prepared for our tests. Students from lower Sindh and upper Karachi can complete inter science, commerce, arts, or BSc and then we will attract them to apply for our Bachelors and Masters programs.  The community college would be run by IBA; its standard will be of IBA.  We can also build boys and girls hostels. Another option is running zero semester there where we can train NTHP students for the whole semester. He said that there are many options we have to think about it and requested the EC members to discuss with others also and give suggestions.  He said that we have to tell BoG on March 9 how we plan to use this plot of land.

 

DECISION

 

3.4       EC members would send their suggestions regarding utilization of the plot at DHA City.

 

b)     Proposal for Reward for Assistants engaged in Summer School Program.

 

3.5       Mr. Abdul Wajed said that Mr. Ahmed Ali, Mr. Mohammad Ayaz, and Mr. Jamil were assisting Ms. Maheen Ghouri in Summer School Program. He suggested that the three may be considered for the award.

 

3.6       The Dean & Director said that he would ask Mr. Izhar Husain Mirza to run the Summer School program because they have marketing department also.  He further said that Mr. Abdul Wajed would continue to look after Summer Program.

 

DECISION

 

3.7       The Dean & Director suggested that when the nominations for award is considered, those three names should also be included.

 

3.8       The Dean & Director would request Mirza Izhar to run the Summer Program School.

 

c)      Rotation / relocation of IBA Staff.

 

3.9       The Dean & Director asked Mr. Adnan Hameed that as decided earlier, the Management and Executive staff who are working with the same department for quite a long time should be rotated / relocated.   

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
