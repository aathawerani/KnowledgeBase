MINUTES OF THE 123RD MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, OCTOBER 11, 2010

 

The 123rd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 11, 2010 at 9:30 a.m. at the Societies’ Meeting Room, Academic Block, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.  Dr. Muhammad Nishat                         Associate Dean

2.  Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN           Registrar

3.  Dr. Wasim A. Khan                             Acting Associate Dean

4.  Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                          Director H.R.

5.  Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                              Director ICT

 

The following attended the meeting on special invitation:

 

1.      Mr. Abdul Rehman, Project Engineer

2.      Mr. Mehmood Bughio, Project Engineer

3.      Mr. Javed Islam, Project Engineer, HEC Projects

 

The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve Revised Cost Estimate for construction of a new KESC sub-station and relocation of the four generators at the City Campus.

 

1.1       This item was deferred as the Director Projects and the Director Finance who were to brief /give their comments to the EC on this issue were not present due to their illness. 

 

 

Item No. 2      To consider and approve the award of work of data centre civil works at IBA Main Campus.

 

2.1       Mr. Abdul Rehman, Project Engineer requested the EC to consider this item, as an urgent decision was required on this issue.  Accordingly the Executive Committee considered the note submitted by the Director Projects in which he had mentioned that:

 

a)      In its meetings held on July 5 and July 12, 2010 the Executive Committee approved to relocate data centre to Multipurpose building ground floor after coordination with ICT department.  After going through the complete requirement of ICT department for data centre the consultant M/s. ICON was asked to design the data centre. The detail drawing & Bill of Quantity were prepared by the Architect M/s. ICON and these were sent to ICT department on August 13, 2010 for their review and approval.

 

b)      The Engineering Estimate prepared by Architect is of Rs.4.5 Million (Excluding furniture) for the civil works of Data centre at ground floor of Multipurpose Building. The Committee advised the Director Projects and the Director ICT to review and reduce the cost estimate and submit the same for approval of Dean and Director. After detailed discussion the estimated cost of civil works was reduced to Rs.2.1 million and submitted the same to Director IBA.  At that time the electrical drawings were not received from the Architect. However, during the preparation of Tender Documents for civil works the drawings of Electrical works were received from Architect and included in the bid document.

 

c)      The invitation of bids was floated to 18 contractors who were pre-qualified for small civil works up to Rs.10 Million. Among 18 Contractors only following five contractors collected the tender documents:

 

1.    M/s. AJM

2.    M/s. B.G

3.    M/s. Fabcon

4.    M/s. Emperor

5.    M/s. Shafiq &Co.

 

d)     Among five contractors four contractors submitted their bids on specified date and time.  M/s AJM regretted to submit the bid. The bids were opened on same day in the presence of IBA officials, Project Managers (M/s. INTEG) & Contractor’s representatives. Following were the results of the bids:

 

1)      M/s B.G C.E & M Company              Rs.3,366,085

2)      M/s Shafiq & Co.                          Rs.3,520,662

3)      M/s Emperor                                Rs.3,637,786  

4)      M/s Fabcon                                 Rs.4,553,415

 

e)      The bids were sent to Project Manager for their Scrutiny. The Project Manager submitted their report on September 30, 2010. According to their evaluation following were the position and bid prices of the contractor:

 

1)      M/s Shafiq & Company                   Rs.3,317,110               1st Lowest

2)      M/s B.G C.E & M Company              Rs.3,366,085               2nd Lowest

3)      M/s Emperor                                Rs.3,657,149               3rd Lowest

4)      M/s Fabcon                                 Rs.4,567,995               Highest

 

f)       As per evaluation and scrutiny of Project Managers (M/s. Integ) the 1st lowest bidder is M/s. Shafiq & Co. at a cost of Rs.3,317,110/- whose bid is 1.45% lower than 2nd lowest bidder. The Project Managers (M/s Integ) recommended the award of work to the lowest bidder i.e. M/s. Shafiq & Co.  The break up cost of the lowest bidder is as under:

 

·         Cost of Civil works                 Rs.2,047,745          

·         Cost of Electrical Works         Rs.1,443,950

 

g)      The cost of civil works of lowest bidder is 6.2 % lower than the approved Estimate which was Rs.2,183,457/-.

 

h)      The work of Electrical conduit and laying of 4 core 95 sqmm cable work for data centre UPS was also included because it had to be done along with civil works being its integral part.

 

i)        Based on the recommendation of Project Managers (M/s. Integ) the Director Projects held a meeting with 1st lowest and second lowest bidders and informed them their positions after evaluation and advised them to submit their letter that the amount quoted in the bid was their final amount of bid and they would execute the work as per specifications given in tender documents. The Contractors submitted their letters accordingly.

 

j)        The Executive committee was requested to approve in principle the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s. Shafiq & Co. at a cost of Rs.3,317,110/- so that the case can be presented in CPC for approval.

 

2.2       To a question Mr. Abdul Rehman clarified that the cost estimate of air-conditioning and electrical work is not included in this estimate.

 

2.3       The Registrar suggested to present the diagram of the existing space.  Mr. Abdul Rehman promised to provide the same to the Registrar.

 

DECISION

 

2.4       The Committee in principle agreed to award the work of Data Centre civil work at IBA Main Campus to the lowest bidder M/s. Shafiq & Co. at a cost of Rs.3,317,110/- subject to approval by the Central Purchase Committee.

 

ACTION

 

2.5       Mr. Abdul Rehman, Project Engineer would provide diagram of the space to the Registrar.

 

2.6       The Director Projects would send the proposal for award of work of Data Centre civil work at IBA Main Campus to the Central Purchase Committee for approval.
