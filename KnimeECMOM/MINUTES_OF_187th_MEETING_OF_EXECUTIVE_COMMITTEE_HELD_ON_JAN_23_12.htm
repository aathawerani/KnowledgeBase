MINUTES OF 187TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON

MONDAY, JANUARY 23, 2012

 

The 187th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, January 23, 2012 at 9:30 a.m. at Conference Room, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                               Associate Dean, FCS

2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                                        Associate Dean, FBA

3.      Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN                               Registrar

4.      Mr. Ali Taha                                                      Director Finance

5.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                                  Director Projects

6.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                              Director ICT

7.      Mr. Zafar Siddiqui                                              Director CED

8.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                                          Director H.R.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

 

The following agenda items were discussed:      

  

 

Item No.1       To consider and approve prequalification of M/s. Siemens for the work of Electrical Infrastructure at Main Campus, Boys Hostel, and the Staff Colony.

 

1.1       This item was withdrawn by the Director Projects.

 

 

Item No.2       Presentation on proposed designing for the houses for professors.

 

2.1       The Director Projects said that the Executive Committee had assigned the job of planning for the new houses in staff colony as replacement of old depleted houses as the renovation of old houses will be uneconomical. The old houses type A are built on plots of 2000 Syd and type B at 1435 Syd - each having built up area of 2100 Sft and 1400 Sft.

He said that the conceptual basic designs have been prepared by our Architect Ms. Sana Tanveer.

 

2.2       The Director Projects requested the EC to witness the conceptual designs and give their comments including their requirements for their living spaces and the broad guidelines for finishes. He further requested the EC to also approve the space and facilities limits for type C and D apartments. He said that later on they would circulate questionnaire for obtaining requirements for colony master planning from the residents and the concerned IBA departments. He said that based on the guidelines they would prepare the RFP for design competition as well as the master planning of staff colony.

 

2.3       The Registrar suggested to defer this activity for two years because in view of the financial constraints we should not start new projects.  He said that our number one priority should be to get the girls hostel ready.

 

2.4       The Director Projects requested to allow him to get the Master Plan of the Staff Town prepared.  The Master Plan would include sewerage, electricity, etc.

 

2.5       Mr. Shameem Ahmed suggested to get a rough estimate and approach HEC or Sindh Government for funding in future and in the meantime feasibility report may be prepared.

 

2.6       The Registrar said that the concept, in principle, may be approved and in-house feasibility report may be prepared for G+2 which would not require elevator.

 

2.7       The Director Projects said that for Master Plan they need an architect for which he would bring a proposal to the EC for approval.

 

DECISION

 

2.8       The Committee deferred the proposal for houses for professors. However, the Committee agreed that an in-house feasibility report be prepared by the Projects Department.

 

2.9       The Committee also in principle agreed that the Master Plan for the IBA Staff Town be prepared.

 

ACTION

 

2.10     The Director Projects would get an in-house feasibility report prepared and present it to the EC.  He would also present a formal proposal for Master Plan for the IBA Staff Town to the EC for consideration.

 

 

Item No.3       Monthly Progress report on Projects for the month ended December 31, 2011.

 

3.1       The Director Projects presented monthly progress report till December 31, 2011 on the following current and up-coming projects:

 

FCS Building, City Campus: The Director Projects reported that there has been 16% physical progress till December 2011 against the scheduled progress of 48%.  He said that due to late removal of debris from roof, and late shifting of 500 KVA Genset, the project is expected to be delayed by at least a month. The FCS staff on the right hand side of ground floor has shifted but the material and fixtures of rooms have not been shifted as yet, thereby providing a pretext to contractor for further delay. He said that against the schedule finish date of July 2012, he would try to complete it by September 2012.

 

Administration Building, City Campus: The project has been 100% completed.  The chiller which encountered a manufacturing fault will be operational in the 3rd week of January 2012 as was informed to EC in its meeting held on December 26, 2011.

 

Aman Tower, City Campus: The work is going on as per schedule. Its scheduled finish date is April 30, 2014.

 

Auditorium, City Campus: The Auditorium at City Campus would be completed in 30 months time i.e. by April 2014.

 

Relocation of Generators and Construction of new KESC Sub-Station, City Campus:  Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that Siemens performance as a contractor is quite disappointing. Their sub-contractor is still struggling with the plinth to come out of the ground level. The completion date which was January 2012 will have to be revised and the project is expected to be completed in April 2012.

 

New 2000 KW Connection, City Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that basically this load is for the Aman Tower + Auditorium and will be pursued on completion of the basement of the building. 

 

The Director ICT said that his data centre will be ready by June 2012 and before that he needs power supply.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that nobody had given him the requirement for the power supply for the data centre. However, he said that the 850 KW will be available by June 2012 which will cater to the requirements.

 

Library, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that 3.5% against the target of 5% was achieved till December 2011.  He said that a lot of structural changes have been made after making test. The lay out was also changed with the approval of the Librarian. The Library will be completed in December 2012 against the scheduled date of August 2012.

 

Administration Building, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that tender has been opened, and evaluation completed. BoG approval is expected in its meeting scheduled for February 4, 2012. The duration of the project is 24 months. It will be completed in December 2013.

    

Electrical Infrastructure System, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that tenders will be issued to the pre-qualified contractors in January 2012.  The completion date of the project is December 2013.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed clarified that in the scope of electrical infrastructure work boys hostel is not included.

 

Sports Grounds & Ancillary Works, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that Letter of Intent has been issued to M/s. D.R. Construction.  The scheduled finish date of the project is May 2012.

 

New Academic Block, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that 30% physical work has been completed against the target of 38% for this period. The project would be completed in July 2012.

 

Students Centre, Main Campus: There has been twelve percent physical progress as against the target of 15%.  The project would be completed in December 2012.

 

External Development & Infrastructure Works, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that the tenders would be issued to the pre-qualified contractors towards the end of January 2012.

 

Mr. Zafar Siddiqui suggested to start plantation right now because the Aman CED will be opened on April 15, 2012.

 

Prayer Hall, Main Campus: Tender has been awarded. The completion date of the Prayer Hall is August 2012.

 

Aman CED Building, Main Campus: The Director Projects reported that 62% physical progress has been achieved against the target of 70% till end of December 2011. He reported that 90% of the time gained in earlier stage has been lost due to unfavourable law and order conditions in Karachi.  The scheduled finish date of this project is April 2012.

 

New Boys Hostel, Main Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed observed that the Project Manager had given an unrealistic deadline to complete this project in one year.  He said that it would take at least 18 months.  He said that he would try to complete this project by July/August 2012.  The schedule finish date for this project was 31st March 2012.

 

Visiting Faculty Hostel, Main Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that the work has been started; it is a one year project and the chances are very high that the project would be completed on time i.e. by end of March 2013.

 

New Girls Hostel, Main Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that due to financial constraints it was decided to make the hostel ready in two phases; two residential blocks now and two residential blocks later.  The Project is scheduled to be completed by end of October 2013.

 

Laying of Water and Sewerage Lines, Main Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that only 62% against the target of 100% has been achieved till December 2011. The project was to be completed by October 2011 but it has been delayed due to obstructions / encroachments in R.O.W. approved by Karachi University. He said that the matter is under discussion with Karachi University.  IBA has already applied for change of route but still no response has been received from KU.

 

Three New Power Connections, Main Campus:  Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that a final approval along with cost estimate of Rs.87 million has been received from KESC.  He said that they have approached KESC to review and reduce the estimate.  He hoped that by June 2012 IBA may get the electricity from the new power connections.

 

DECISION

 

3.2       The Committee noted the progress upto December 31, 2011 as presented by the Director Projects and the Director HEC Projects.

 

 

Item No.4       Progress Report on Data Centre Project.

 

4.1       The Director ICT reported that bids submission date is January 25, 2012. M/s. H.K. Shah is working on the design of the data centre for Main Campus.

 

DECISION

 

4.2       The Committee noted the up-date given by the Director ICT.

 

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
