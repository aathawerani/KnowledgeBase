MINUTES OF THE 104TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON SATURDAY, MAY 29, 2010

 

The 104th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Saturday, May 29, 2010 at 10:30 a.m. at the Conference Room, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                             Director

  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Associate Dean

  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Associate Dean

  4. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN          Registrar

  5. Mr. Ali Taha                                    Director Finance

  6. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                 Director Projects

  7. Dr. Arshad. Siddiqi                            Director ICT

  8. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director H.R.

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider status implementation report 2009-10 on Strategic Direction for IBA and to finalize the Business Plan and Academic Plan for the year 2010-11.

 

1.1       The Director H.R. presented the Implementation Report 2009-10 provided by concerned individuals on the Strategic Direction for IBA. The status reports pertained to the following Strategic Goals:

 

·         Governance and Internal Management

·         Technology up-gradation

·         Physical Infrastructure

·         Faculty Development

·         Community Outreach

·         Quality Assurance and Academic Development

·         External Linkages and Partnerships

·         Academic Organization

·         Resource Mobilization

·         Communication and Public Affairs

·         Career Development Centre

·         Organizational Development

 

1.2       The Director advised the Director Projects to include only those projects in the list of New Buildings which are to be completed or will become functional during 2010-11 as well as those construction of which will be started during 2010-11.

 

1.3       The Director Projects added that the FCS building would be started in October 2010 and the Library would be started in January 2011.

 

1.4       The Director H.R. reported that 09 faculty members would spend a semester at National University of Singapore and Babson College. She also reported that 11 Teaching Assistants have been appointed. She said that 21 new faculty members which include 4 Ph.D’s were appointed since May 2008. She further said that out of 24 faculty members who left IBA during this period, 13 had only local qualification of Master’s degree so there was a quality substitution.

 

DECISION

 

1.5       The Status Implementation Report 2009-10 was noted by the Committee.

 

1.6       The Director asked the Director H.R. to circulate the Strategic Director for IBA among all EC members identifying their goals which they have to achieve. 

 

1.7       The Director suggested that the business plan should highlight what has been done this year and what is planned to be done in the coming year.

 

 

Item No. 2      Manning requirements.

 

2.1       The Director H.R. presented the manning requirements given by various departments as well as the proposed Program Office structure.

 

DECISION

 

2.2       The Director asked the Director H.R. to remove all frozen as well as vacant and approved positions except the positions approved / recommended for the centres.  He further said that for project related requirements which are to be charged to the projects, the concerned HoD would be required to give justification for such positions and if the Director is satisfied with the justifications, he would allow appointment on such positions.

 

2.3       The Director asked the Director H.R. to make provision for 10 new faculty positions during 2010-11 and if the 10 positions are filled during the year, then they will increase the number.  

  

2.4       The Director said that on his return he would have a meeting with the program directors and discuss the Program Office structure.

 

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

3.1       The Director asked the Director Projects to transfer the furniture head to the Registrar so that he may plan for the future requirements and make bulk purchases.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
