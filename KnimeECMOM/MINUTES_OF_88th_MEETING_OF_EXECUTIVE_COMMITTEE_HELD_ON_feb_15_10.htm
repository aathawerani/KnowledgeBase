The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider email from Controller of Examinations regarding placement of separate computers with printers at both the campuses for the faculty members.

 

1.1       Mr. Mobin Khalili reported that it has been more than a year when the Executive Committee at his request had decided that separate computers with printers, which should not be connected to the Network / Server, will be placed at both campuses for the faculty members to type and print their examination question papers in secured environment. He said that the decision is still awaiting implementation. As a result faculty members, having no other option, are still using the Network PC’s and printers for typing and printing of their question papers and thus seriously compromising the confidentiality of the question papers. He said that he discussed the issue with few faculty members who have strong IT background and they pointed out serious risks in using the Network computers and printers for typing and printing of question papers. He said that according to them, anyone with computer background, whether student or staff member, can make access to the files of Network computers used by faculty. Being aware of the risks due to their computer background, none of them uses Network computers or printers for this purpose. He said that they cannot continue to live with this hazardous risk that could one day prove very damaging for IBA and its examination system. He again suggested that independent computers with printers should immediately be arranged and placed in cubicles in the faculty rooms at both campuses. These computers and printers should not be connected to the Network/Server. These computers and printers will be used by the faculty exclusively for typing and printing of question papers. He suggested that a notice from the Associate Deans should also go to all faculty members informing them the potential risks of using Network computers and printers for printing of question papers and requesting them to use dedicated computers and printers for this purpose.

 

1.2              The Director ICT said that the printers are locally connected but he said that some teachers have systems which are directly connected with the net printers.  He said that the computers are disabled from  the network but he would recheck it.

 

1.3              Mr. Mobin Khalili said that he wants PC and printers without having any connection with network.

 

1.4              The Director ICT said that he would install two computes with printer at both the campuses which would not have any connection with the network.

 

DECISION

 

1.5              The Committee decided that two computers with stand alone printers will be installed at each campus on priority and when it is installed the Director ICT would inform the Dean & Director about the installation. On confirmation from the Director ICT, the Dean & Director would issue a notification to all faculty in this regard.  The draft of the notification would be prepared by the Controller of Examinations.

 

ACTION

 

1.6              The Director ICT would get two computers with printers without having any networking connections installed at each campus and confirm to the Director about the installation.

 

1.7              The Controller of Examination would send a draft of the notification to the Director for issuance from the Director to all faculty members.   

 

 

Item No. 2      To consider email from Mr. Mobin Khalili regarding decision of the Executive Committee on office vehicles.

 

2.1              Mr. Mobin Khalili said that the following notice was issued in compliance with the decision of the 82nd meeting of the Executive Committee held on January 4, 2010:

 

“As per the decision of the Executive Committee all office vehicles and drivers at the City Campus will be part of the Pool; no vehicle or driver would be specifically at the disposal of any department.  Mr. Faisal Rizvi, Security Officer, City Campus, is being assigned additional duty as Transport Incharge for the City Campus.  He will be responsible to manage the Pool, maintain relevant record and provide transport for official purposes on request and submission of requisition form in advance. A detailed SOP for the City Campus Car Pool shall be circulated shortly.”

 

2.2              It was clarified that only the first sentence of the above para is part of the EC decision.  The second para is not a part of the EC decision of January 4, 2010.

.

2.3              Mr. Khalili wanted to know if there is any change in the decision by the Executive Committee in its subsequent meetings which he could not attend so that the decision could be implemented accordingly.

 

2.4              Mr. Khalili stated that the Director ICT wants a Cultus exclusively for his department.

 

2.5              The Director said that every vehicle will be in the pool.  However, preference will be given to Security, ICT and Maintenance staff for official purposes.  He said that in regard to the implementation of this priority allocation which should be made by the Car Pool incharge i.e. by Mr. Faisal Rizvi at the City Campus and by Mr. Rashid Ali Khan at the Main Campus. He asked Mr. Khalili to prepare a SOP in consultation with Maintenance, Security and ICT.

 

2.6              The Director clarified that in emergency where late sitting is required and the concerned employee has to come back early morning, then the concerned official may take the vehicle home but at his / her own responsibility as well as at the responsibility of the person authorizing the employee to take the vehicle home.  He further said that it should not be made a habit.

 

2.7              Mr. Zafar Siddiqui suggested that the Cultus at the City Campus should be replaced with one old Corolla because at times some alumni visiting IBA are to be dropped back.

 

2.8              The Director said that the Registrar will look into it.   

 

DECISION

 

2.9              The Committee agreed with the Director that every vehicle will be in the pool.  However, preference will be given to Security, ICT and Maintenance for official use. The implementation of the decision regarding priority allocation of the transport should be made by the Car Pool incharge i.e. by Mr. Faisal Rizvi at the City Campus and by Mr. Rashid Ali Khan at the Main Campus. 

 

2.10          The Committee requested Mr. Khalili to prepare a SOP in consultation with Maintenance, Security and ICT.

 

ACTION

 

2.11          Mr. Mobin Khalili would prepare a SOP for vehicles management at City Campus in consultation with Maintenance, Security and ICT.

 

2.12          The Registrar would look into the suggestion of Mr. Zafar Siddiqui for replacing the Cultus with old Corolla at City Campus. 

 

 

Item No. 3      Briefing on 360 degree feedback by Ms. Rahila Narejo, CEO & HR Consultant.

 

3.1       The Director H.R. said that the section heads and business units have completed six months or more so it was time to rate them.  She said that in addition to the performance appraisal exercise, it was suggested that they should obtain 360 degree feedback.  She said that they contacted various consultants and after going through the procedural requirements, they selected M/s. N.H.R. Private Limited (Narejo Human Resources) and have invited Ms. Rahila Narejo, CEO & H.R. Consultant of NHR to give a brief presentation on 360 feedback.

 

3.2       The Director said that there were two reasons for this feedback. It circumscribes the discretionary powers of the Director.  One individual cannot really assess the direct reportees. So in order to bring in the perspectives of all the stakeholders feedback from the supervisors, peer and boss is necessary and this reduces the degree of arbitrariness.  Second reason is that they are already using this for the faculty so there should not be a different rule for others.  He further said that it is easy to manage the boss but it is difficult to manage the sub-ordinates. He asked the members present to raise their concerns, if any, so that Ms. Rahila Narejo may explain to them.

 

3.3              Ms. Rahila Narejo after introducing herself and her organization, made a detailed presentation on 360 degree feedback exercise.  The members present raised certain queries which were also responded by Ms. Rahila Narejo.

 

DECISION

 

3.4              At the end of the presentation the members thanked Ms. Rahila Narejo for making an exciting presentation.

 

 

Item No. 4      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Request from Mr. Faizul Hasan Farooqui to allow him to continue his studies on compassionate grounds.

 

4.1       Mr. Faizul Hasan Farooqui stated in his email that he had appealed to the High Court and the Honorable High Court acknowledged it and passed an order saying “We keeping in view the fact that the petitioner is a bright student and capable to take MBA classes, he should not be restrained from continuing his classes in MBA course”. However, he said that the Registrar did not allow him to attend classes.

 

4.2              Dr. Nasir Afghan informed the Committee that the legal advisor of IBA had advised him that they have implemented the decision of the Honourable High Court by offering the five courses in Fall 2009 and one in Spring 2010 and now the case is pending with the Supreme Court. He said that the Registrar has informed that the legal advisor has advised not to allow Mr. Faizul Hasan in the next semester and that by doing this there would not be any contempt of court.

 

4.3              The Director read out the order of the High Court of Sindh and in light of the Order, the Committee was convinced that IBA has fulfilled the order of the Honourable Sindh High Court by offering six courses.

 

DECISION

 

4.4              The Committee decided that the Registrar would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mr. Faizul Hasan Farooqui that by following the order of the High Court of Sindh IBA has allowed Mr. Farooqui five courses in Fall 2009 and one course in Spring 2010.

 

ACTION

 

4.5              The Registrar would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to Mr. Faizul Hasan Farooqui.
