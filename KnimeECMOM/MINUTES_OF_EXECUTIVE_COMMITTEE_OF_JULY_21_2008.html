MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON JULY 21, 2008 (FIFTH MEETING)

The fifth meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, July 21, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                        Professor
  2. Mr. Inayat N. Din                              Registrar
  3. Dr. Wasim A. Khan                            Acting Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Mobin Khalili                               Controller of Examinations
  5. Mr. Moeid Sultan                              Acting Head of Finance & Accounts
  6. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                       H.R. Manager

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No. 1      Renovation of the City Campus Auditorium

1.1       The Director said that the Auditorium of the City Campus does not need major repair and renovation; replacement of chairs will be enough at the moment. The Director was informed that along with chairs, repair of false ceiling and water proofing of the roof are also needed.

DECISION

1.2       The Committee decided that an estimate for the necessary renovation work at the City Campus Auditorium be obtained. The Committee further decided that since there is no budgetary allocation for such work, the budget will be re-appropriated through the Board of Governors of the IBA.

ACTION

1.3       The Committee decided that the Registrar will request the Manager Projects to get an estimate for the renovation work of the IBA City Campus Auditorium.   

 Item No. 2      Shuttle service between IBA Main Campus and City Campus for the students, faculty and staff. 

2.1       The Director said that because of the ever increasing prices of fuel, it is becoming unbearable to the faculty, students and staff to commute between the IBA Main Campus and City Campus on their own vehicles. He said that the IBA should facilitate them and start shuttle services for them for which they may be required to pay nominal fares.

DECISION:

2.2       The Committee appreciated the decision to start shuttle services between the two campuses for faculty, students and staff at a nominal fare.

ACTION

2.3      The Registrar will instruct the Transport Incharge to arrange shuttle services.

Item No. 3      Digitalization of all minutes of the BoG, A&FC, Academic Board and the Selection Board.

The Director wanted to know if the minutes of the BoG, A&FC, Academic Board and the Selection Board are digitalized or not to which the reply of the Manager H.R. was in the negative.

DECISION

The Committee decided that all the minutes of the BoG, A&FC, Academic Board and the Selection Board should be digitalized.

ACTION

The Manager H.R. in coordination with MIS personnel will get all the minutes digitalized.

There being no other business, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
