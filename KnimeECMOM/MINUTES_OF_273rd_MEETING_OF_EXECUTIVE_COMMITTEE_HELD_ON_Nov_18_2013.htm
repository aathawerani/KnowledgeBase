3.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                                              Director Projects

4.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                           Director Finance

5.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                          Acting Head of ICT

6.      Mr. Adnan Hameed                                                        Manager H.R.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No. 1      Status Reports from:

 

Manager H.R.: 

 

1.1       Appointment of an Executive Secretary for the Registrar:  Mr. Adnan Hameed reported that the position was advertised on October 27, 2013. He said that the list of short-listed candidates have been forwarded to the Registrar.

 

1.2       H.R. to include exam duty also in faculty’s performance evaluation: Mr. Adnan Hameed said that the faculty attendance report during exams invigilation would also be shared with the Calibration Committee.

 

DECISION

 

1.3       The up-dates given above were noted and approved.

 

Item No.2       To consider and approve the payment of additional project management fee to NESPAK for extended period of 5 months in respect of project “Construction of KESC Sub-station and Relocation of Four Generators at IBA, City Campus.

 

2.1       Mr. Shameem Ahmed stated that the contract for the construction of KESC Sub-station and Relocation of four Generators at IBA, City Campus was awarded to Siemens and the work commenced on September 4, 2011. The completion period was six months. The Consultant for the project was EA Consultants (Pvt.) Ltd., while the project management services were provided by NESPAK. However, due to various unavoidable reasons (which were already brought to the notice of and approved by CPC and the EC), completion of the project was unduly delayed.  Ultimately it was completed on July 31, 2012.  The extension of time was approved by CPC in its meeting held on March 16, 2013 and reconfirmed by removing an ambiguity on November 2, 2013.

 

2.2       Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that as per contract agreement with NESPAK, the fee payable to the Project Manager is as follows:

 

o   Total cost of the Project                                         Rs.44,072,768

o   NESPAK’s fee @3.5%                                              Rs.  1,542,547

o   80% of the above fee payable to

o   NESPAK in six equal monthly Instalments                    Rs.  1,234,038

o   Therefore, per month fee is                                     Rs.     205,673

             

2.3       Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that the terms and conditions of IBA’s contract with NESPAK stipulate that project management fee for the extended period will also be payable on pro rata basis viz @Rs.205,673/- per month.  As such an additional amount of Rs.205,673 x 5 months = Rs.1,028,365 is also payable to NESPAK.

 

2.4       Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that the proposal was presented to the CPC in its meeting held on November 16, 2013 which has approved the payment of additional fee of Rs.1,028,365/- to NESPAK.  Accordingly, he requested the EC to approve the additional payment of Rs.1,028,365 to NESPAK. Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that the actual cost of the project is lower than what was estimated. He said that NESPAK has agreed to give some discount and hoped that the additional fee may be around Rs.800,000/- after discount but he requested approval from EC for Rs.1 million.  The Committee suggested to negotiate with NESPAK and obtain as much discount as possible. 

 

DECISION

 

2.5       The Committee approved the payment of additional fee of Rs.1 million to NESPAK.

 

ACTION

 

2.6       Mr. Shameem Ahmed would try to get the amount of fee reduced from NESPAK to the extent possible.

 

Item No.3       Walk through of IBA Library of Main Campus.

 

3.1       The Committee visited the IBA Library under renovation at the Main Campus.  The Registrar suggested that the front portion of the library be cleaned and the pathway be developed before the convocation to which the Director Projects replied that instructions have already been passed on to the contractor. The Head Librarian and the entire library staff also visited the site. The Head Librarian suggested reducing the sizes of the pipe shafts on the ground floor. Director Projects agreed to it.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
