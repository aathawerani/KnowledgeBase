The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Up-date on Boys’ Hostel – Block ‘B’.

                       

1.1       The Registrar had requested the Projects Department to present the fortnightly status report on construction work of Block ‘B’ of Boys’ Hostel.  The hostel was needed to be completed in view of the agreement with the Federal Board of Revenue to conduct MBA(Tax Management) Program for officers of the FBR commencing Fall 2015.  As per agreement IBA will provide accommodation for 20 male participants in the Boys Hostel and for 5 female participants in the Girls Hostel.  The Registrar vide his email of March 3, 2015 had requested the Projects Department to initiate the following actions:

 

a.      The construction work on Block ‘B’ at the Boys Hostel be expedited.

b.      Arrangements be made to cordon off Zone 1 of the Block B from the rest of the block, so as to enable completing of civil works of 17 rooms (Room 57 to Room 73), lounge and common toilets, making them ready in all respects by 01 August 2015.

c.       Arrangements be made for provision of temporary supply of water and electric power from utilities available in the already commissioned Block ‘A’.

d.      Furniture requirements be tendered soonest and supplier be asked for phased delivery, with those for Rooms (57 to 73) and lounge furniture to be delivered latest by 10 August 2015.  

 

1.2       Mr. Mahmood Ali reported that they have divided Block ‘B’ into Zone 1 and Zone 2. For Zone 1 uptil March 31, 2015 from Excavation to First Floor Beams and Slab Pouring have been 100 % completed. Layout of 2nd floor is complete and formwork is in progress.  Shop drawing approval and procurement of HVAC & Plumbing Works and Electrical Works are in progress which would be completed by September 29, 2015 and October 5, 2015 respectively.  He reported that from Excavation to First floor columns pouring of Zone 2 has also been 100% complete.  He said that in zone 1 block masonry work is almost 90% complete.  He said that they would try that the wet work is 100% completed by August 2015. He said that the ground floor tiling will be completed by end of July 2015. He said that by end of May 2015 overall structure will be completed and work for electricity and water services will be going on in parallel. He said that procurement of furniture needs to be initiated. The Dean & Director asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to ask Mr. Abdul Rahman to issue tender for the furniture. The Registrar suggested to prepare a mock up room so that other pieces of furniture could be fabricated on the basis of approved samples in the mock up room.  Mr. Mahmood Ali said that by May 10, 2015 a mock up room will be ready. Mr. Mahmood Ali said that there is no attached toilet and AC.  

 

1.3       The Dean & Director said that electricity and water can temporarily be taken from Block ‘A’.  He said that for the first semester FBR students can be accommodated in Block ‘A’ and other students can be provided accommodation in Block ‘B’.

 

1.4       The Dean & Director asked if it could be possible to increase number of toilets in Block ‘C’.  Mr. Mahmood Ali said that there was no additional space available for the toilet; the only option is to convert two rooms into toilets on each floor. The Dean & Director said that Block ‘C’ can be modified for the FBR students so that they may feel comfortable and the program may continue. He asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to coordinate with NBCL and see how many rooms can be provided with attached toilets. He clarified that Block ‘B’ will remain as it is; in Block ‘C we can make modifications and if needed split ACs can also be provided.

 

1.5       Mr. Mahmood Ali said that the modifications of DBs etc in the existing Boys Hostel is not in anybody’s scope of work and being a civil engineer, he does not have the expertise in it.  He requested help in this regard.  The Dean & Director asked Mr. Mushtaq to help out Mr. Mahmood Ali in electrical work.

 

1.6       Mr. Mahmood Ali said that septic tank is overflowing because of which work on Block ‘C’ is affected. He said that as per schedule Block ‘C’ would be ready by January 2016.   Mr. Fahim Uddin said that a soakage pit was prepared but it was only for one block.  He said that at least two more soakage pits are needed. The Dean & Director advised to plan it and asked Mr. Fahim to get the septic tank cleared whenever needed.

 

DECISIONS & ACTIONS

 

1.7       The EC decided that electricity and water can temporarily be taken from Block ‘A’, and for the first semester FBR students can be accommodated in Block ‘A’ and other students can be provided accommodation in Block ‘B’.

 

1.8       The EC asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to get Block ‘C’ modified by NBCL and provide attached toilets and make provision for split ACs so that FBR students they may feel comfortable and the program may continue.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)                  In-house Fresh water supply connection – Admin Initiative.

 

2.1       Mr. Fahim Uddin reported that as of February 2015 all fresh water was supplied by water tankers @Rs.3500 per tanker. About 150 tankers per month were required to meet the water requirements which for the month of January 2015 only cost Rs.511,000/-  He said that with the approval of EC the Admin took the following initiatives:

 

o   Water tanker supplier was approached for carrying water from Staff Town to all Main Campus facilities. The Supplier’s response was to provide a tanker for 8 hours @Rs.180,000/- per month.  But it could not be done due to non-availability of a hydrant.

 

o   Next initiative was to have a new control valve at main line to connect Fauji Foundation Building – old water tank.  Technical Maintenance provided the same and General Maintenance connected old connection of all renovated buildings with supply lines.

 

2.2       The Registrar said Hydrant would have cost around Rs.450,000/- Now we have controlled water pipeline available and Mr. Fahim Uddin and Mr. Mushtaque completed it.

 2.3       Mr. Fahim Uddin reported that after having new water connection from main water line previously serving Boys Hostel only – about 50% fresh water requirement of Main Campus will be met using old water lines network serving following buildings:

 

-   Fauji Foundation Building

-   NBP Technology Centre

-   Mian Abdullah Library + Library Chiller Plant

-   Adamjee Academic Block

-   G&T Auditorium

-   Old Canteen

-   Adamjee & G&T Chiller plants

-   Old Prayer Hall

-   Main and Outer Lawns

-   Old staff bath rooms serving stores, transport and security staff.

 

2.4       The Registrar said that now water through tanker is provided to Aman-CED Building, Tabba Building and Students Centre only. He said that if connection is provided then there would be no need of water tanker at all. Mr. Mahmood Ali said that lines around these buildings have been provided but pressure testing has not been done.  Mr. Fahim said that if the Contractor gives connection they can provide water within a week.  The Dean & Director asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to ask the Venture Crest to expedite the work and complete the pressure testing.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.5       The Executive Committee recorded it appreciation for Mr. Fahim Uddin, Mr. Mushtaque, Mr. Mahmood Ali, and the General Maintenance Staff Mr. Habib Zada, Mr. Kamran, Mr. Aamir, Mr. Faisal Gulzar, Mr. Qamar Uddin and Mr. Azfar Abbasi who did an excellent job under the guidance of the Registrar.  The EC decided that they may be considered for Activity Based Reward.  Mr. Adnan Hameed to work on it.   

 

b)                 Completion of Automated Performance Appraisal System.

 

2.6       Mr. Imran Batada reported that they have completed automated Performance Appraisal System. He said that all modules of HRMS was given in July 2014 and this was missing which has now been completed.  He said that employees evaluation form will be available through the system. He said that it is based on organogram (reporting relationship). The Dean & Director said that if the organogram is not up-dated then the system will not work. The Registrar suggested that the Organogram can be sent to the concerned HoDs for verification. He suggested to give one week time to verify if the information (reporting relationship) is accurate or not.

 

2.7       Mr. Imran Batada demonstrated the functioning of the system.  The Dean & Director suggested to provide three years’ rating to Mr. Imran Batada for comparison purpose. To a question from Dr. Sayeed Ghani if the weightage of 80:20 can be changed, the Dean & Director clarified that category wise weightage for management staff, executives and faculty can be changed but for individual it cannot be changed. The Dean & Director suggested that there should be some provision in the overall rating (system calculated) for the comments of the HoDs after the comments of the supervisor in order to make it more transparent. Mr. Imran Batada said that he would add a “comment box” for the HoD.
