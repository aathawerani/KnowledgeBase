The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No.1       Review of Reward Nominations (under IBA’s Rules on Recognizing and Rewarding Employees at IBA).

 

1.1       Mr. Adnan Hameed presented the names of the nominees, justifications for the reward for each individual nominee, and level of reward recommended.

 

1.2       The Committee considered the recommendations for all the nominees except the nominations received from the Registrar’s Office as Capt. (Retd) Ahmed Zaheer who was to present the justifications for his nominees could not attend the meeting due to his another important commitment. However, the Dean & Director said that he would discuss with the Registrar and convey the decision to the EC through the minutes of this meeting.  

 

1.3       Mr. Anwar suggested to include another reward level of Rs.30,000/-, as the difference between level 3 & level 4 is double.  The Committee agreed to this suggestion and advised Head of HR to incorporate this in the rules and present it to A&FC and BOG for approval.

1.5       The Committee also decided that subject to the approval of the recommendation by the A&FC/BoG, the rewards and certificates would be given to the nominees at the Convocation or at any appropriate occasion. The Committee also decided that the families (excluding young children) of the recipients of cash awards / letter of appreciation would be invited in the award distribution ceremony.

 

1.6       The Committee also decided that department heads would be invited to the Convocation.

 

ACTION

 

1.7       The Head of H.R. would present the recommendation of the EC for the award/certificates and amendment in the rule to the A&FC for approval.

 

1.8       The Registrar Office would invite the families (excluding young children) of the recipients of cash awards / letter of appreciation in the award distribution ceremony.

 

1.9       The Director Projects would inform Mr. Mahmood Ali that he has been recommended for the award with the expectation that he would get the projects assigned to him completed on time.

 

1.10     The Registrar Office would invite department heads also to the Convocation.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
