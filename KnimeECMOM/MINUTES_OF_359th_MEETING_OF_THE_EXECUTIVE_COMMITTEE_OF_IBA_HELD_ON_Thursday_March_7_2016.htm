MINUTES OF 359TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON
MONDAY, MARCH 7, 2016

The 359th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, March 7, 2016 at 9:30 a.m. at Board Room, Fauji Foundation Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. There was no formal agenda for the meeting except to bid farewell to our Dean & Director, Dr. Ishrat Husain who on his joining the IBA started to hold EC Meeting every Monday. The first meeting of the Executive Committee was held on June 9, 2008 and the last meeting that was chaired by him was the 359th meeting.

2. The members paid rich tribute to Dr. Ishrat Husain for turning around IBA as well as providing the opportunity to them to learn a lot from his versatile knowledge and experience. In return the Dean & Director also thanked the members for their cooperation and support and working as a team.

3. With regard to the proposed policy framework for IBA non-teaching staff engagement in assignments / activities not directly related to their job, it was reported that the Grievance Committee would be considering the policy in their meeting on March 9 and then send their recommendations for consideration in the EC.

4. At this juncture the Executive Committee recorded its appreciation of the work done by the Grievance Committee which has so far submitted six reports. The Executive Committee asked Mr. Adnan Hameed to request the Grievance Committee to give their last report by March 20, 2016 after which the GC would stand dissolved.

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
