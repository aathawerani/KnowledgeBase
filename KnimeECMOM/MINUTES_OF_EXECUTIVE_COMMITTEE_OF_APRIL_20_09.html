MINUTES OF THE 44th MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON APRIL 20, 2009

The 44th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, April 20, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Associate Dean
  2. Mr. Ali Taha                                    Director Finance
  3. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                    Director Projects
  4. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                         Director I.T.
  5. Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                         Director CED
  6. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                        Director H.R.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider note from Director Projects regarding award of work to the lowest bidder for construction of pantry toilets and security check post at Main Campus.

1.1       The Director Projects stated that subsequent to the approval by the Executive Committee of the work of toilets and pantry at the ground floor of Administration block at Main Campus, the bids from pre-qualified contractors were invited for the construction of pantry toilets and security check post at Main Campus. He said that there was a need to improve the security kiosk too, as such, work items were added for the improvement of the existing kiosk. He presented the following list of six contractors who collected the bids:

1.      M/s The Strength

2.      M/s B.G Civil Electrical & Mechanical

3.      M/s Electro Systems

4.      M/s Link International

5.      M/s Jahangir Corporation

6.      M/s The Turn Key Group

1.2       The Director Projects stated that from the above six contractors following three contractors submitted their sealed bids on April 17, 2009.

1.      M/s The Strength

2.      M/s Link International

3.      M/s Electro Systems

1.3       He said that the bids were opened the same day in front of contractors representative and Tender Opening Committee which consist of following members.

1.      Engr. Abdul Rehman, Project Engineer (Planning & Contracts)

2.      Engr. Musharaf Hussain, Project Engineer (Execution & Control)

3.      Mr. Naved Sayeed, Manager Maintenance

4.      Mr. Shabbir Firdousi, Cashier (Finance Department)

1.4       The Director Projects presented the quoted price and standings of the contractors which are:

            Name__________                  Price Quoted              Standings

1.      M/s The Strength            Rs.273,051/-            1st Lowest

2.      M/s Link International      Rs.341,249/-               2nd Lowest

3.      M/s Electro Systems        Rs.390,346               Highest

1.5       The Director Projects stated that the price quoted by M/s The Strength is reasonable and the rates are workable which also include the improvement of the security kiosk. He recommended that the work may be awarded to the lowest bidder i.e. M/s. The Strength at a cost of Rs.273,051/-.

DECISION

1.6       The Committee approved the recommendation of the Director Projects for the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s. The Strength at a cost of Rs.273,051/-for construction of pantry toilets and security check post at Main Campus.

ACTION

1.7       The Director Projects would issue work order to the lowest bidder M/s. The Strength for construction of pantry toilets and security check post at Main Campus.

Item No. 2      To consider email from Sr. Manager MIS regarding Implementation Team for ERP.

2.1       The Director I.T. said that they need ERP Implementation Team which needs to be created within each department and they should own the system and go through the whole implementation process. He requested that each department should nominate two to three persons on the team. He said that the larger the department the larger the number of persons to be on the team. 

2.2              The Director Projects said that he finds it difficult to spare any person from his department as all of them are heavily loaded.  However, he agreed that the person who will be keeping accounting records of the Projects will be nominated for ERP Implementation Team. 

DECISION

2.3       The Committee decided that the Director I.T. would request all the department heads to nominate two to three employees from their respective departments on the ERP Implementation Team.

2.4       The Committee also decided that Program Coordinators would be part of the ERP Implementation Team. 

Item No. 3      To consider application from Mr. Zakir Husain, Bearer for medical bill reimbursement.

3.1       Mr. Zakir Husain, Bearer, submitted an application for reimbursement of medical bills for Rs.8,750/- on account of hospitalization of his daughter at Anwar Memorial Hospital, Kotli (Azad Kashmir).

3.2       The Director H.R. referring to the EC decision that such cases of medical reimbursement would be decided by the EC on case to case basis, requested the EC to consider the request from Mr. Zakir Husain.

DECISION

3.3       The Committee approved the request of Mr. Zakir Husain for reimbursement of medical bills. However, the Committee decided that the reimbursement will be made according to the Liaquat National Hospital rates. 

ACTION

3.4              The Director H.R. would process the medical bills submitted by Mr. Zakir Husain for reimbursement.

Item No. 4      To consider application from Mr. Mohammad Omair Azhar, student of MBA-II for semester break.

4.1       Mr. Mohammad Omair Azhar, a student of MBA-II (Direct) requested for a semester break for some personal circumstances.

DECISION

4.2       In view of the flexible policy to be implemented from the Fall 2009 on the choice of courses and the stated reasons being genuine, the Executive Committee approved the request of Mr. Mohammad Omair Azhar for a semester break.

ACTION

4.3              The Controller of Examination would convey the decision of Executive Committee to Mr. Mohammad Omair Azhar and to the concerned Program Office.

Item No. 5      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

            a)         Request for Message for IBA CD.

5.1              The Director I.T. said that they are preparing CD for the Program Announcement for which photographs and messages (write up of the jobs) from all directors are required.  He said that he has received photographs and messages from Dr. Sayeed Ghani and Mr. Mobin Khalili only.  The Director Projects said that he has also sent his message and photograph.

DECISION

5.2              Dr. Sayeed Ghani requested the other members on the Committee to send their photographs and messages to Dr. Arshad Siddiqi as soon as possible. 

5.3              The Committee requested Dr. Arshad Siddiqi to send soft copy of the Program Announcement to EC before the CD is ready.

b)                 Recommendation of sub-committee on H.R. Consultancy

5.4       The Director H.R. stated that as per the decision of the EC in its meeting held on 13-4-2009, a committee comprising Director Alumni, Director Finance, and Director HR was formed to open the Financial bid for HR consulting work. The meeting of the committee was held on 17-4-09. The Director PP&FM attended by special invitation.

5.5       The Director H.R. stated that based on the conditions laid down in the RFP, and the technical bid, the committee decided to open the financial bid of only Prolink as this was the only firm that exceeded the technical criteria. The other 2 short listed firms did not meet the minimum marks specified in the technical bid.

5.6       The Director H.R. reported that the amount of Rs.840,000/- quoted by M/s. Prolink was based on their initial estimate that 30 benchmarked positions would be assessed in modules 1 and 2. For these modules the rate quoted is per position, subject to the number of positions agreed upon finally.  She said that if the number of jobs increases, the amount would also increase.

5.7       She said that the Committee decided to place the order with M/s. Prolink and meet with them early next week. The meeting will be attended by the Director HR and the Director Alumni from IBA and Mr. Pervez Saeed ( Chief Executive), and Mr. Farrukh Nasim (Sr. Consultant), from M/s. Prolink.

5.8       It was suggested that the Consultant should tell us which positions are to be evaluated and which are not to be evaluated.

5.9              The Director Projects suggested that there should be a ball park amount for all the positions.

DECISION

5.10          The Committee approved the recommendation of the sub-committee to award the job to M/s. Prolink.

ACTION

5.11          The Director H.R. would place work order with M/s. Prolink.

c)                  SOP for up-loading news on the IBA web / Portal.

5.12          The Director I.T. presented web policy of IBA for consideration and approval by the Executive Committee.

DECISION

5.13          The Committee after certain modifications approved the web policy of IBA.  The modified policy is attached.

 There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
