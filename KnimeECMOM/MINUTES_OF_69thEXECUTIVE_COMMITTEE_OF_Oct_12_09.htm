MINUTES OF THE 69TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, OCTOBER 12, 2009  

The 69th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 12, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA, Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                         Associate Dean

2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                 Associate Dean

3. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer        PN Registrar

4. Mr. Ali Taha                                Director Finance

5. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz          Director Projects

7. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                  Director HR

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1     To consider note from Director ICT for approving CommTel System as the Single Source supplier of the Tandburg Video Conferencing Equipment for IBA.

 

1.1       The Director ICT stated in his note that the criteria for the Video Conferencing Equipment have been set forth keeping in view the layout of our lecture room for distant learning / e learning programs. He said that these guidelines have been developed internationally by visual communication experts and the same guidelines have been adopted in Pakistan by Higher Education Commission and being implemented at over 60 universities across Pakistan. He stated that the RFP was designed by H.E.C in consultation with experts from different universities internationally. Any deviation from the design document impacts the overall functionality of the distant e learning lecture rooms. Thus IBA wanting to implement a similar solution has followed the same guidelines to ensure that there is full interoperability with all universities across the globe and the best possible quality. He stated that this project would connect IBA with international universities. Further the vendor, who has turned out to be technically most compliant and financially lowest in IBA tender is the same vendor who has implemented video conferencing solutions for Higher Education Commission at more than 60 universities.

 

1.2       The Director ICT informed the Committee that two bidders namely M/s. CommTel System and M/s. Wateen Solution (Pvt.) Ltd., applied for the video conferencing tender. He said that M/s. Wateen Solution (Pvt.) Ltd was disqualified as they did not follow PPRA rules to submit earnest money check in financial bid. M/s. CommTel System qualified because their proposed solution not only technically qualified but also met PPRA rules for financial requirements. He further stated that qualified bidder’s proposed quoted solution met all technical standardization required for the video conferencing solution. He said that M/s. CommTel System is a sole partner of principal (Tandburg) for the proposed solution in Pakistan. CommTel System is also approved vendor by HEC for video conferencing solution. He further said that IBA’s existing video conferencing solution aided by HEC is also done by CommTel System and also support contract for video conferencing with the same vendor.

 

1.3       The Director ICT stated that M/s. CommTel System proposed Tandburg video conferencing solution and they (CommTel System) are the only partner in Pakistan and have all HEC aided video conferencing solution. Therefore, CommTel System is the only vendor which can meet the video conferencing solution and fulfil technical requirements. Tandburg video conferencing solution is considered one of the top video conferencing solutions.

 

1.4       The Director ICT requested the Executive Committee to approve M/s. CommTel System as the Single Source supplier of the Tandburg Video Conferencing Equipment for IBA.

 

DECISION

 

1.5              The Committee approved the request of the Director ICT of treating M/s. CommTel System as the single source supplier of the Tandburg Video Conferencing Equipment for IBA on the justification given by the Director ICT.

 

 

Item No. 2      To review and approve the revised Master Plan incorporating re-location of Centre for Entrepreneurship at Main Campus.

 

2.1       The Director Projects reported that a meeting was held on 3rdAugust 2009 with M/s. Icon, represented by Mr. Hanif Daud & Ms. Saman Mehmood on the re-location of Centre for Entrepreneurship from its present location to the rear of existing library and the Auditorium so that the open area in front of IBA existing building is saved.  Following were also present in the meeting:

 

1.      Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                   : Director ICT

2.   Mr. Shameem Ahmad                   : Director HEC Projects

3.   Mr. Aqeel Bilgrami                       : Architect M/s. Najmi Bilgrami

                                                              Collaborative (Pvt) Limited (NBCL)

4.   Mr. Zayd Bilgrami                        : Architect NBCL

5.   Mr. Shahab Ghani                         : Architect M/s. Shahab Ghani &

                                                               Associates (Consulting Architect IBA)

 

2.2       The Director Projects reported that all pros and cons of the re-location were discussed in detail and all agreed to re-locate the building and to rationalize the traffic pattern around the buildings and the connecting streets and the pathways. It was also suggested to create two parking bays for ease of parking and accesses to the buildings. M/s. Icon agreed to revise the Master Plan layout to incorporate the Centre for Entrepreneurship and promised that they would issue the revised layout plan within 3 days. M/s. NBCL had forwarded them the footprint of that building designed by them, on 4th August 2009. On 10th August 2009, M/s. Icon submitted the revised plan incorporating re-location of Centre for Entrepreneurial Development (CED) which was sent to the IBA Consulting Architect Mr. Shahab Ghani, and a copy was also sent to M/s. Najmi Bilgrami (NBCL) who are the designer for CED, for their comments.  He reported that on 12th August 2009, Mr. Shahab Ghani, furnished their comments and recommended that M/s. Icon should not use the footprint of those buildings designed by them which have not been awarded to them. He also recommended that open plot marking should be made for the future buildings.

 

2.3       The Director Projects stated that M/s. Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative (Pvt.) Limited (NBCL) submitted a plan with the demarcation of the re-location of CED Building and Academic Block for the reasons that they had already won the competition of both the buildings.

 

2.4       The Director Projects reported that in order to finalize the Master Plan, a meeting was held on 1st September 2009. In that meeting the proposal for re-location of CED Building was discussed in detail in the presence of the following:

 

1.         Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi             :           Director ICT

2.         Mr. Shameem Ahmad             :           Director HEC Projects

3.         Mr. Zafar Ahmed Siddiqui     :           Director CED

4.         Mr. Mobin Khalili                   :           Controller of Examinations

5.         Engr. Rehan A. Riaz               :           Director Projects

6.         Mr. Shahab Ghani                   :           Architect M/s. Shahab Ghani &

                                                                         Associates

7.         Mr. Aqeel Bilgrami                 :           Architect M/s. Najmi Bilgrami

                                                                         Collaborative (Pvt) Ltd (NBCL)

8.         Mr. Zayd Bilgrami                  :           Architect NBCL

9.         Mr. Hanif Daud                      :           Architect, ICON

 

2.5       The Director Projects informed the Committee that M/s. NBCL Architects,  M/s. Aqeel Bilgrami and Zayd Bilgrami,  who were invited to the meeting as desired by Dean & Director, IBA with the objective that the Master Plan should be finalized with their consent as they have won design of new Academic Block too. M/s. NBCL also proposed a location for CED Building.  He said that in that meeting re-location of CED was discussed threadbare with reference to M/s. ICON’s proposal and proposal by M/s. NBCL. The Director Projects reported that the following discussions ensued in that meeting:

 

·         Mr. Zafar Siddiqui, in reply to query regarding the necessity of placing CED near Academic Block, stated that initially the students at MBA level would be interested in courses pertaining to entrepreneurship. Ultimately two years down the road BBA and MBA degree courses will be held in CED Building. It is thus necessary that CED be located near New Academic Block and the library where presently proposed by NBCL. He did not agree to the location proposed by M/s. ICON.

 

·         Architects Shahab Ghani and Aqeel Bilgrami recommended that the Master Plan should show only plots for future buildings (such as Student Centre) and facilities instead of the suggested footprints.

 

 

·         Detailed discussions were held on the overall master planning of roads, walk-ways, and parking areas. The decision of 3rd August, 2009 meeting was endorsed by all that there should be parking lots at two ends of the campus (East & West) and proper road and pathway network be designed so that students and teachers do not have to walk too long under the sun .

 

·         M/s. ICON promised to submit the revised plan within a week based on the above mentioned principles.

 

2.6       The Director Projects presented the revised master plan Option-I and Option-II submitted by M/s. ICON as well as the Master Plan submitted by M/s. NBCL.

 

2.7              The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to review all the three options in the light of the principles given to M/s. Icon.

 

2.8              The Registrar wanted that the time of completion (month and year) of each component of the project should be mentioned.

 

2.9              Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that M/s. NBCL have submitted conceptual proposal and it is not according to scale.

 

 

DECISION

 

2.10          The Committee reviewed all the three options and approved the proposal for relocation of the Centre for Entrepreneurship at Main Campus suggested by NBCL. However, the Committee wanted that a drawing on scale with completion date of each project should be presented to the Executive Committee.

 

ACTION

 

2.11     The Director Projects would request M/s. NBCL to prepare a drawing on scale with completion dates of the projects assigned to them and present it before the Executive Committee.
