MINUTES OF THE 47th MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MAY 11, 2009

The 47th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, May 11, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                            Associate Dean
  2. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar
  3. Mr. Ali Taha                                  Director Finance
  4. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                  Director Projects
  5. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                       Director I.T.
  6. Mr. Zafar S. Siddiqui                       Director CED
  7. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                       Director H.R.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      Selection of Consulting Architect for Multistoried Building Complex at IBA.

1.1       The Director Projects presented table and evaluation report regarding selection of Consulting Architect for the Multistoried Building Complex at IBA. He informed that in the previous meeting of Executive Committee, it was decided that three architects viz:

·         M/s. Habib Fida Ali

·         M/s. Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative (Pvt) Limited

·         M/s. F.M.N.H.

would be issued the terms of reference for  submission of their technical and financial proposals to provide consulting services to IBA as spelled out in the letters inviting proposals. Out of the above three Architects, M/s. Habib Fida Ali regretted to participate. The Technical and Financial proposals were thus received from M/s. NBCL and M/s. F.M.N. H.

1.2       The Director Projects further informed that technical evaluation of the proposals was done against evaluation criteria based on their manpower strength, expertise, associates, work approach methodology, working plan and the presentation of the proposal. Total marks assigned were 100.  The marks secured by M/s. NBCL & M/s. F.M.N.H. were 90% and 80% respectively.

1.3       The Director projects requested the Executive Committee to open the financial proposals of both the Architects as their ranking was close to each other. He also suggested that the final evaluation be done on the basis of weightage of technical and financial evaluation to be 60% and 40% respectively. He also suggested that the baseline estimate for carrying out the financial evaluation has been assigned as Rs.350,000/-

1.4       The financial bids were then opened, the results were as follows:

·         M/s. NBCL                       :           5.0    Million

·         M/s. F.M.N.H.                  :           1.6    Million

1.5       The Executive Committee discussed the financial bids in reference to the baseline suggested by Director Projects and found that there was vast variation between the baseline and the financial bids. The Executive Committee suggested that the baseline may be reviewed by the Director Projects and negotiation be carried out with the lowest bidder to reduce the amount of fee.

Decision

1.6       The Committee decided that the Director Projects will review the baseline and present it before the Executive Committee in its next meeting.

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
