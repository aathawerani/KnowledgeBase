MINUTES OF 291ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD

ON MONDAY, APRIL 7, 2014

 

The 291st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, April 7, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Conference Room, Ground Floor, Tabba Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                                  Dean & Director

2.      Dr. Mohammad Nishat                                           Associate Dean, FBA

3.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                                 Associate Dean, FCS

4.      Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN                                  Registrar

5.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                  Director Finance

6.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                                     Director Projects

7.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                 Head of ICT Department

8.      Mr. Adnan Hameed                                               Head of HR

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No.1       Demonstration of Smart Board.

 

1.1       Mr. Imran Batada said that last time when the demonstration of Smart Board was given to the EC, the members had desired that the smart board should have the capabilities of text conversion also which was lacking in the previous one. He said that the system he is demonstrating has many options including text conversion capability and it can work with the single touch.  He said that even the font size can also be selected and it can be connected to the laptop and internet. However, he said that in the previous system any hard board could work but for this, smart board has to be purchased along with software. It altogether (hardware and software) costs around $250,000 whereas the previous one would cost $100,000/- He said that the system is very easy to operate and many educational institutions are using this system. He presented a list of the organizations where this system is being used and the number of units they have acquired.

 

1.2       The Dean & Director asked as to what is their response time for fixing any problem or malfunction in case anything goes wrong. Mr. Imran Batada replied that the response time can be within 24 hours. He further said that he would get it incorporated in the agreement if approved. He said that the product is manufactured by Smart Tech of Canada and in Pakistan it is marketed by Commerce Aid.  The Company offers five years warranty. He clarified that it is a single source supplied item.

 

1.3       The Dean & Director said that the whole purpose of acquiring smart board is to increase the interaction between student and teacher in the classroom. He said that students spend their time in taking notes and cannot use their brain for grasping the material, seeking clarifications and raising questions. Under this system they can concentrate in class room as they can review the soft notes later. Thus the absorptive capacity of the students would increase.  He asked as to how many sets are required. The Registrar said that there are forty classrooms at both the campuses. Mr. Imran Batada suggested to procure five sets. He said that if EC approves, he can get two for the Summer from the existing budget. The Dean & Director said that initially we can procure 5, then 20 and then 15.  The Dean & Director asked Mr. Imran Batada to visit NED and other universities where this system is working and ask what their experiences are.

 

1.4       Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know as to how soon the lectures can be transmitted to the students. Mr. Batada said that the lectures can be sent through email. The Dean & Director suggested to interface it with LMS. He said that it would also solve photocopying and toner issue. 

  

DECISION

 

1.5       The Committee approved the procurement of five sets of the Smart Tech Board from the existing budget and remaining 35 in phases from next year’s budget. 

 

1.6       The Committee also approved the procurement of the Smart Tech Board from Commerce Aids & Equipment (Pvt.) Ltd. (the sole distributor of Smart Tech in Pakistan). However, before procurement of the sets Mr. Imran Batada would get feedback from the users at NED and other universities.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Library of projects related documents.

 

2.1       The Dean & Director asked the Director Projects to prepare a library in the MP Building related to the documentation of project of every building and facilities from the initiation to the completion.  He suggested to start from the HEC Projects and complete it before June 2014.  All HEC Project files should be arranged and organized and a list of all the documents be provided to the Registrar. These should be bound hard copies and also digitized versions of these documents for future reference by the Maintenance Staff.  The Library when complete should be handed over to the Registrar. The Director Projects said that he would prepare the library of the projects and would also prepare CDs and handover to the Registrar. 

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
