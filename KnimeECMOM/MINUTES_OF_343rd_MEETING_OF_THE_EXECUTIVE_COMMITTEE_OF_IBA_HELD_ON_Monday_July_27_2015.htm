The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1      Approval for award of work for supply and fixing of furniture at IBA Boys Hostel.

 

1.1     Mr. Abdul Rehman reported that advertisement was floated in newspapers for supply and fixing of furniture at New Boys Hostel at IBA on May 30, 2015. The Bids were invited on the basis of single stage two envelop procedure. Following contractors collected the Tender document:

 

       1.    M/s Husnain

       2.    M/s The Systems

       3.    M/s Gujrat

       4.    M/s Honestly

       5.    M/s Mustansir Industries

       6.    M/s Sheetal

       7.    M/s S. Ziaul Haq

       8.    M/s S.F Incorporated

       9.    M/s Simab

 

1.2     Mr. Abdul Rehman reported that pre-bid meeting was held on June 17, 2015 on contactors request. The bid submission date was fixed as June 30, 2015. Following five contractors submitted their bids. Technical bids were opened in front of IBA officials and contractor representatives:

 

       1.     M/s Husnain Brothers

       2.     M/s S.F incorporated

       3.     M/s Simab

       4.     M/s Sheetal

       5.     M/s Gujrat Steel

 

       The bid of M/s Gujarat Steel was rejected due to the following reasons:

 

a.    They enclosed BOQ along with Technical proposal which was not as per the rule of SPPRA.
 b.    They reshaped the IBA original documents.

1.3     Based on above two reasons their bid was rejected and their financial proposal returned to them. The technical proposals of the following four were scrutinized technically as per the given evaluation criteria:

 

       1.       M/s Husnain Brothers

       2.       M/s S.F Incorporated

       3.       M/s Simab

       4.       M/s Sheetal

 

1.4     Only M/s Sheetal and M/s Simab were the two bidders among four to achieve the qualification marks.  M/s Husnain Brothers and S.F Incorporated could not achieve the required marks for qualification. The Case was presented in Technical Evaluation Committee (Projects) which recommended the opening of financial proposals of above two qualified bidders M/s Simab and M/s Sheetal.

 

1.5     The case was then presented to Procurement Committee which allowed to open the financial bids. The financial bids of the following contractors were opened on July 10, 2015 in the presence of IBA officials and contractor representatives. Bids were checked arithmetically and found correct. As per the amount the standing of the contractors is as follows:

 

1.    M/s Sheetal                     Rs.14,564,277.00       1st Lowest
 2.    M/s Simab                       Rs.15,432,919.00       Highest

 

1.6     Mr. Abdul Rehman informed that the case was presented in Procurement Committee on July 11, 2015. The Procurement Committee discussed the case and flowing were the discussions and decisions.

 

          “ Discussion: Mr. Abdul Rehman suggested that M/s Sheetal have been seriously lacking in providing the services/products to IBA on the two projects that they have won, giving them an additional project would seriously affect existing projects as well as the project under consideration.

 

          The Committee discussed the comments made by Mr. Abdul Rehman, but suggested that SPPRA rules don’t permit us to go to the 2nd lowest bidder after technically accepting the bid of lowest bidder. We have to award the project to lowest evaluated bidder.

 

            Decision: The Procurement Committee recommended to award the ‘Supply and Fixing of Furniture at IBA Boys Hostel’ to M/s Sheetal Interiors at Rs.14,564,277.”

 

1.7     Based on the recommendation of Procurement Committee Mr. Abdul Rehman requested that Executive Committee to approve the award of work to M/s Sheetal Interior at a cost of Rs.14,564,277/-

 

1.8     The Dean & Director asked as to who has done the technical evaluation and given marks. Mr. Abdul Rehman replied that he had done it. The Dean & Director suggested that in future the technical evaluation and its marking should be done by a committee of three members instead of one person.  He further suggested that our previous experience with the contractor, if any, should also be included in technical evaluation. He said that we have to follow the SPPRA rules.

 

DECISION

 

1.9     The Committee endorsed the recommendation of the Procurement Committee to award the work for supply and fixing of IBA Boys Hostel to M/s. Sheetal Interior at a cost of Rs.14,564,277/-

 

ACTION

 

1.10    Mr. Abdul Rehman would present the recommendation to the A&FC for consideration and approval.

 

 

Item No.2      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

            a)        To consider the draft MOU for establishment of IBA Campus at DHA City.

 

2.1     Mr. Moeid Sultan reported that a draft MoU has been sent by Pakistan Defence Officers Housing Authority, Karachi, for the establishment of IBA Campus at DHA City. DHA has offered to allot 10 acres of land in the Health Care District of DCK @Rs.0.5 million per acre (subsidized rate) plus External Development charges @ Rs.1.5 million.

 

2.2     The Dean & Director said that we may use this land for faculty residence and hostel.  It could also be used for community college there.

 

2.3     Mr. Moeid Sultan briefed about the changes that have been suggested by IBA in the draft MoU.

 

DECISION

 

2.4     The Dean & Director asked Mr. Moeid Sultan to present the MoU with a comparison of what was originally proposed in the MoU and what changes have been suggested for consideration and approval of A&FC and BoG.

         

          There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
