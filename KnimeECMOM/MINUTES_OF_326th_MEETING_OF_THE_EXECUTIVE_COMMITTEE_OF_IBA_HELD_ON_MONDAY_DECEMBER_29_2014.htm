The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Status Reports from:

 

Registrar:

 

1.1       Preparation of list of distinguished alumni: The Registrar reported that the matter was under perusal of Mr. Shahid Shafiq. Meanwhile portraits belonging to 49 distinguished alumni have been put up on display in the Alumni Student Centre.  He said that he would follow this up with Mr. Shahid Shafiq.

 

1.2       The Registrar would talk to the tanker owner if they can fill their tanker with IBA’s overhead tank and supply water to IBA at a comparatively lesser rate: The Registrar said that he could not talk to the tanker owner. However, he said that he would examine the possibility of getting empty tanker.

 

1.3       The Registrar to seek extension for retaining the APWA Building up to January 31, 2015:  The Registrar reported that APWA Auditorium has been vacated and cleared of all IBA belongings.  Letter for extension of rent period of APWA Library was written and APWA agreed to allow IBA to use the same at existing rates until 15th February 2015.

 

Head of ICT:

 

1.4       The Head of ICT would make a presentation of the Business Intelligence System in the Program Directors and Chairpersons’ meeting and then in the faculty meeting: Mr. Imran Batada reported that they were still working on the changes suggested by EC and that they would finalize and start circulating the application by next month.

 

Director Projects:

 

1.5       Shed for the motorcycle stand: The Director Projects reported that the requirements were communicated to the Architects and the design from them was awaited. However he suggested that instead of getting its design prepared, they should select readymade shed made of fibre glass with steel pipe.

 

1.6       The Director Projects to instruct the contractor to start and complete the work for road from Students Centre, to Aman-CED and NBP Technology Centre on priority basis: The Director Projects said that the road section as per master plan is 12’- 0” whereas the space available including the planters is 29.0”. He said that they have proposed the Architects to increase the road width to at least 18’-0” because of the heavy traffic. He said that they were waiting for the Architect to respond.

 

The Dean & Director said that there was no need to increase the width of the road as most of the people would walk through the footpath.  The width of the road should remain as given in the master plan i.e. 12’. 

 

1.7       The Director Projects to talk to Mr. Jeffrey and get the designing issue of Centre of Excellence in Journalism resolved and give go ahead to the Contractor: The Director Projects reported that Conference call was held. Jeffery Prah promised to send the drawings by 22nd December but they have not been received as yet.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.8       The Committee noted the up-date given above.

 

1.9       The Director Projects would get the road from Students Centre to Aman-CED and NBP Technology Centre completed according to the Master Plan.

 

1.10     The Registrar would select the design of motorcycle shed and present its details including cost to the EC.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

2.1       The following decisions were taken:

 

Lecture Recording:  The Dean & Director requested the Associate Deans that four faculty members from FBA and one faculty member from FCS should be requested to use lecture recording system and get their recorded lectures up-loaded beginning Spring Semester.

 

Monitoring and Tracking of TA’s assignments:  The Dean & Director said that there were 121 TAs in both faculties; 51 of them were for the visiting faculty but he has no idea as to how they were being utilized.  He said that the system has to be revamped and to be made part of the HRMS in which every teacher who is provided with TA will feed the data about the utilization of his / her TA.  He said that there has to be a validation process.

 

The Dean & Director suggested the two Associate Deans and the Head of H.R. to develop a monitoring and tracking system of the TAs and the Chairpersons should also be involved into it.  He said that some faculty members have got two TAs. He said that a teacher should not get more than one T.A.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani clarified that if any teacher has got two TAs, the maximum 20 hours are divided and accordingly payment is divided between the two TAs.

 

The Dean & Director asked the Head of H.R. to manage the database of TAs and prepare a standard format covering the duty, and accomplishment of TAs. He said that teachers who have been given TAs be required to give a report mentioning how many tutorials and counselling were given by his / her TA.  The payment to the TA should be linked with the report.    

 

Projects Library: The Director Projects reported that he has assigned the job of          preparing Projects Library to Mr. Muhammad Tariq, Quantity Surveyor.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
