MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON JUNE 30, 2008 (THIRD MEETING)

The third meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, June 30, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                          Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Chairman, MIS & CS
  4. Mr. Inayat N. Din                                Registrar
  5. Mr. Mobin Khalili                                 Controller of Examinations
  6. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                         H.R. Manager

Mr. Moeid Sultan, Acting Head of Finance & Accounts could not attend the meeting due to illness.

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Manager Projects attended the meeting by special invitation.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      Revised Training Proposal for Phase I of ERP – Campus Management System.

1.1       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that the training for Phase I of ERP as approved in the Executive Committee on June 23, 2008, was planned for 11 days with full day training schedule. However, on the basis of feedback from the Controller of Examinations that the program officers involved would not be able to spend full days on training due to their ongoing commitments with regards to Fall semester, the training will be conducted in half day training sessions for 23 days. As a result of increase in the number of days, the training cost would be revised from Rs.275,000/- to Rs.400,000/- which would be credited back to IBA in case IBA chooses to award implementation of PeopleSoft Campus Solutions to Techlogix.

1.2              Dr. Ghani said that the budget estimate of the cost of Lunch and Tea would be reduced from Rs.100,000/- to Rs.30,000/- as lunch will not be served on a regular basis.

DECISION:

1.3       The Committee approved the Revised Training Proposal for Phase 1 of ERP – Campus Management System along with revised training cost.

Item No. 2     Guest Speakers Sessions

2.1       The Committee felt that the IBA should revive inviting guest speakers in the classes as it used to in the past.

DECISION:

2.2       The Committee decided that each class should conduct at least four guest speaker sessions in a regular semester.

2.3       Each faculty should have an approved list of guest speakers.

2.4       The guest speaker sessions would be in lieu of classes and would be counted towards total sessions.

ACTION

2.5      The Registrar and Secretary, Executive Committee will intimate concerned department chairs to ensure that each faculty member in respective departments arranges at least four guest speaker sessions in a regular semester.

Item No. 3      Degree Programs in Banking, Financial Services, Insurance.

3.1    The Director wanted to know as to why the MBA Banking and MBA (MFS) programs were discontinued and felt that the IBA should re-start these programs. He suggested that the Academic Committee should consider starting these programs. He further said that Mr. Saleem Umar, former head of IBP is available and could be hired on the basis of his experience in running similar courses as a consultant to design these courses.  He also said that an Advisory Committee comprising Mr. Shaukat Tarin, Mr. Zubair Soomro, Mr. Badar Kazmi and a person from insurance company, should be formed for such programs.

ACTION:

3.2       The Academic Committee would prepare a proposal for such programs.

Item No. 4      Young faculty to be attached with senior faculty.

4.1      The Director proposed that the Academic Committee should consider making it mandatory for young faculty to be attached with senior faculty members for one or two semesters before giving them independent course/courses.

Item No. 5      Empowerment of Department Chairpersons

5.1       The Committee felt that department chairpersons need to be empowered. The input from Department Chairpersons should be given weightage in faculty evaluation for annual increment, promotion etc. of faculty members.

DECISION

5.2       As an initial step the Committee empowered Department Chairpersons to grant leave and approve participation in conferences and seminars if they are justified.  Other delegation of powers would be proposed in the context of the overall delegation of administrative, financial powers under the proposed rules being worked out by the Executive Committee. 

ACTION

5.3       The Registrar and Secretary, Executive Committee will issue notification to this effect.

Item No. 6      Note from Chairman MIS & CS for Rehiring of Services of Mr. Zahid Iqbal on contract.

6.1      Dr. Sayeed Ghani informed the Committee that Mr. Zahid Iqbal, Admin. Officer, CCS was retiring from IBA w.e.f. August 1, 2008. He said that Mr. Zahid Iqbal has been one of the honest, dedicated and competent workers of the IBA.  He suggested that Mr. Zahid Iqbal may be considered for rehiring for a suitable position because of his 25 years of valuable experience.

DECISION

6.2     The Director suggested that instead of rehiring the retired employees, we should appoint and train young persons. Another option he suggested was that the IBA should engage senior MBA students for specific tasks.  This will on the one hand supplement needy students’ income and on the other provide training to them.

Item No. 7      Appointment of Grading Assistant

7.1      The Committee decided that for a group of faculty there would a Grading Assistant to be appointed from amongst the senior students. However, it would not be disclosed as to who is grading whose paper.

Item No. 8      Reporting Item

Dr. Sayeed Ghani reported that he has deputed Mr. Imran Khan, faculty member and a Ph.D student for the University Alliance Program. He said that Mr. Khan would submit his concept paper by the end of July 2008.

The Committee noted and approved the deputation of Mr. Imran Khan for University Alliance Program.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
