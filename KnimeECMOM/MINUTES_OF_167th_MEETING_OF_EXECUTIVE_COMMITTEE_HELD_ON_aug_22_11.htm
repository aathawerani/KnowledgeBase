MINUTES OF 167TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY,

AUGUST 22, 2011

 

The 167th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, August 22, 2011 at 9:00 a.m. at Societies’ Meeting Room, Academic Block, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                   Dean & Director
2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                            Associate Dean, BA
3.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                  Associate Dean, FCS
4.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN               Registrar
5.      Mr. Mobin Khalili                                    Controller of Examinations
6.      Mr. Ali Taha                                         Director Finance
7.      Mr. Zafar Siddiqui                                  Director CED
8.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                              Director H.R.

 

Dr. Zeenat Ismail, Member Grievance Committee, attended the meeting on special invitation. Dr. Shahid Mir, member Grievance Committee, could not attend the meeting.

The following agenda items were discussed:      

Item No. 1      To consider Grievance Committee Report.

1.1       Mr. Mobin Khalili presented the highlights of the Grievance Committee Report submitted by the Committee on June 10, 2011. The report consisted of eight parts.                     

1.2       The Dean & Director thanked the Chairman and members Grievance Committee for in-depth examination of the issues and the recommendations and requested EC members to give their comments / suggestions.

1.3       The Committee thoroughly discussed the report and the recommendations.

 DECISION

1.4       The Committee recorded its appreciation and thanks for the Grievance Committee for putting in extensive efforts and submitting a detailed finding, analysis and recommendations to the EC.

1.5       The Committee decided that certain recommendations which were approved by the EC will be intimated to the employees through a Circular.  Other recommendations for which the EC does not have the power to approve at its own, would go to the A&FC and the BoG for consideration.

1.6       The Committee formed a Technical Committee comprising (i) the Registrar, (ii) the Director Finance, (iii) the Director H.R. and (iv) the G.M. Finance to evaluate financial implications of certain recommendations in light of the comments / suggestions given by the EC members and present them to the EC as soon as possible for further consideration/recommendation to the A&FC/BoG. Mr. Adnan Hameed will provide assistance to the Committee.

      

Item No.2       To consider and approve variation order of Wateen Optical Fiber Project.

2.1       As the Director ICT could not attend the meeting, this item was deferred.  

  

Item No. 3      Progress Report on Data Centre Projects.

3.1       As the Director ICT could not attend the meeting, this item was deferred.            

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
