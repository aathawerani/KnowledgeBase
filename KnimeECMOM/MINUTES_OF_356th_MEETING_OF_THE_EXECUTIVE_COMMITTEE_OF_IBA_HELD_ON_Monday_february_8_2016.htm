Item No.5       Presentation on “Hacking at IBA – Findings and Corrective Measures”.

 

5.1       Mr. Imran Batada presented a detailed report on perceptions of some IBA students on IT Security and his detailed reply to those perceptions/apprehensions on IT Security at IBA along with the remedial actions taken. 

 

5.2       To a point related to access to IBA CCTV camera systems, Mr. Khalid Javed Rishi informed the members of the additional safeguard measures they have taken through which access to IBA CCTV camera systems cannot be possible.  The Dean & Director said that few years back we had invited a Consultant from Islamabad to look into the system and point out its weaknesses.  He suggested to invite an outside expert to audit our IT system including, CCTV, Data Centre, Servers etc. with regard to hacking.

 

5.3       On the observation that IBA Faculty is not trained in IT security practices the Dean & Director requested Dr. Sayeed Ghani to ask Dr. Irfan Nabi if he could arrange a workshop for faculty on IT Security.

 

5.4       To an observation that there is just one password for the entire IBA wifi network, and unknown USBs can be freely used on IBA computers, Mr. Imran Batada said that after the implementation of ACS Server the Wi-Fi user authentication issue would be resolved and they may also block the USB access. However, he said that although unknown USB is blocked at corporate sectors, it is not done at educational institutions because faculty / students may face the issue such as presentation / data copy. He said that the solution is to log in through the domain. 

 

5.5       The Dean & Director said that some faculty members are not satisfied with ERP system that is why they are not using it. He asked Mr. Imran Batada to address ERP attendance issue also.   

 

5.6       Mr. Imran Batada said that some PCs at CED are netted and bypassed from proxy server which may cause some problem. He suggested that, as agreed in principle by Dr. Shahid Qureshi, we should charge the CED for the net and should provide separate internet to them, independent of IBA infrastructure. Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that Dr. Sajjad Haider has suggested that CED students should not be allowed to use IBA’s IP telephones. Mr. Imran Batada said that it has been agreed that we would phase out the IP telephone also from the CED and they would have their own device and IP phones.

 

5.7       The members were informed about the complaints made by two faculty members with regard to alleged hacking attempt made by two students of IBA. The Committee requested the Registrar to refer such cases to the Discipline Committee.   

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

5.8       The Committee noted with satisfaction the safeguard measures taken by the IT and Security Teams.

 

5.9       The Head of ICT would get the IT systems audited through an external expert.

 

5.10     Dr. Sayeed Ghani would discuss with Dr. Irfan Nabi about conducting a workshop for IBA faculty on IT Security.

 

Item No.6       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)         Proposal for construction of boundary walls & Security fence at Main and City Campuses.

 

6.1       Mr. Khalid Javed Rishi said that in view of the recent attack on Bacha Khan University, he along with Mr. Moeid Sultan in order to augment the security discussed the possibility of construction of boundary walls with Security Fence at both the Campus and the availability of resources.  He said that he has surveyed the existing structure in both campuses and prepared a BOQ for the project. Summary of the project is as under:-

 

-   Scope of work: In Main Campus, demolition of damaged portions of boundary wall & construction of 10 feet high boundary wall based on RCC plinth 2 feet, block masonry 6 feet. The existing 2 feet plinth will be used beneath the new construction and will be plastered. RCC columns will be erected at a distance of 10 feet to support the new plinth.

 

-   Erection of 4 feet high fence above the boundary wall.  Area required to be covered is 2900 ft.

-   Total obstacle value will be 14 feet from the ground level.

-   Construction of two watch towers of 20 feet high at Main Campus at tactical location.   

-   At City Campus, erection of 4 feet high security fence all around the campus.  Area required to be covered is 670 ft.

-   The estimated cost of the project is Rs.108,59,050/- excluding taxes.

 

6.2       Mr. Moeid Sultan said that considering the risk on life and properly the budget may not be an issue; it can be re-appropriated from other heads. He said that they have got Rs.10 million from HEC which can be utilized on this head.

 

6.3       The Dean & Director observed that the barbed wire inside concertina fence aesthetically would not look good specially at City Campus. Mr. Khalid Rishi said that concertina can be removed; simply barbed wire can be place. The Registrar suggested that concertina should be placed at both the Campuses.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani asked if it can be electrified. Mr. Khalid Rishi said that it can be done but he has not worked on it.

 

6.4       When asked as to how long time it would take to complete the project, Mr. Khalid Rishi said that the project would be completed in six months’ time. The Dean & Director said that six months’ time is too much; it should be completed on an emergency basis.  Mr. Khalid Rishi said that it could be completed much earlier if pre-fabricated walls are used which would cost the same but its life would not be more than 4 to 5 years. Moreover, barbed wires cannot be placed on such walls. Mr. Moeid Sultan said that there is a provision of direct contracting under emergency situations which has to be justified by the CPC and approved by the Dean & Director.

 

6.5       Mr. Khalid Rishi suggested that, if the proposal is accepted, the Projects Department should be given the BoQ and assigned this project.  He said that he can provide his assistance as and when needed by the Projects Department.  The Dean & Director contacted Mr. Mahmood Ali, Senior Manager Projects to know if the work can be assigned to pre-qualified petty contractors.  Mr. Mahmood Ali replied that because of large quantum of work, it cannot be given to the petty contractors.  The Dean & Director asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to check the validity of pre-qualified main contractors and suggested to give the BoQ to Syed Nasim Mumtaz, Senior Quantity Surveyor to check it.  He asked Mr. Khalid Rishi to coordinate with Mr. Mahmood Ali and give the tender documents to the prequalified contractors if still valid.   

 

6.6       The members also discussed the internal security arrangements in detail. One of the suggestions was that the respective building incharge should be more vigilant and extra cautious. Mr. Khalid Rishi was advised to remain in touch with Karachi University and extend all sorts of cooperation pertaining to security at the Campus. Mr. Khalid Rishi said that they have been extending full cooperation to Karachi University.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

6.7       The Committee approved the proposal for construction of boundary walls & Security fence at both the campuses of IBA as proposed by Mr. Khalid Javed Rishi.

 

6.8       Mr. Mahmood Ali would get the projects completed on emergency basis by following the laid down procedures.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
