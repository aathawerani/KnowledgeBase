MINUTES OF 272nd MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON

MONDAY, NOVEMBER 11, 2013

 

The 272nd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, November 11, 2013 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                                              Dean & Director

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                                             Associate Dean, FCS

3.      Dr. Mohammad Nishat                                                       Associate Dean, FBA

4.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                                          Registrar

5.      Mr. Mobin Khalili                                                               Controller of Examinations

6.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                                                 Director Projects

7.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                              Director Finance

8.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                             Acting Head of ICT

9.      Mr. Adnan Hameed                                                           Manager H.R.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No. 1      Status Reports from:

 

Registrar: 

 

1.1       Surveillance Cameras for Students’ Centre, Library, and Admin. Building: The Registrar reported that the tender has been deferred as the case has been referred to the Technical Evaluation Committee (TEC-ICT). However, he reported that in the meanwhile the Security Manager has purchased two cameras.

 

1.2       Request to be made for making Aman Foundation Ambulance available at Main Campus: The Registrar reported that he had approached Mr. Abid Naveed, the Head of Ambulance Unit of Aman Foundation who said that they would work out the feasibility and let us know.  However, the Registrar said that the chances are quite low as they require the positioning of vehicles at a place of public convenience.  The Dean & Director said that there are about 25000 students in Karachi University also who may benefit from this service. The Registrar said that Aman Foundation will depute their ambulance outside the premises and can be available, if needed, in a few seconds.

 

Projects Department:      

 

1.3       Designing and preparation of tender for the faculty apartments: Mr. Shameem Ahmed requested the EC to postpone this matter as currently he was focusing on completion of the projects in hand which are expected to be completed by December 31, 2013.

 

About the status of new Boys’ Hostel, Block ‘B’ & ‘C’, Mr. Shameem Ahmed said that there are some minor changes in the design for which the Consultants have to make several drawings.  The Dean & Director said that if there is no complaint then why are we going to make changes in the inner configuration. The Registrar said that his main concern is the stoppage of rainy water coming into the hall of the existing Boys’ Hostel Block ‘A’ through the stairs.  The Dean & Director asked Mr. Shameem Ahmed to get it fixed.  The Dean & Director said that we have to replicate the earlier design in Blocks ‘B’ & ‘C’. He said that except the control of rainy water, there would be no change in the design. He said that he would not make any payment for the additional drawings.  He asked Mr. Shameem Ahmed to arrange his meeting with the Registrar, the Director Projects, Mr. Jami Moiz, and the NBCL and finalize the minor changes. 

 

1.4       Requirements of facilities to be provided to the Transport Department and its employees – The Director Projects to work out the financial implications and resubmit the proposal along with its financial impact: The Director Projects reported that the cost estimate for the facilities (details presented in the 267th meeting) as prepared by M/s. NBCL is Rs.4 million.  He said that the total area which was 1300 sft will now be 2000 sft including the ramp area. The per sft cost is Rs.2000/-  He said that if the EC approves it, he would send it to the designer.  The Committee noted and approved the requirements of facilities along with cost estimate of Rs.4 million.

 

1.5       Status of existing canteen: The Director Projects said that it was decided that the existing canteen would not be demolished.  Its facade needs to be up-graded.  The cost estimate for the improvement of facade as submitted by the NBCL is Rs.4 million.  He said that as the building is next to existing Academic Block, they have asked ICON also to submit their proposal.

 

The Dean & Director said that there is no need to spend Rs.4 million on the improvement of the facade of the existing canteen. Only colouring / painting can be done. He said that it is a small canteen for the staff. He asked the Director Projects to get the colour and paint work done internally.  

 

1.6       The Director Projects to arrange a meeting of Engr. Khalid Pervez of KP Associates with the Dean & Director: The Director Projects reported that a meeting of Engr. Khalid Pervez was held with the Dean & Director on October 3, 2013. He stated that Mr. Khalid Pervez has submitted his revised offer based on reduced fee, which is being sent to the CPC for their review and approval.

 

1.7       Mr. Abdul Rahman to provide profile of M/s. Sheetal to the Registrar: The Director Projects reported that the profile was provided to the Registrar who has approved the same.

 

Acting Head of ICT:

 

1.8       Mr. Imran Batada to develop a system of keeping track of deadline, calculation of fine and intimation to finance department: Mr. Imran Batada informed the EC that the report has been developed in the ERP System.  He said that the Finance & Examination departments will be given the right of access through which they would be able to track the name of the faculty member(s) who have not posted the results after 15 days of the final exams of Fall 2013.  

 

Mr. Mobin Khalili wanted to know if concerned faculty can be given an alert message through this system.  Mr. Batada said that in the existing system it has not been provided but through email it can be done.

 

1.9       Mr. Imran Batada to develop a system through which each Teaching Assistant could be given the task and its monitoring can be done: Mr. Imran Batada reported that they are doing customization in the ERP System, so the faculty members can add the task list of TA along with hours. This will be implemented during this month. The rights will be given to HR & Finance Dept., so that they can review the progress of TA as well.

 

The Dean & Director said that he wants to match task with the outcome.  He asked Mr. Batada to do a pilot with a Teaching Assistant and then apply it.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
