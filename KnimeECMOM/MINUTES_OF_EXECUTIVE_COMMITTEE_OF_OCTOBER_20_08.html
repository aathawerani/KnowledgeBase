MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON OCTOBER 20, 2008 (EIGHTEENTH MEETING)

The eighteenth meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, October 20, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                              Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                        Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                              Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Inayat N. Din                              Registrar
  5. Mr. Ali Taha                                     Director Finance
  6. Mr. Shameem Ahmad                         General Manager, Planning  & Development
  7. Mr. Shamsuzzoha Jafri                       H.R. Manager

Mirza Sardar Husain, Chairman, Department of Management, and Ms. Ayesha Menai, the would be Director HR of IBA attended the meeting by special invitation.

Before the start of the meeting the Director introduced to the Committee Ms. Ayesha A. Menai who has been selected for the position of Director H.R.  The Committee welcomed Ms. Ayesha Menai. 

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider Minutes (Report) on the Selection of core courses by students

1.1       In compliance with the decision of the Executive Committee meeting of October 13, 2008 Dr. Sayeed Ghani and Mr. Mobin Khalili held meeting with two chairpersons and program officers including Senior Manager MIS on October 16, 2008.  They discussed in the meeting issues pertaining to allowing selection of core courses in addition to elective courses by students.  They discussed in detail the pros and cons of giving students the option to choose a teacher of their choice for required courses, and the technical issues to manage this process by the program offices and the current CMS. They finally recommended that:

a)      The IBA should give students the right to choose core courses of their choice offered by various teachers. The students should be allowed to finalize core courses of their choice within the first week of the semester.

b)      It should be mandatory for all chairpersons to meet the deadline in allocating courses to their faculty members so that when registration dates are announced for the next semester students are aware of the teachers assigned to their selected courses.

c)      The students’ selection of course(s) should be monitored to determine whether their choice is driven by quality of teacher or their leniency in grading and class discipline.

d)     In order to implement the policy for selection of core courses registration into the CMS via OCRS, it would require at least 1 month time to make the changes in the system after the go ahead is given. Hence the earliest it can be implemented is for Fall 2009.

1.2              Mirza Sardar Hussain suggested that the IBA should determine correlation, if any, between the evaluation of teachers and their grading style by assigning this job to students.  He said that if a course is over-subscribed, the grading pattern of the faculty taking that course should be looked into.  He said that the faculty evaluation questionnaires also need to be modified.

1.3              It was observed that the same course outline, reading material, and a common exam of a particular course would minimize the tendency of doing a course with a particular teacher.

DECISION

1.4              The Executive Committee approved the recommendations put forth.

1.5              The Director renamed the Committee which presented its recommendations on selection of core courses into an Implementation Committee and decided to include all department chairs, and one member of the Academic Committee into the Implementation Committee.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani will have the authority of the Executive Committee in implementing the policy and approach the Director to resolve any difficulty he faces.

1.6              The Committee decided that a student may be hired to serve as Secretary of the Implementation Committee.

ACTION

1.7              Dr. Sayeed Ghani will take action to implement the proposal approved by the Executive Committee in regard to the choice of core courses by the students with effect from Fall Semester 2009. He will inform and consult with all the department chairs. He will also ensure that the CMS is modified as required.

Item No. 2      To consider a note from Mr. Shameem Ahmed, General Manager Planning &Development, regarding repair and maintenance work in preparation of Convocation 2008.

2.1              Mr. Shameem Ahmad referring to a request from Assistant Admin Officer for repair and maintenance work in preparation of Convocation 2008 stated that their department does not have the capacity to accomplish the tasks related to repair and maintenance in a month without ignoring all other jobs and projects that they have in hand.  He further stated that the completion of the 10 tasks listed in the request would involve an expenditure of about Rs.3 million.  He requested the Committee to approve the required funds and curtail the list of the tasks to a couple of essential and urgent items.

DECISION

2.2              The Committee decided that there was no need to carry out the major tasks outlined in the request. The Registrar and the General Manager would make a physical inspection and identify the minor repairs which need to be done within the approved Maintenance Budget so that no extra budget approval is required and get the job completed before Convocation 2008.

Item No. 3      To consider request from Mr. Syed Mohsin Ali Naqvi, Web Administrator for attending a course on Web Engineering conducted by the NED University.

3.1              Mr. Mohsin Ali Naqvi, Web Administrator requested the Committee to allow him to attend a course on Web Engineering conducted by the NED University.  He mentioned in his application that the classes will be on Sundays for four hours duration each day and the total duration of the course is three months.  After discount the fee for the course would be Rs.8,000/-

DECISION

3.2              The Committee allowed Mr. Mohsin Naqvi to attend the course as mentioned in 3.1 above.

3.3              The Director said that he would approve such requests as long as provision exists in the budget and there is no need to bring such requests to the Executive Committee for approval. He further said that the Director H.R. will be requested to prepare a policy on training and development of faculty and staff. 

ACTION

3.4              Dr. Sayeed Ghani will inform Mr. S. Mohsin Naqvi of the decision of the Executive Committee.

Item No. 4      Implementation Plan of the Academic Committee.

4.1              Dr. Muhammad Nishat presented the implementation plan of the Academic Committee.

DECISION

4.2       The Committee noted the implementation plan of the Academic Committee.

Item No.  5     Policy on disposal of old record

5.1       The Director Finance said that there is no policy of disposal of old record due to which they are facing space limitations for new records.

DECISION

5.2              The Committee decided that the Finance, HR, Purchase/Administration and Examinations departments should prepare a policy on retaining / disposal of old record and present it to the Executive Committee for approval.

ACTION

5.3              The Registrar will convene a meeting of the heads of the departments mentioned above to prepare a policy on retention / disposal of old records and present it before the Executive Committee for approval.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
