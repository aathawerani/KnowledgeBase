 1.2       The Dean & Director asked as to why mobilization of the new contractor to library was taking so much time. He said that priority should be given to selected works such as false ceiling, floor and furniture.  Mr. Mahmood Ali said that those are the priority works.  He said that HVAC has been completed upto 80%.  The Registrar suggested that instead of giving up-date in percentage they should itemise the major work of the Library as well as Admin Building and present the progress in itemised form. Mr. Mahmood Ali agreed that from next report he will do accordingly.

 1.3       Mr. Amin Qureshi reported that by end of July, 2014 second and third floor of the Admin Building would be ready and by August 15, 2014 1st floor would also be ready. The Dean & Director advised the Registrar to get the Finance Department shifted to the first floor of Admin Building by August 15, 2014.  He said that if shifting is done during the presence of the contractor, any defect / fault in the building or facilities can be rectified easily.

 1.4       On MP Building Mr. Mahmood Ali reported that there was an issue of ground floor ACs’ relocation for which rerouting was required which took time but finally it was resolved. Mr. Mahmood Ali said that they would try to complete it in November 2014. The Registrar said that he had identified the shifting and relocation of ACs in the very beginning.

 1.5       The Dean & Director asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to arrange a meeting of the Architect with Mr. Asif Jaffer and the Registrar to finalise the design of the Old Mosque.

 1.6       Mr. Mahmood Ali said that the Architect will submit bid evaluation report of the Clinic on June 2, 2014 and the award of work is expected by end of June 2014 as it will first go to the Procurement Committee and then to the A&FC for approval. The Dean & Director said that the A&FC meeting is scheduled for June 7, 2014. He asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to obtain approval of Procurement Committee through circular resolution or by requesting an emergency meeting so that the recommendation of the PC could be presented to the A&FC on June 7, 2014 for approval.

 1.7       The Registrar suggested that for Convocation purpose at least front area of the Internal Road should be completed. Mr. Amin Qureshi said that the Contractor for External Development has been given several options to complete the work failing which the contract will be terminated for non-performance. IBA has asked for bank guarantee but the contractor expressed his inability to provide bank guarantee. Now they have been given the option similar to the library residual work option. The Dean & Director said that the agreement will be between the Venture Crest and the new Contractor but the selected contractor should be of such standing and reputation that he is able to deliver on time. He suggested that the internal road from Canteen to Aman-CED, and from Gate No.1 to Gate No.4 be completed.

 1.8       Mr. Amin Qureshi reported that material samples for Monument Garden have been submitted for approval of NBCL. He said that Venture Crest will be working on the Monument Garden as it is not a complicated work. The Dean & Director suggested to give the work to Venture Crest which is on priority and then give other work so that he may deliver on time.

 1.9       The Dean & Director said that Ms. Adeela Tarek has given approval of Rs.15 million for the Courtyard. He said that they (Ms. Adeela Tarek) would engage a contractor who would work according to their design.       

 1.10     Mr. Mehmood Ali said that carpeting material is brought in bulk quantity for which they need larger area. He said that on arrival of the carpet material it would not take more than two days to complete the parking lot. The Registrar said that carpeting material in bulk quantity is required as ring road, parking lot, and road are to be carpeted. The Dean & Director said that if Venture Crest is short of resources both money as well as human resources, then they should first complete the ring road and parking road and then courtyard and monument garden.

 1.11     Mr. Mahmood Ali said that in 10 days time pathways of the Main Lawn would be completed.  He reported that pavers in front of library are also being placed. He said that landscaping will be done after the pathways and roads are completed.

 1.12     Mr. Mehmood Ali reported that enough work has been done on Sewerage Treatment Plant. To a question as to when sewerage water will be pumped to the grounds, Mr. Mahmood Ali replied that by end of July 2014 it will be ready and connected to pump. On the Internal Drainage System Mr. Mahmood Ali said that the work has been started.  The Dean & Director suggested to check the slope and angle of the water flow so that rainy water should not be standing on the ground.

 1.13     Mr. Mahmood Ali reported that gas pipeline has been received and the work is in progress.

 DECISIONS & ACTION

 1.14     The Committee noted the monthly progress report along with the observations noted above.

 1.15     The Projects Department would present itemised up-dates on major items of Admin Building and Library in their monthly reports.

 1.16     Mr. Mahmood Ali would obtain approval of Procurement Committee through circular resolution or by requesting an emergency meeting for bid on Clinic so that the recommendation of the PC could be presented to the A&FC on June 7, 2014 for approval.

 Item No.1(b)   Progress Report on Aman Tower.

 1.17     The Dean & Director said that he had invited all stakeholders on May 29, 2014 to discuss and resolve all outstanding issues and bottlenecks in the progress of Aman Tower for smooth working and timely completion of the project. He said that on furniture package he was surprised to know that there was a change in design due to which much time was taken.  The Registrar said that there was no change except tablet chairs which was very minor.  The Dean & Director said that the furniture will be ordered into the following two parts:

a)  Classrooms, breakouts, faculty, seminar rooms delivery of which would be made by October 2014.  b)  Residential furniture for 9th and 10th floors would be delivered by December 2014.  

Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that there is a defect in the furniture design as there is no spring. The Registrar said that he has taken care of it and it is being rectified.

1.18     The Dean & Director said that bridge design was not decided as there was no cost estimate. He said that he has instructed that no change would be done in any floor now. He said that EA Consulting has been asked to give the drawings and details of civil, electrical and mechanical works in a week. The civil work will be executed after completion of MEP works in 5 days per floor as committed by MEP Contractors. He asked Mr. Mahmood Ali to tell Mr. Rehan Sheikh to follow the time line and keep the Dean & Director informed of the progress. 

1.19     The Dean & Director said that his concern was that during the month of Ramazan the work may be slowed down but the contractor has assured that in Ramazan they would work at night.  He said that this is also to be monitored by Mr. Rehan Sheikh.  

1.20     The Dean & Director said that granite was rejected because its grains were not acceptable.  Now it is to be brought in a month so that work on facade is completed. He said that Mr. Mansoor had said that he would give approval on data cable of 3M because there was a dispute on the genuineness of 3M.

1.21     MEP Contractors will deploy their senior persons having some empowerments for good coordination and finishing of project on target dates.  Mr. Rehan Sheikh will monitor their deployment. On lighting issues M/s NEC were asked to fulfil Consultant`s requirement and arrange samples and mock ups from Philips as it is of good standard and used all over the world.

1.22     The Dean & Director said that there was a problem on elevators due to late approval of shop drawings.  Some material like guardrails will be delivered in advance within one & half months to start the lift installation work. M/s. Ziafco have been instructed to complete their work in all respect by October, 2014. 

DECISION & ACTION

1.23     The Committee noted the progress on Aman Tower. Mr. Rehan Sheikh will monitor the progress and time line of each and every item and report to the Dean & Director if there is any deviation or any issue.

 Item No. 3      Status Report by the Registrar.

3.1 Suggestions on Design Aspects of Prayer Hall - The Registrar would talk to Mr. Aamir Abbasi for the additional expenditure of Rs.1 million:  The Registrar reported that Mr. Aamir Abbasi is not accessible despite his repeated efforts. The Registrar informed the Committee that the work of wind-wall of the Prayer Hall has been completed. The Dean & Director asked the Registrar to remind him to talk to Mr. Aamir Abbasi for the additional Rs.1 million.

 3.2       The Registrar to coordinate with Mr. Khalid Pervez of KP Associates for fire safety audit at the Campus: The Registrar reported that the Manager Operations & Maintenance has done the required coordination.

 3.3       Smart Card: The Registrar reported that detailed proposal has been forwarded to the Head of ICT for preparation of BOQ and tender.

             The Dean & Director said that we have to introduce the Smart Card in September 2014.  Mr. Imran Batada said that it would be difficult to introduce Smart Card in September this year. However, he said that in up-coming semester implementation would be started.

 3.4       ATM Machine/Bank Branch: The Registrar reported that one each HBL ATM has been installed at Main and City Campuses.  He said that the proposal to have access available for UBL ATM from behind the cafeteria was examined but not supported for security reasons. Moreover, soon another ATM will be available to City Campus community from the JS Bank in the MJS Block.

 DECISION

 3.5       The up-dates given above were noted and approved.

 There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
