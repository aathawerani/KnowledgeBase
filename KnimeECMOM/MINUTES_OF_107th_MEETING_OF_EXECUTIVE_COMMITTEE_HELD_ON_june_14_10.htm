MINUTES OF THE 107TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, JUNE 14, 2010

 

The 107th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, June 14, 2010 at 9:30 a.m. at the Conference Room of IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                            Director

  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                     Associate Dean

  3. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN        Registrar

  4. Mr. Ali Taha                                  Director Finance

  5. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz               Director Projects

  6. Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                           Director ICT

  7. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                       Director H.R.

 

Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects attended the meeting by Invitation

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      Renovation & Refurbishment of Administration Building at IBA Main Campus – Final Approval of Area and Cost

 

1.1       The Director Projects stated in his note that the work for the detailed planning & designing for the Renovation and Refurbishment of Administration Building at IBA Main was awarded to M/s. ICON by Audit & Finance Committee in its meeting held on July 19, 2007 and the conceptual layout plan was approved by A&FC in its meeting held on January 26, 2008. The approved plan was sent to the Architect for finalization of schematic drawings who submitted the same in March, 2010. Architect was also asked to provide the drawing mentioning the increase in area in order to know the existing and the new addition area for analysis.

 

1.2       The Director Projects informed the Committee that as per budget proposed for the year 2008-09, the estimated cost of the Project as of October, 2008 was taken as Rs.76 million (excluding the cost of furniture, fixtures & ICT infrastructure). He said that the existing Administration Building had an area of 47,520 sq-ft at that time and the cost of renovation was estimated at Rs.1,600 per sq-ft.  He said that as now, M/s. ICON have given the estimated cost of construction of Rs.246.592 million @ Rs.3,853 per sq-ft for Civil, MEP & HVAC Works for an area of around 64,500 sq-ft. Thus, there is an increase in area from 47,520 sq-ft to 64,500 sq-ft and the increase in estimated cost from Rs.76 Million to Rs.246.592 Million (excluding the cost of furniture, fixtures & ICT infrastructure). Taking an average cost of ongoing renovation works, the cost shall be @ Rs. 3,600 per sq-ft. By applying this rate to an area of 64,500 sq-ft, the total estimated cost comes to Rs. 232 Million. He provided following details of the total increase of Rs.156 million:

 

·         Increase in cost because of Area increase = 16,980 say 17,000 sq-ft @ Rs. 3,600 per sq-ft = Rs.61 million.

 

·         Increase due to increase in cost per sq-ft i.e. (3,600 – 1,600 = 2,000 per sq-ft) Old Area 47,520 x 2,000 = Rs.95 million

 

·         Total Increase due to increase in area and cost of construction = 61 + 95 = Rs.156 million

 

1.3       The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to approve the cost of Rs.232.20 million for an area of 64,500 Sq-ft @ Rs.3,600 per Sq-ft.

 

1.4       The Director wanted to know as to why there is no difference of cost on the renovation & refurbishment and new construction.  He said that the renovation and refurbishment work should not cost more than Rs.1600/- to Rs.2000/- per sq-ft.  He said that he would not allow renovation and refurbishment of old building at the construction cost for new buildings.  He asked the Director Projects to ask the architect to redesign the building at lower costs than he has proposed.

 

1.5       He asked the Director Projects to shift the proposed ICT set up from the 3rd floor to the ground floor and concentrate on the ICT requirements. He said that his priority is ICT servers to be installed as soon as possible and he would not make huge investment for refurbishment.

 

DECISION:

 

1.6       The Committee agreed with the Director and did not approve the proposal for renovation and refurbishment of Administration Building at Main Campus @Rs.3,600/-.

 

ACTION:

 

1.7       The Director Projects would make arrangements to accommodate ICT at ground floor without making any extension in the area.  Tenders for the ICT floor would be given priority.

           

 

Item No. 2      Renovation & Refurbishment of Existing Library at IBA Main Campus – Final Approval of Area and Cost

 

2.1       The Director Projects stated that the proposal to approve the increase in area from 11,138 to 27,011 Sq-ft. was presented in the 90th meeting of the Executive Committee and the EC in principal had approved his recommendations to increase the construction area from 11,138 Sq-ft to 27,011 Sq-ft.  He further said that as per budget proposed for the year 2009-10, the estimated cost of the Project as of October, 2008 was taken as Rs.60 million (22,565 sq-ft @ 2,660 per sq-ft =  Rs.60 million). He clarified that this estimate does not include the cost of furniture, fixtures & ICT infrastructure.

 

2.2       The Director Projects reported that based on the revised estimate of M/s. ICON, the estimated cost of construction comes to Rs.97.240 million @ Rs. 3,600 per sq-ft for a revised area of 27,011 sq-ft. There is an increase in area from 22,565 sq-ft to 27,011 sq-ft and the increase in estimated cost from Rs.60 Million to Rs. 97 Million.

 

2.3       The Director Projects requested the EC to approve the cost of Rs.97.240 million for an area of 27,011 Sq-ft @ 3,600 per Sq-ft.

 

DECISION:

 

2.4       The Committee did not approve the proposal for the same reasons as recorded in 1.4 above i.e for there being no difference in cost for the construction of new building and renovation of old building.

 

2.5       The Committee further decided that the Career Development Centre presently housed at the Library would later be shifted to Multi-purpose building.

 

 

Item No. 3      To consider and Approve SOP for Staff Welfare Fund

 

3.1       The Director Finance informed the Committee that the Policy for Staff Welfare Fund has already been approved by the Board. However, he said that the SOP after approval by the EC would be presented to the A&FC for approval.  He presented the SOP for Staff Welfare Fund.

 

DECISION

 

3.2       The Director requested the Director HR to review the draft SOP for Staff Welfare Fund and send her comments on the SOP to the Director Finance.

 

ACTION

 

3.3       After incorporating the changes, if any, to be suggested by the  Director HR, the Director Finance would present the draft SOP to the A&FC.

 

 

Item No. 4      To consider request from Mr. Hizabbar Hussain for extension in contract.

 

4.1       The Director stated that Mr. Hizabbar Hussain retired on June 30, 2009.  He had applied for extension of service and the EC decided to rehire him for one year only. He has requested for further extension on humanitarian grounds.  She also presented the recommendations from faculty and staff which Mr. Hizabbar had submitted for his rehiring.

 

4.2       The Director said that it was not the right way to present recommendations / get signatures of all faculty and staff and submit it. He said that they have to induct new blood, with advanced skills so that new and fit people can come up. If retired people, continue like this then younger people would not be able to get jobs.

 

4.3       Dr. Muhammad Nishat suggested that if EC is considering Mr. Hizabbar Hussain’s request for extension for another one year, he should be told that this would be the last extension and after that no further extension would be allowed.

 

DECISION

 

4.4       The Executive Committee decided that Mr. Hizabbar Hussain will be given one year’s extension and this would be last time extension.

 

ACTION:

 

4.5       The Director HR to sanction one year extension in the contractual appointment of Mr. Hizabbar Hussain and notify him in writing that there would be no further extension after the of the contract is over.

 

 

Item No. 5      Settlement of Disputed Claims of Architect M/s. ICON.

 

5.1       The Director Projects presented a detailed report on the disputed claims of Architects M/s. Icon. He classified the claims into three categories. The total amount claimed by the Architect was of Rs.13,992,284/- out of which an amount of Rs.1,308,851/- under category III was payable.

  

DECISION

 

5.2       The Committee advised the Director Projects to first obtain the opinions of Finance Department, Audit Department and Legal Advisor pertaining to the matter and bring in his recommendations based on the opinions of the above mentioned departments /legal advisor to the Executive Committee for consideration.

 

5.3       The Committee approved the recommendation of the Director Projects to clear the Payable Invoices under Category - III to the Architect which amount to Rs.1,308,851/-

 

ACTION

 

5.4       The Director Projects would obtain the opinions of Finance Department, Audit Department and Legal Advisor on the claims of Architect M/s. Icon and give his recommendations based on the opinions of the above mentioned departments /legal advisor to the Executive Committee for consideration.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
