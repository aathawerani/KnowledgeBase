MINUTES OF THE 45th MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON APRIL 27, 2009

 

The 45th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, April 27, 2009 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Associate Dean
  2. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Associate Dean
  3. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN          Registrar
  4. Mr. Mobin Khalili                               Controller of Examinations
  5. Mr. Ali Taha                                    Director Finance
  6. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                    Director Projects
  7. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                         Director I.T.
  8. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                        Director H.R.

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve Strategy Implementation Plan.

1.1       The Director H.R. presented the Strategy Implementation Plan, organization oversight, strategic agenda, objective, role profiles for department heads as provided by the members Executive Committee.

1.2       Mr. Mobin Khalili said that the Enrolment & Degree Section and the Affiliation Department have not been included in the Service Units of the Organization Structure of his Department. The Director H.R. clarified that the Enrolment & Degree Section and the Affiliation Department are already in the Department of Examinations. 

DECISION

Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that these are additional sections which may not necessarily be part of the Examinations Department.  He suggested that the service units should be included in the Examination Department.  The Committee agreed to his suggestion.

1.3       While commenting on the organogram of Projects & Facilities Management Department, the Director Projects observed that the line of control shown in dotted line between Director Projects and General Manager (P & D) does not truly represent the practical picture of the existing working relationship between the Director Projects and the General Manager (P & D). He further observed that practical difficulties are being faced when it comes to project execution, monitoring, control and schedule. He cited the example of accountability aspects related to the project wherein division of all renovations and new projects has been done between him and G.M. (P&D). He then suggested that the G.M. (P&D) should be made fully responsible for the projects assigned to him and that one senior person for those  projects which are  G.M.(P&D’s) responsibility, be engaged because of limited staff available with him. He informed that this is also the requirement of HEC authorities too. If Director Projects is to be responsible for all the projects given to G.M. (P&D), then that senior person be engaged to report to Director Projects. He requested that this matter be sorted out as early as possible.

DECISION

The Committee requested the Director H.R. to resolve this issue.

1.4              The Registrar said that there is an understanding that the Maintenance Department would be given under the Registrar’s control.  

1.5              The Director I.T. said that he would send a revised organogram of I.T. Department moving the position of Data Base Manager from the lower end to the middle level.

1.6              The Director H.R. then presented the IBA Strategy Map. 

DECISION

1.7              The Committee agreed to the following amendments / modifications in the IBA Strategy Map:

·         Academic Excellence be included in Operations

·         Financial Assistance / Merit Scholarships to be included in Differentiators under the main heading of ‘Customer’ and the words “City Campus” be replaced with “Strategically Located Campus”.   

1.7       After discussion on Strategy Map, the Director H.R. presented the Strategic Agenda provided by the EC members.

1.8       While discussing the Strategic Agenda submitted by the Projects Planning & Facilities Management Department, the Registrar suggested that the scope of renovation projects and the schedule must be identified.

DECISION

1.9       The following additions were made to the Strategic Agenda of the Faculty of Business Administration:

·         The text of the Strategic Intent be rephrased as “To be ranked amongst the top 100 global business schools”.

·         “Quality Assurance to be ensured” and “Academic Research and Case Study” to be added in Operational Excellence.

·         “Membership and Partnership” and “Exchange Program” to be added after “External Linkages”.

1.10          Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that he would re-submit the Strategic Agenda of the Faculty of Computer Sciences.

1.11          The Director H.R. also presented the Role Profile submitted by the EC members.

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
