MINUTES OF THE 133RD MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, DECEMBER 20, 2010

 

The 133rd meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, December 20, 2010 at 9:30 a.m. at the Conference Room, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                   Associate Dean

2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                             Associate Dean

3.      Mr. Ali Taha                                          Director Finance

4.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                       Director Projects

5.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                   Director ICT

6.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                               Director H.R.

 

The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      Appointment of Project Managers for detailed supervision and Project Management Services of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus.

 

1.1 The Director Projects reported that this item was a part of the agenda for 127th meeting of the Executive Committee held on November 8, 2010 but at his request it was deferred till the finalization of an Inquiry Report against M/s. Integ who were the lowest evaluated responsive bidder. He stated that M/s. Integ were undergoing an inquiry in response to the Dean and Director’s suggestions regarding their disqualification for any future projects. He said that he had sent a detailed letter on October 27, 2010 to M/s. INTEG asking them to submit explanations of the observations raised by Mr. S. N. Mumtaz, Quantity Surveyor, IBA. M/s. Integ submitted their explanation on November 22, 2010 which was analyzed by Mr. S. N. Mumtaz and then by Engr. Abdur Rehman, P.E. (Planning & Contracts). According to the reports, M/s. INTEG admitted that some mathematical mistakes had occurred which were duly corrected. Another point was that M/s. Integ had not provided detailed reasons of variations, to which M/s. INTEG had assured to provide complete details while describing the variations in future. He said that the mistakes were identified in running bills and all such mistakes are taken care of in final bills. He further said that M/s. INTEG provided some statistics showing how they have saved 35% on the variation orders of the contractors.  He said that it has been noted by Engr. A. Rehman that M/s. INTEG have been performing their duties with sincerity and devotion. The mathematical mistakes in running bills are unavoidable but rectifiable in final bills.

 

1.2 The Director Projects reported that in view of the explanation of M/s. INTEG and its subsequent verification he had recommended to the Dean & Director to waive the condition imposed by him for disqualifying M/s. Integ for any future projects and the Dean & Director had very kindly waived the imposed condition.

 

1.3 The Director Projects then resubmitted the proposal for appointment of Project Managers which was deferred in the 127th meeting of EC held on November 8, 2010.  He stated that a meeting of the Technical Evaluation Committee was held on 1st November, 2010 to discuss and decide the appointment of Project Managers for Detailed Supervision and Project Management Services of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus. Out of 10, nine Project Managers submitted their Technical Proposals. Out of 100, the points scored by the nine Project Managers were as under:

1.      M/s. EA Consulting (Private) Limited                       90 Points        

2.      M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited                    91 Points        

3.      M/s. Consult-Tech International                             90 Points        

4.      M/s. Shahzad Associates                                      73 Points        

5.      M/s. MM Pakistan (Private) Limited                         91 Points        

6.      M/s. NESPAK                                                      88 Points        

7.      M/s. INTEG                                                        89 Points        

8.      M/s. Arif Shah Associates                                     76 Points        

9.      M/s. Shankland COX Limited                                  88 Points

           

1.4       The Director Projects said that after the Technical Evaluation, the Financial Proposals were opened in the presence of the Technical Evaluation Committee. The financial proposal of M/s. Shahzad Associates was not opened as they scored less than 75 Points and were not able to meet the minimum technical criteria. The rates quoted by the eight Project Managers were as under:

1.      M/s. EA Consulting (Private) Limited                 6.90% of the total cost of Project      

2.      M/s. AAA Partnership (Private) Limited              6.72% of the total cost of Project      

3.      M/s. Consult-Tech International                       6.03% of the total cost of Project

4.      M/s. MM Pakistan (Private) Limited                    10.82% of the total cost of Project    

5.      M/s. NESPAK                                                 3.50% of the total cost of Project      

6.      M/s. INTEG                                                   3.00% of the total cost of Project

7.      M/s. Arif Shah Associates                                6.00% of the total cost of Project

8.      M/s. Shankland COX Limited                             16.79% of the total cost of Project    

 

1.5 The Director Projects stated that as per the Comparative Statement, M/s. INTEG have a combined valuation of 91 points and obtained 1st Rank.

 

1.6 The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to consider the selection of M/s. INTEG based on competitive biddings and accord their approval. He further suggested that in case M/s. INTEG are not selected, then M/s. NESPAK being the 2nd lowest be considered for award of work at a rate of 3.50% of the total cost of the Project. They have already been awarded the Project Management of Renovation & Refurbishment of Existing Library at IBA Main Campus at a rate of 3.50% of the total cost of Project through competition.

 

DECISION

 

1.7 The Committee approved the recommendation for appointment of M/s. Integ for detailed supervision and Project Management Services of Students’ Centre at IBA Main Campus at 03% of the total cost of the Project.

 

            There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
