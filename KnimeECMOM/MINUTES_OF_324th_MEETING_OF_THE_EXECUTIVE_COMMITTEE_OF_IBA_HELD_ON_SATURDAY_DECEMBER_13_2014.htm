Mr. Muhammad Anwar, Head Librarian attended the meeting on special invitation. 

 

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Up-date on implementation of new Library Automation System.

 

1.1       Mr. Muhammad Anwar, Head Librarian, presented the up-date on the Library Automation System (LAS).  He said that the automation process with data extraction from previous system was started in June 2014.  All the data was migrated to test server in September 2014, after debugging and converting into MARC standards. To orient staff with the new system, a six days training about the different modules was conducted online through webex.  He reported that finally, without any stoppage in any of the library activities or services, all the data was migrated to live server in October 2014 which included around 53000 items, 10000 members record, and 30000 transactions of historical circulation data.

 

1.2       Mr. Muhammad Anwar briefed the members about the key features of the Library Automation System, key benefits, and the overall plan of action.   

 

1.3       The Dean & Director asked if he can access e-book.  Mr. Anwar replied that they have included in their next step to incorporate e-books which would hopefully be implemented by February 2015.

 

1.4       Dr. Sayeed Ghani asked if the System is integrated with the financial system for recovery of fine, if any. Mr. Anwar said that the fine is not received at Library. However, when the invoice for semester fee would be generated by the Finance Department, it would include the fine, if any.  

 

1.5       The Dean & Director asked if the LAS has been shared with the Library Committee. Mr. Anwar confirmed that the Library Committee has been apprised of the system. The Dean & Director suggested to brief the faculty about the Library Automation System from user point of view and to prepare a pamphlet highlighting its capability and functionality.

 

1.6       The Dean & Director asked the status of RFID based Library Security and Material Management System which is planned in the second phase of implementation.  Mr. Anwar informed the Committee that the RFID equipment and hardware have been delivered. He hoped that the RFID would be functional by March 2015.

 

1.7       The Dean & Director requested the Registrar to get the highlights of the Library and the Automation System included in the IBA Documentary.

 

1.8       Mr. Muhammad Anwar further informed the members that library has subscribed to a database titled Press Display that provides access to 2000 newspapers from all over the world. The news headlines can also be listened and translated through this system.  

 

1.9       The Dean & Director suggested to inform the faculty and students of the facilities added to our Library and do their proper marketing through seminars.

 

DECISIONS & ACTION

 

1.10     The members were very pleased to note the implementation of the Library Automation System and congratulated the Head Librarian on making the system functional.      

 

1.11     The Head Librarian would brief the faculty about the Library Automation System from user point of view and also prepare a pamphlet highlighting its capability and functionality.  He would also inform the faculty and students of the facilities added to our Library and do their proper marketing at various forum.

 

1.12     The Registrar would get the highlights of the Library and the Automation System included in the IBA Documentary.

 

Item No.2       To consider observations of Mr. Muhammad Asif Jaffer, on Technical Evaluation Committee.

 

2.1       Mr. Muhammad Asif Jaffer, faculty member who had also been the Chairman, Technical Evaluation Committee sent some observations related to criteria in RFQ.  According to him the restricted criteria in RFQ results in poor response from the vendors and the TEC is left with no choice.

 

2.2       The members considered the observations made by Mr. Asif Jaffer. It was pointed out that for RFQ and tender they have to follow the SPPRA rules. It was also stated that TEC has been helping in revisiting the criteria.

 

DECISION

 

2.3       The Committee decided that Mr. Asif Jaffer be invited to the EC meeting with his proposal how to make the criteria more flexible.

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

3.1       The following decisions were taken:

 

o   CED and first floor of NBP Technology Centre is to be vacated on priority and offices to be shifted to the Fauji Foundation Building.

o   Breakout Rooms in both the Campuses should exclusively be used by students. The offices located there should be provided alternate space.

o   Program Directors, Program Coordinators and the persons who have joint appointments (FCS and Maths for example) will be provided separate offices at both the campuses. Other faculty members based in Main Campus will be provided satellite office (shared office) at the City Campus and vice-a-versa.

 

ACTION

 

3.2       The Registrar would get the above decisions implemented.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
