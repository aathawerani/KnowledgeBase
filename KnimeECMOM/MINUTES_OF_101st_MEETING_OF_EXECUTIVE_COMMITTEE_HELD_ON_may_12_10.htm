MINUTES OF THE 101ST MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON WEDNESDAY, MAY 12, 2010


 

The 101st meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Wednesday, May 12, 2010 at 4:30 p.m. at the Conference Room of APWA Building, IBA City Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                              Director

  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                       Associate Dean

  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                             Associate Dean

  4. Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN          Registrar

  5. Mr. Mobin Khalili                               Controller of Examinations

  6. Mr. Ali Taha                                    Director Finance

  7. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                 Director Projects

  8. Dr. Arshad. Siddiqi                            Director ICT

  9. Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                         Director H.R.

 

The following attended the meeting on special invitation:

 

1.      Mr. Shahid Zaki                                   Director EMBA

2.      Mr. Muhammad Saleem Umer Director EMBA-BFS

3.      Mirza Izhar Husain                              Director CEE

 

The following items were discussed:

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider Business Plans of Centres for 2010-11.

 

Business Plan of EMBA-BFS

 

1.1              Mr. M. Saleem Umer, Director EMBA (Banking & Financial Services) presented the Business Plan for 2010-11. He gave a brief introduction of MBA-BFS program, the business process, its current standing and the future direction. He presented the following highlights on the current standing (where they are):

 

·         Batch I of EMBA BFS is on board since October 2009 and has completed learning of two trimesters. Batch II was brought in from January 2010 and has since completed its first trimester. Batch III is in the process of registration and is scheduled to commence its knowledge journey from May 14, 2010.

 

·         In all 75 participants will be attending EMBA BFS program by the end of Current Financial year (2009 -2010).

 

·         Nine learning faculty members will be steering the courses offered under current trimester ( May  to August 2010)

 

·         Program Director, EMBA BFS with the help of one assistant is managing the program.

 

 

·         Financials:

Estimated fee revenue for FY 2009 -2010                             Rs.10 million

                  Estimated cost (All inclusive) FY 2009-10                            Rs.  7 million

                      Fixed cost                                 Rs.  4 million

                      Variable cost                             Rs.  3 million

      Estimated Surplus                                                                   Rs.  3 million

 

1.2              Giving details of the future direction, Mr. Saleem Umer presented the following estimates:

 

·         Continue the learning process of all the three batches through following trimesters:

                        September to December 2010

                        January to April 2011

                        May to July 2011

 

·         Re-offer the program in September 2010 and January 2011 to bring 50 new entrants for regular EMBA BFS and certification program.

 

·         15 learning faculty members will be engaged to steer the courses at maximum load level for five batches.

 

·         Financials (Budgeted for FY 2010 to 2011)

                                   

Estimated fee revenue for FY 2010 -2011                       Rs.23.6 million

                  Estimated cost FY 2010 - 11                                            Rs.15.0 million

                        Fixed cost                               Rs.   5 million

                        Variable cost                           Rs. 10 million

                  Estimated Surplus                                                             Rs. 8.6 million

 

1.3              Mr. Saleem Umer made a request for (a) three additional learning / classrooms, and (2) one additional officer for day to day and micro management.

 

1.4              The Director suggested to utilize the Main Campus for classes. He also advised Mirza Izhar Husain to plan to offer his classes at Main Campus. Mr. Saleem Umer said that for 4th batch they would think of utilizing the Main Campus.    

 

1.5              The Director asked the Director Finance to charge 30% overhead cost to all programs.

 

1.6              The Director approved the requirement of one additional officer as requested by Mr. Saleem Umer.

 

Business Plan of EMBA (Public Sector, and Corporate Sector)

 

1.7              Mr. Shahid Zaki presented the business plan of EMBA Public Sector (PS), and Corporate Sector (CS) programs. He talked about EMBA program positioning, Porter’s 5 forces model for business education, target market, business and marketing objectives, and marketing strategy. He also presented timeline of student growth in EMBA (CS & PS) Programs, expected revenue from both CS & PS Programs and the proposed organogram which included (a) Marketing & Liaison, (b) EMBA Program Coordinator, (c) Program Development.  He said that he would form a committee to ensure relevance of courses by up-dating curriculum and accommodating changing industry needs.  

 

1.8              The Director suggested to mention about the Qarz-e-Hasna that would be available to eligible students who have financial needs.

 

1.9              The Director advised the Director Finance to separately present the regular programs and the EMBA, CEE, CED and NTH in the budget.  He approved the organogram presented by Mr. Shahid Zaki and asked him to revise the budget figures.

 

Business Plan of CEE:

 

1.10          Mr. Izhar Hussain Mirza presented the Business Plan of CEE. He presented the Mission Statement of CEE, the following three Strategy choices out of which the third they have opted (i)  LBS Model: Capitalize on Faculty, (ii) Hire CEE Specific Faculty, (iii) Course or workshop design with consensus + benchmarking & offer with multiple facilitators (faculty + practitioners), and the products.  He also presented highlights of 2009-10, budget and forecast for 2010-14. He requested for approval for additional positions for a Marketing Executive, a Course Development, a Program Office Assistant, and a Messenger. He estimated an Operating Revenue for the year 2010-11 at Rs.23.5 million and the Operating Expenses at Rs.23.14 million.

 

1.11     The Director advised to include NGO’s also in the Mission Statement. He also advised to revisit the budget and try to turn it into surplus of at least Rs.3 million. He approved the manpower requirements except the position of Messenger.      

 

Budget Proposal of Director Projects:

 

1.12     The Director Projects reported that because of the delay in the start of the projects and non-completion of the projects only Rs.220 million out of a budget of Rs.737 million has been spent. 

 

1.13     The Committee discussed the budget presented by the Director Projects and finally decided that the Director Finance and the Director Projects should reconsider the budget for Projects Department.

 

Budget Proposal of Director ICT:

 

1.14    The Director ICT presenting his budget proposal stated that the ICT requirements are dependent upon the completion of the buildings.

 

1.15     The Director said that the ERP requirements be reviewed separately.  He asked the Director Finance and the Director ICT to revise the ICT budget.

 

Budget Proposal of Director Finance:

 

1.16    The Director Finance presented the budget.  The Director inquired on some budget revenue figures and in reply the Director Finance said that he would revise the revenue figures in the final drat budget.

 

1.17    The Director decided that he would discuss the budget with major budget holders before finalizing the budget.

     

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
