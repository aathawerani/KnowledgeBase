The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No.1 To consider and approve conceptual design of Faculty Apartments at IBA Staff Colony.

 

1.1    Mr. Shameem Ahmed, Director HEC Projects said that Ms. Sana Tanveer, Junior Architect of IBA has prepared a design of faculty apartments at IBA Staff Colony.  He asked Ms. Sana Tanveer to present the design to the EC.

 

1.2    Ms. Sana Tanveer presented the conceptual design of G+1 faculty apartments proposed to be built near the Visiting Faculty Hostel.  She said that the covered area of each apartment would be 1520 sqft.

 

1.3    The Dean & Director appreciated Ms. Sana Tanveer for preparing and presenting the design on her own.  He suggested her that the common services and infrastructure facilities should be optimized by placing the apartment blocks in area of higher density of population. He asked Ms. Sana to present comparative picture before and after the demolition of the existing buildings and construction of new apartments.

 

1.4    To a question of entitlement of houses, the Dean & Director clarified that the buildings will not be associated with entitlements. A lecturer with larger family members may have larger apartment and a single faculty member may have small apartment.  The rent will be charged for the type of the apartment one would live in. Faculty members may be given menu of options to choose from.

 

1.5    The Director ICT suggested that one block of apartments may have variable covered area; ground floor may have larger covered area than first floor and so on.

 

1.6    The Director H.R. suggested that since the faculty apartments are basically proposed for the new faculty members coming from abroad, they should be modern, functional and aesthetically pleasing.

 

1.7    The Dean & Director appreciated the suggestions of the Director ICT and the Director H.R.

 

1.8    The Dean & Director referred to the 194th meeting of EC held on March 12, 2012 wherein some suggestions regarding topographical survey, development of questionnaire, development of master plan etc. were presented by the Director Projects and requested the Director HEC Projects and the Registrar to jointly sort them out.

          

1.9    Ms. Sana and Mr. Shameem Ahmed informed that the topographic survey was done already and they have the AutoCAD Version of the drawing.

 

DECISION

 

1.10  The Committee asked Ms. Sana Tanveer to prepare the conceptual designs for two faculty blocks and one block for IBA staff of G+2 each. The faculty block should have variable space on each floor. The blocks should be placed near the visiting faculty residence by making maximum use of the existing infrastructure.  The first block will be on the open space (on the edge of the play ground) and the second and third blocks will be on the demolished houses.   

 

ACTION

 

1.11  Ms. Sana Tanveer would prepare the conceptual design on the basis of the guidelines provided in 1.10 above and present it to the EC. 

 

1.12  The Registrar and the Director HEC Projects would look into the suggestions of the Director Projects noted in the minutes of 194th meeting of EC held on March 12, 2012 sort them out.

 

 

Item No.2 (a) Status of Time Bound Projects.

 

2.1    Relocation of Generators and Construction of new KESC Sub-station at City Campus: Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that Siemens is being pressurized to finish the work but despite their best efforts they are not willing to give a completion date. However, he hoped that from the way the work is progressing it would be substantially completed by June 30, 2012.  He said that by June 30, 2012 electrical room will be completed, generators would be moved and old KESC sub-station would be shifted.

 

          The Dean & Director said that the Executive Committee would visit the City Campus on June 30, 2012 and inspect the completion of the job.

 

        The Dean & Director wanted to know as to when the KESC sub-station will be energized.  Mr. Shameem Ahmed replied that it would be energized before June 30, 2012. However, he said that additional power would take time  maybe, by end of July 2012.  

 

2.2    The revised deadline for Aman CED Building: The Director Projects reported that the Ground Floor of Aman CED Building has been completed; first floor will be ready by 15th June 2012. Air conditioning system has been commissioned on 9th June 2012 and generator set has already been commissioned. Building ICT passive works completed.

He said that the second floor would be substantially completed by 30th June, 2012, however work on a few interior works items will continue. However, he said that they expect to get KESC supply by end of July 2012.

 

The Dean & Director contacted the KESC and asked Mr. Shameem Ahmed to follow up with Mr. Naveeds Office.  He said that we should move to CED so that Admin. Building is completely vacated for renovation because work in phases is taking too much time.

 

The Director Projects said that the electrical load of those areas of Admin. Building which would be vacated may be diverted to Aman CED Building.  Calculations would be done with the help of the Manager Maintenance to see if that load would meet the load requirement of two floors of CED building. The load requirement will not include the load of central A.C system.

 

The Director Projects said that the electrical load of Admin. Building would be switched to Aman CED Building.  He would get the electrical load of Admin. Building worked out to see if it would be enough to meet the power requirements at CED Building.

 

The Dean & Director asked the Registrar to immediately shift his (Dean & Directors) office to the CED. Later on, after physical inspection he decided that the shifting will take place only when the IT and phone facilities become available in the new building.

 

b)  Status Reports from:

 

Director HEC Projects and Director Projects:

 

2.3    Installation of central address system in the entire building of Students Centre: The Director Projects reported that the installation of Public Address System is included in the scope of work and all the certain amendments have been incorporated.

 

2.4    Sui Gas Connection for IBA: Mr. Shameem Ahmed reported that SSGC is being pursued vigorously. He reported that he has been told that the cost estimates are under preparation at SSGC.

 

        The Dean & Director said that if there is any problem he be told so that he may talk to the M.D.,SSGC.

 

2.5    Slashing of estimated cost of both phases of FCS Block: The Director Projects reported that the combined BOQ for both phases, minus the recent changes, has been signed and stamped by project managers and contractors after extensive value engineering, thus locking the cost of the whole project to Rs. 70 million from approved combined cost of FCS building including HVAC works of Rs.79,120,477/- for Phase I and Phase  II. As such the savings or Rs.9.12 million has been achieved. The cost impact of changes in plan, (let us say Phase-3) is being worked out currently by the Architects & IBA team. A preliminary cost figure of Rs.3.0 million over and above Rs.70 million (excluding multimedia projectors) was verbally quoted by Architects in the meeting with FCS team on June 02, 2012. He said that a detailed cost break up would be communicated to FCS team early next week before approval stage from EC.

 

2.6    The Director ICT would send the Content Responsibility format of the ICT Web Management Policy to all EC members and department heads for their input:  The Director ICT reported that the ICT Web Management Policy was circulated to all HoDs and the EC members; with the addition of the title of the responsible person for each for uploading the required information.  He reported that as there were no further questions the policy was implemented and is also loaded on the Portal.
