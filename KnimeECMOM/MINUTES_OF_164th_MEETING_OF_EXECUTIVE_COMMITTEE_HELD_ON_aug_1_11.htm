Item No. 1      To consider Status Reports presented by:

 

Dr. Muhammad Nishat:

 

1.1       Dr. Muhammad Nishat to discuss with department chairs about one year’s Master Program: Dr. Muhammad Nishat reported that comments on MS Marketing and HRM have been received and are being sent to Academic Committee.  He reported that the proposal for MS Finance is ready for circulation.

 

            The Dean & Director said that the students who complete their BBA are required to gain two years’ industry experience in order to apply for MBA but in the proposed one year’s MS Program there would be no time lag and they may do MS specializing in Marketing, Finance, HRM. They may also go for Ph.D without any break.  He said that although half of the BBA students are interested in pursuing MBA, yet because of the two years condition, for them particularly for the girls it is not acceptable. He said that the opinion of the students is that they should be given an option in which they may continue their education after BBA.  He said that the proposal is that there will be no admission test for MS program for IBA’s BBA students; they would automatically get admission in MS but for students coming from other institutions there will be a test. The duration of the program will be 12 months including summer in which students will be required to do exponential learning project. 

 

Mr. Mobin Khalili expressed his concern about the market value/acceptability of the proposed MS Program.

 

            The Dean & Director said that there is a great demand of specialized function because the MBA is not able to handle the functional aspect. He said that in MS there may be 12 electives.  He clarified that he does not want to dilute MBA Product.

           

Mr. Zafar Siddiqui suggested that there should be subject function test (GMAT Format) for admission for students coming from other institutions.

 

Dr. Sayeed Ghani

 

1.2       Dr. Sayeed Ghani to discuss and design one year’s Master Program in the Academic Committee and present the recommendation of the Academic Committee before the Academic Board: Dr. Sayeed Ghani reported that this item has been included in the up-coming Academic Committee meeting’s agenda.              

 

DECISION

 

1.3       The up-dates given by Dr. Muhammad Nishat and Dr. Sayeed Ghani along with the comments were noted and approved.

2.7       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that in the table presented in 2.1 above, the seminar hall has been mentioned in the CCS building but there is no seminar hall in the CCS Building.

2.10     Mr. Mobin Khalili questioned the quality of renovation works. He said that there is a general concern about the quality of work done and material used. He pointed out that cracks have appeared in walls, door handles have become loose or have come out. Doors in washrooms are falling on users. 

 

The Director ICT said that some mirrors have also got rusts.

 

2.11     The Dean & Director agreed with Mr. Khalili and said that the HEC Planning people were of the same view that the quality of material is not as good as the amount has been spent. 

 

2.12     The Director Projects said that they can still get the damaged material replaced / repaired and charge to the Contractor and Project Manager who have verified about the quality. He said that the retention money of the contractor will be retained at IBA for a year and if the contractor does not do the repair and replacement work, they may get it done and deduct the cost from the Retention Money.  He further said that the EC and A&FC will be giving approval for financial closure not for technical closure.  

 

2.13     The Registrar reported that the problem of cooling in Control Room of the Main Campus Auditorium still persists despite several efforts.

 

2.14     The Dean & Director requested the Registrar to get a list prepared in consultation with the Director Projects of the items which are to be fixed/refixed at the Main Campus.  For City Campus he requested Mr. Mobin Khalili to get a list prepared and present a report in a month to the EC. He requested the Director Projects to look into the cooling issue of the Auditorium and report to the EC.

 

2.15     The Dean & Director wanted to know as to why the per sft cost of Admin. Block, City Campus is so high.  The Director Projects clarified that it also includes 25% new construction.

 

2.16     Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested that maintenance team should be included when the handing and taking over of a project is done.

Both of them would present a report in a month to the EC.

Item No. 4      To witness presentation on Interior Decoration Planning of all the floors of Aman Tower.

 

4.1       Mr. Hasnain Lotia of M/s. E.A. Consulting (Pvt.) Ltd. presented building schematic design of each floor of Aman Tower. The following is the floor-wise plan:

 

·         Basement                    -           Parking area

·         1st floor                        -          Library

·         2nd floor                       -          Classrooms/Lecture Hall

·         3rd floor                       -           Classrooms and Breakout rooms

·         4th floor                       -           Lecture Hall

·         5th floor                       -           Faculty and Students Dining Floor

·         6th & 7th floors             -            Faculty Offices

·         8th floor                       -           Mechanical floor (Building Services)

·         9th & 10th floors           -            Executive Suits

·         11th floor                     -           Conference Centre Reception

·         12th floor                     -           Two Seminar Rooms

·         13th floor                     -           Roof Building Services

 

4.2       Mr. Hasnain Lotia said that he has suggested a flat facade because natural light will not give the desired result for multimedia presentations.

 

4.3       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that at least for classrooms natural light is necessary.  Other members also agreed with Dr. Ghani and decided that only the seminar rooms will have a flat facade; other floors will have the windows.

 

4.4       The Dean & Director said that there would be a dedicated floor for CEE which may be used, if needed, for other programs also.

 

4.5       Mr. Lotia said that fourth floor has been dedicated for CEE.

 

4.6       The Dean & Director requested the Director Projects to ask Mirza Izhar Husain what his requirements are for the CEE and inform the Consultant accordingly.

 

4.7       The Dean & Director said that he wants arrangements for round table discussions for 10 each (9 participants and one instructor/mentor at one table) in the seminar rooms.  He wanted to know if such kind of flexibility can be designed.

 

4.8       Mr. Lotia clarified that since the seating arrangements are not of fixed nature, they can make it flexible to meet the requirements. He further suggested that on the 11th floor (below the seminar rooms), there is a Conference Room which is an open block floor and it can be used for multipurpose.    

 

4.9       The Dean & Director asked the Consultant that they have to be very economical in interior work because for this Aman Foundation will not be paying; it will be borne by IBA.  At the same time he cautioned that it should not compromise on the overall facade of the building.

 

4.10     Mr. Mobin Khalili said that the proposed seating capacity in the library particularly during exams will be insufficient.

 

4.11     The Dean & Director said that break out rooms and cafeteria are also used by the students so there would be no seating capacity issue.  

 

4.12     For the classrooms, the Dean & Director suggested that horse-shoe seating arrangements be made.  He also suggested to make some variations in the classroom size to cater to different needs e.g. some may have the capacity of 45 students, some of 30 and so on.  The configuration of classrooms of all the three floors will be mixed one.  Similarly, he said that faculty rooms will also be of different sizes.  There should be five shared rooms for Lecturers (two in one room), 20 single rooms for Assistant Professors, 10 for Associate Professors, and five for Professors.  He suggested to increase the size from 16 to 20 in the Conference Room by removing small rooms. He also suggested to find space for the indoor games and gym.  He said that the size of the Executive Dining Room may be reduced to accommodate indoor games and gym. The Registrar said that the size of the Conference Room can also be reduced to find space for Gym.     

 

4.13     Mr. Lotia said that he would find solutions to all the points raised above.

 

4.14     The Dean & Director wanted to know the time line for completion of the project.

 

4.15     Mr. Lotia said that after the finalization of tender process which according to the Director Projects would take 8 to 10 weeks, they would complete the projects in 30 months time. He hoped that the ground breaking will be done in October this year so after that 30 months would be required to complete the project.

 

4.16     Mr. Mobin Khalili emphasized the need of proper Store Room. He wanted to know whether the elevators will be able to handle the rush of students, visitors, and guests to carry them to the Seminar Halls at the 12th Floor in a short span of time.

 

4.17     The Dean & Director asked the Consultant to find some space in the basement for stores.

 

4.18     The Consultant reported that there would be 5 elevators; each will have a capacity of 16 passengers and should be sufficient to handle the rush of passengers.

 

4.19     The Dean & Director said that at this stage we should see the integrated plan where we are surplus and where we are short of.  If there are classrooms and other facilities available for the programs we are planning to introduce.  He asked Mr. Khalili and Mr. Rehanul Ambia to jointly analyse the requirements of classrooms, common areas, sports facilities etc. so that, based on the findings, if any changes are needed in the Aman Tower they may be done at this stage.

 

DECISION

 

4.20     The presentation on Interior Decoration Planning of all the floors of Aman Tower as presented by Mr. Hasnain Lotia along with the suggestions made by the EC members were noted and approved.

 

4.21     The Committee decided that except the seminar rooms which will have a flat facade, all the floors will have windows.

 

ACTION

 

4.22     The Consultant will try to incorporate in his interior design all the suggestions noted above.

 

4.23     Mr. Mobin Khalili along with the Director Projects would analyse the existing and future requirements of classrooms and other facilities keeping in view the new programs to be introduced and find out where we will have surplus arrangements and where we are short of facilities according to the proposed plan and inform the Consultant to make adjustment accordingly.

 

4.24     The Director Projects would request Mr. Izhar Husain Mirza, Director CEE to provide his requirements for the CEE at Aman Tower and forward them to the Consultant for incorporation.
