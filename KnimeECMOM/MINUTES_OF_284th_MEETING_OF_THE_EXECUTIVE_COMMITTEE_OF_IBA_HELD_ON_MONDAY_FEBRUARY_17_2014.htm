 1.2    Mr. Moeid Sultan said that if we specify brand then we can get the product directly from the manufacturer and we may get it at ex-factory price which would be lower than the market rate.

1.3    Mr. Mobin Khalili said that brand is specified when only one company meets our requirements. But if there are different companies manufacturing products of the same specifications then we should approach all such companies who meet our specifications. The Registrar said that on a single source we have to approach that particular company. Mr. Mobin Khalili said that there is no need of single source when the products of the same specifications are manufactured by different companies. The Dean & Director said that if there is any competition then we can pre-qualify the manufacturers. He said that the items listed above are for maintenance and replacement purposes.

1.4    Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed said that for electric cables, Pakistan Cables has been recommended. The Director Projects said that Newage is also better. Mr. Khalid Pervez suggested to prequalify Pioneer and Universal as they are also equally good. The Dean & Director said that if there is a compatibility issue with the existing cable of Pakistan Cables then we can go for Pakistan Cables; in other cases tender be issued to all the four prequalified companies.

1.5    The Director Projects suggested to keep some stock of cables of various mili meters to meet any emergency requirement. The Dean & Director agreed with the Director Projects and advised Mr. Mushtaque to identify the need and anticipate the annual requirement and start procurement of cables from the pre-qualified companies. He asked Mr. Mushtaque to follow the basic principle of value for money. He also advised the Registrar to revisit our procurement process so that every now and then purchases are not made.

1.6    Electrical Fittings: Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed said that for electrical fittings Clipsal/ Schneider (both are the same Company) has been recommended which is the single source. The electrical fittings would be required for replacement purposes.

DECISION

1.7    The Committee approved the proposal of Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed for brand names of Philips Pakistan, and Clipsal/Schneider for items listed at Sr. Nos. 1 & 3 of the proposal.

1.8    For Electric Cables, the Committee decided to prequalify the four; Pakistan Cables, Newage, Pioneer Cables, and Universal and issue tender to them.

1.9    The Committee further decided that to meet any emergency requirement, Manager Operations and Maintenance should procure and keep in stock electric cables of various sizes anticipating the annual demand.


Item No.2 To consider and approve the Integrated Fire Alarm system based on reduced cost.

2.1    The Director Projects stated that in the Executive Committee meeting held on 13th January 2014, M/s KP Associates had presented an integrated system design and cost of the fire alarm system for entire buildings of Main Campus which was based on converting all conventional Fire Alarm Panels into addressable ones in which case the additional cost was coming as Rs.2.26 million. He said that in this system the Central Panel could show the Building under fire as well as the location within the building. He said that the EC had decided that Consultants should work out an economical solution. The EC had further advised that the new options would be scrutinized by Mr. Khalid Javed Rishi and Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed. Finally Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed and the Director Projects would send their recommendations to EC for its consideration and approval.

2.2    The Director Projects reported that M/s KP Associates submitted their revised proposal on 8th February to which both Mr. Khalid Rishi and Mr. Mushtaque have agreed. He presented the revised proposal. He said that with this revised proposal the net cost increase will now be Rs.0.581 million instead of Rs.2.26 million. In the new system the Central panel will show the particular building where fire is indicated but not the exact location within the building. However, the building’s own panels will show the location. The Director Projects requested the Executive Committee to consider and approve the revised system as proposed by the consultants.

2.3    The Dean & Director thanked Mr. Khalid Pervez for showing flexibility and suggesting a more economical solution. Mr. Khalid Pervez suggested that fire safety audit for the campus should be carried out at IBA. The Dean & Director asked Mr. Khalid Pervez if they can do the fire safety audit. Mr. Khalid Pervez agreed to do fire safety audit for IBA. The Registrar was requested to coordinate with Mr. Khalid Pervez for the fire safety audit.

DECISION

2.4    The Committee approved the Integrated Fire Alarm system which is based on reduced cost of Rs.0.581 million.

ACTION

2.5    The Registrar would coordinate with Mr. Khalid Pervez of KP Associates for fire safety audit at the Campus.


Item No. 3 Proposal for monument garden.

3.1    The Director Projects said that in earlier meeting the Executive Committee was informed that due to various amendments in the External amendments we would be saving considerable amount which can be utilized to complete the front area of the Administration Building including the flag area pavements and the Monument Garden. He said that in the original presentation the Architects had proposed names of graduating classes. He requested the Executive Committee to finalize as to the inscribing the names of any one of the following categories:

Distinguished Alumni
Names of graduating class of -----years
Students winning medals, awards and positions

3.2    The Registrar said that he has searched on the internet various monument gardens. He suggested that there should be photo space also in the garden. He said that he would forward the various designs of the monument garden that he has downloaded from internet to the Director Projects.

3.3    Mr. Mobin Khalili said that the proposed monument (pillars) do not give a good look; it resembles yaadyar-e-shohda. He further said that inscribing names of all graduating classes would require much space. He suggested that names of gold medallists should only be inscribed. The Dean & Director agreed that instead of graduating classes, the names of gold medallists and students on Dean’s List of each class should be inscribed. The Registrar suggested that at the front arcade of the Library the names can be inscribed. The Committee, after the meeting, visited the place proposed by the Registrar.

DECISION

3.4    The Committee decided that the names of gold medalists only would be inscribed.

3.5    The Committee did not approve the proposed pillars for inscribing the names.

ACTION

3.6    The Registrar would send the designs of the monument gardens to the Director Projects for onward transmission to the Consultant.


Item No.4 Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

a) Designated person for each facility.

4.1    The Dean & Director said that the two Project Departments would gradually be wound up. He advised Mr. Mushtaque Ahmed and the Registrar to identify the skill gaps in the Maintenance Department and see if any of the Projects Department or HEC Department staff can fill in those gaps. He further suggested to the Registrar to designate a person for every facility such as sewerage, bulk water supply, electric supply etc. so that our facilities are used in a systematic manner.

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
