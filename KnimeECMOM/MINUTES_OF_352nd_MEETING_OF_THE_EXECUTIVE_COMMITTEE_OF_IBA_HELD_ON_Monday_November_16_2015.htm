The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       Follow up on the proposal of charging students for printing:

 

1.1       The members were reminded of the suggestion that was made in the 348th meeting of Executive Committee held on September 14, 2015 for charging students for printing.  It was decided in that meeting that Mr. Sharaf Hussain would obtain and present to the EC data of printing at each lab and its cost on toners.

 

1.2       Mr. Sharaf Hussain presented lab printing record for one year - from October 2014 to September 2015 - of both the campuses which showed that the total number of pages printed during the period was 278,022.  The highest number of printing at City Campus was recorded at CFL1, Admin Block i.e. 58700 pages including 874 color printing in a year and on an average 4965 pages per month. Similarly at Main Campus MTL4 Tabba Academic Block, 88156 pages including 1031 color printing were printed during the year; the average printing in a month of this lab was 7346.  

 

1.3       Mr. Sharaf said that there is no quota for students to print number of copies. He said that although the students bring their own paper for the print out, IBA has to bear the cost of toners. The Dean & Director suggested that the students be asked that instead of taking print out, they should upload their work on the LMS.  Mr. Batada said that most of the faculty members require students to submit their assignments and reports in print out. He suggested that the faculty should be requested not to ask for the assignments and report in hard form.  The Dean & Director asked as to what is the total cost on toners.  Mr. Batada said that it is between Rs.4.5 million to Rs.5 million for the year.

 

1.4       The Dean & Director said that we have to give a road map, say for example, by next semester i.e. Spring 2016 we will cut down the use of papers as much as possible and by Fall 2016 there would be no printing – everything has to be uploaded on the LMS and used from there both by the faculty and the students. He said that we may tell the students that in next semester each student will have a quota of x number of pages beyond that they cannot print. There should be an alert message on reaching the allotted quote.  All the printers should be interlinked and there should be a software to keep track of the quota of each student and to stop printing when quota is fully used.

 

1.5       Mr. Imran Batada said that they have the centralized system for printing and if a student prints papers from any printer installed in the campuses it would be centrally recorded and printing would be stopped if the allotted quota has been used. However, he suggested to place cap on number of pages as well as on the amount because different printing entails different costs.

 

1.6       The Dean & Director asked as to what is the total cost of printing of 278022 pages.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that it would cost around Rs.1 million. The Dean & Director said that out of Rs.5 million that we are spending on toners annually, Rs.1 million on labs is not a big component. However, he said that we have to emphasize on going for paperless system at IBA for the cause of environmental sustainability and to increase the use of LMS on which we have made huge investment. He suggested to have a break down of Rs.5 million between Administration and Academic use. He said that if there is any misuse in the administration, it would be responsibility of the HoD to control it.

 

1.7       Mr. Abdul Wajed suggested that instead of having so many printers, we should have centralized printers barring the confidential printing for which there should be a separate printer. The Dean & Director agreed with Mr. Abdul Wajed and asked Mr. Imran Batada and Mr. Sharaf Hussain to carry out a survey that if we can centralize the common services such as printing, fax machine and scanner on each floor and a person should be made incharge of it.  He asked the Associate Deans to suggest the number of copies to be allowed to print to faculty and the students. Dr. Sayeed Ghani requested Mr. Sharaf to provide faculty members printing details and its associated cost.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.8       The Associate Deans would request the faculty members that for the sake of environmental sustainability, reduction in printing cost and to increase the use of LMS they should encourage their students to upload their assignments and reports on LMS instead of print out.

 

1.9       Mr. Imran Batada and Mr. Sharaf Hussain would carry out a survey on making available centralized common services such as printing, fax machine and scanner on each floor and making a person responsible for such services.

 

1.10     The Associate Deans would suggest the number of copies / cost to be allowed to print to faculty and the students. Mr. Sharaf would provide faculty members printing details and its associated cost to the Associate Deans.

 

Item No.2       Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Change of date of Convocation 2015.

 

2.1       The Registrar said that earlier the Convocation 2015 was planned for December 5, 2015. However, due to public holiday on account of Local Bodies Elections on December 5, he suggested that the Convocation be held either on Sunday, December 6, 2015 or on any other date. It was observed that Sunday is most suitable for the parents of the graduating students to attend the Convocation.

 

2.2       The Dean & Director requested the Registrar to send a formal invitation to the Honourable Governor of Sindh for the Convocation.  He also requested the Associate Deans to identify the best teachers and researchers.

 

2.3       The Dean & Director asked Mr. Adnan Hameed to request the Grievance Committee to give go ahead on the EC’s recommendation for Rewards and Recognition so that the recommended rewards could be given on the Convocation, and if there is any modification the GC should inform him.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.4       The EC decided that in view of public holiday on December 5 due to Local Bodies Elections, the Convocation would be held on Sunday, December 6, 2015.

 

2.5       The Registrar would send a formal invitation for the Convocation to the Honourable Governor of Sindh. The Associate Deans would identify the best teachers and researchers on the basis of two semesters evaluation / publication on top journals or ISI Indexed journals.

 

2.6       Mr. Adnan Hameed on behalf of the EC would request the Grievance Committee to give go ahead on the EC’s recommendation for Rewards and Recognition so that the recommended rewards could be given on the Convocation, and if there is any modification GC should inform the Dean & Director accordingly.

 

b)  Conversion of Faculty Lounge into Students Lab.   

 

2.7       The Dean & Director suggested to convert faculty lounge into students lab. Mr. Sharaf Hussain said that since the faculty lounge is very large where 60 computers can be accommodated, it should be made a general purpose lab. The Dean & Director suggested to consult Dr. Shakil Khoja and Dr. Sayeed Ghani in this regard.

 

c)  Course Substitution – Delegation of Authority

        

2.8      Mr. Abdul Wajed said that some students do extra courses which are treated as non-credit courses and sometimes students get the elective replaced with another elective in order to improve their CGPA.  He said that such approvals are given by the Associate Deans at the final semester. He requested that this authority be delegated to the Controller of Examinations.

 

2.9       The Dean & Director said that the substitution is done all over the world and is a normal practice. He requested the Associate Deans to delegate the substitution authority to the Controller of Examinations.  The Associate Deans agree.

 

DECISION

 

2.10     The EC authorized the delegation of course substitution authority from Associate Deans to the Controller of Examinations.

 

d)   Students Access to Fee Ledger.

 

2.11     Mr. Moeid Sultan said that earlier the students did not have the access to the fee ledger which has now been allowed. He said that henceforth when the fee is charged, an email would be sent to the student to check it for reconciliation purposes. The Dean & Director advised to make SMS alert if the fee is not received on due date and the second step should be to send SMS alert to the parents if the student does not respond. 

 

e)   Charging of Degree Fees for various programs.

 

2.12     Mr. Abdul Wajed suggested that degree fee should be added in the last semester fee. The Committee agreed with Mr. Abdul Wajed that degree fee should be added and charged at the last semester.  

 

2.13     Mr. Abdul Wajed further said that the approved fee of MS, and M.Phil degrees is equal to PhD degree which is Rs.10,000/- and for MBA degree the approve fee is Rs.6,000/-  He suggested that the degree fee of all Masters program should be the same. Similarly the fee of all PhD degrees should be the same.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

2.14     The Committee decided that the degree fees would added and charged in the last semester.

 

2.15     The Committee also decided that the fee of all Masters degree programs should be equivalent i.e. Rs.6,000/-, and fee of all PhD programs should be the same i.e. Rs.10,000/-.

 

2.16     The Director Finance would get the above decisions implemented.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
