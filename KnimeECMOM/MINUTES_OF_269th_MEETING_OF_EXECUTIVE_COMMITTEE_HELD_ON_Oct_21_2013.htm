MINUTES OF 269TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD

ON MONDAY, OCTOBER 21, 2013

 

The 269th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, October 21, 2013 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                                           Dean & Director

2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                                                     Associate Dean, FBA

3.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                                           Associate Dean, FCS

4.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                                        Registrar

5.      Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                                               Director Projects

6.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                            Director Finance

7.      Ayesha A. Menai                                                            Director H.R.

8.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                           Acting Head of ICT

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No. 1      To review and finalize the Convocation arrangements.

 

1.1       The Registrar up-dated the EC on the arrangements made for the Convocation being held on December 7, 2013. He said that there is a demand from the graduating students that they be individually allowed to get snaps with the Chief Guest while receiving the degrees. The Dean & Director said that there are around 500 graduating students who would be receiving their degrees. Allowing them to get photograph individually would require at least 3 to 4 additional hours which will tax the patience of the Chief Guest and other invited guests.

 

1.2       The Registrar said that every year they use a slogan in the Convocation.  For the coming Convocation, he proposed the slogan of “Aspiring for a Diversified Future”. He requested the EC to approve the proposed slogan, if deemed appropriate.  The Director Projects suggested to see if the word “Excellence” can also be added somewhere to the proposed slogan. The Registrar said that he would think about it. 

 

1.3       The Registrar reported that the consent on the proposed award of Honorary Degree at the Convocation has not yet been received from the Governor’s House. The Dean & Director said that he would talk to the Honourable Governor.

 

1.4       The Dean & Director asked the Registrar to show him the list of invitees for the Convocation. He suggested that after he approves the list, the invitees should be informed in advance telling them that proper invitation card would be issued after the security clearance.

 

Item No. 2      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Allocation of space for Centre for Writing.

 

2.1       The Registrar said that there are two options for locating the Centre for Writing; either it can be established at the Library or at the Students’ Centre.  He said that at the Students Centre would be more suitable for the Centre as all the required facilities are available over there.

 

DECISION

 

2.2       The Committee agreed with the Registrar that the Centre for Writing should be located at the Students Centre.

 

ACTION

 

2.3       The Registrar would inform Dr. Framji and Ms. Maria Hasan accordingly.

 

b)     Task list of teaching assistants (TAs).

 

2.4       The Dean & Director said that there should be a task list for each Teaching Assistant. He said that one of the purposes of TAs is to give tutorial to the weaker students thereby improving the overall performance of the class. The performance of the weaker student in the final exam will reflect upon the performance appraisal of the TA. He asked Mr. Imran Batada to develop a system through which each TA could be given the task and its monitoring can be done.          

 

DECISON & ACTION

 

2.5       Mr. Imran Batada would develop a system through which each Teaching Assistant could be given the task and its monitoring can be done.

 

c)      Permission for one lab for Macintosh.

 

2.6       Mr. Imran Batada suggested to make one lab available for Macintosh for 20 students at Lab 16.  The Director Finance when asked about the budget for this lab, Mr. Batada said that there is no separate budget for this. However, he said that there were some savings from other heads which could be utilized for the proposed Macintosh lab.

 

DECISION

 

2.7       The Committee approved the proposal for one lab for Macintosh as presented by Mr. Imran Batada.

 

d)     Nomination of Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz to the MJS-IBA Project Committee.

 

2.8       The Director Finance informed that Mr. Zafar Siddiqui is no more the Director of CED as such he needed to know if  his substitution on the MJS-IBA Project Committee is to be nominated. The Dean and Director informed that as Mr. Zafar Siddiqui is still an employee of IBA, he will continue to remain on the Committee.

 

2.9       The Director Finance further informed that the Director Projects has agreed to rejoin the MJS-IBA Project Committee from IBA side and as such his nomination may be approved. He said that earlier the Director Projects had resigned from the membership of the Committee because of the load of work on multiple projects.

 

DECISION

 

2.10     The Dean and Director approved the nomination of Director Projects and advised the Director Finance to issue the letter to the MJS Foundation as per the terms of the signed agreement between IBA and MJS Foundation.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
