Mr. Mahmood Ali, Senior Manager Projects, Mr. Amin Ashraf Qureshi, Manager Projects, and Mr. Imran Mumtaz of NBCL attended the meeting on special invitation.

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1       To consider and approve the Design and Cost Estimate of Gate No.4 at IBA

                    Main Campus.

 

1.1       Mr. Mahmood Ali said that the design and cost estimate of Gate No.4 of IBA Main Campus was earlier presented in the 330th meeting of Executive Committee. Then the Executive Committee had suggested to re-submit the revised drawings and cost estimate. Accordingly he presented the revised design of Gate No.4. The following has been provided with Gate No.4:

 

-  Guard Room

-  Security Check Room

-  Storage Room

-  Entry / Exit Gate

 

1.2       The Registrar asked as to what would be the arrangement for traffic movement during the construction of Gate No.4. Mr. Mahmood Ali said that for traffics movement another gate will have to be used. The Registrar suggested to start the work in Summer (June) so that there is lesser traffic movement during this period.  He also suggested to provide a window in the proposed design and make the green patch round for smooth flow of traffics.

 

1.3       Dr. Muhammad Nishat said that he was concerned on the congestion on the gate because of security check and barriers. Mr. Imran Mumtaz said that the hydraulic barrier would be placed near the canteen because of which the queue outside the gate will not be so long. The Registrar briefed about the arrangements of physical and sticker checks of the vehicles and the smooth flow of vehicles.

1.5       The Registrar said that the cost estimates of Electrical Works and Façade were very high. He suggested to reduce the cost. He further said that no lighting arrangement in the design either on the ground or on the top is visible. Mr. Imran Mumtaz confirmed that lightings have been provided.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani asked as to what was the earlier cost estimate. Mr. Imran Mumtaz said that earlier it was Rs.5.276 million. Mr. Moeid Sultan suggested to review the BoQ items and reduce the cost estimate as much as possible without making any compromise on the design and quality.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.6       The Committee approved the design of Gate No.4 as presented by Mr. Mahmood Ali with the minor changes i.e. placing a window and making the green patch round as suggested by the Registrar. 

 

1.7       The Committee approved the cost estimate. However, the Architect would review the BoQ items and try to reduce the cost estimate if possible.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
