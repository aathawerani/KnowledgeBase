The following items were discussed:

Item No.1 To consider and approve revised design and cost estimate of the Transport Facilities Building at Main Campus.

1.1 The Director Projects reported that originally the Transport Facilities Building was designed incorporating the following requirements:

      1. Bus /vehicle Service Station
      2. Store for new parts
      3. Store for old parts
      4. Drivers’ waiting room
      5. Bathrooms
      6. Duty Officer room
      7. Lobby

1.2 The Director Projects said that the area was 2936 Sft and the cost was Rs.12 million (Approx. Rs 4150 Sft.). However, the Architects were asked to redesign to cut down the cost. The second revision was based on an area of 2880 Sft costing Rs.8.0 million. This was not accepted by the Executive Committee and the Architects were asked to again redesign the building with simple layouts and finishes within a budget of Rs.5.0 million.

1.3 The Director Projects reported that they have received the newly revised design which he showed to the EC. He said that the revised area was 1567 Sft and the estimated cost was Rs.5.0 million (Rs.3200 per Sft.). Mr. Shabih presented the breakup of the estimated cost and said that the cost may further be reduced when the bids are received, specially on electrical side the expense may be slightly less than what has been estimated. The Director Projects clarified that the requirements have not been changed except that the room sizes have been reduced and that the lobby has been removed. He requested the Executive Committee to approve the design and the cost estimate.

1.4 The Registrar asked if there was standard pit and high pressure water and air pumps. Mr. Shabih confirmed that there would be standard pit and the pressure pump has been included in plumbing work. The Director Projects said that compressor was not included in the BoQ. He requested Mr. Shabih to include compressor in the BoQ.

1.5 The Registrar suggested that the POL stores should be separate from spare parts stores. The Director Projects said that they can combine the bathroom and shower together and convert one of the bath rooms into POL stores room. The Registrar further suggested that a window (fixed glass) be given between the duty officer and the drivers room.

DECISION

1.6 The Committee approved the design and cost estimate along with the suggestions noted above.

1.7 Mr. Shabih of NBCL would get the suggestions noted above incorporated in the design and BoQ of Transport Facilities Building at Main Campus.


Item No.2 To consider and approve relocation of the Clinic Building to its original plot at Main Campus.

2.1 The Director Projects stated that the Executive Committee had decided to relocate the Clinic from its original location from the front of the Students’ Center to the plot behind it. He said that the EC had advised him to obtain advice of the Architect whether they could use the plot in front of the Students’ Center for parking or should they develop it as a green area. He reported that the Architects have given the following advice:

According to the Architect the areas of the plots are as follows:

      IBA Clinic Plot Area : 8,348 SFT
      Plot Behind Students Center: 14,402 SFT

2.2 Considering the logic of the Architect, it was recommended that the Clinic be built on the plot already reserved for it.

2.3 The Dean & Director being out of city could not attend the meeting. However, he had emailed his views which were as under:

"We are not going to utilize the entire plot behind the ASC but only a portion on one side to build the clinic. The right orientation on that plot will be decided by the architect.
I took Mr. Tariq Wajid, CEO of Martin Dow around the two sites and he agrees that the present location would block the view of the expanse of our Sports complex while the clinic adjoining the ASC on the rear side plot would be a better option. I would therefore vote in favor of the earlier decision taken by the EC."

2.4 The Registrar suggested that instead of constructing the Clinic in the centre of the plot behind the Students Centre, it should be located at one side of the plot and its opening should be towards the West.

DECISION

2.5 The Committee decided that the Clinic be constructed on the plot behind the Students Centre as was decided in the earlier EC meeting. The clinic should be sited at one side of the plot having its opening towards the West.

2.6 The Director Projects would convey the decision of the Executive Committee to NBCL.

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
