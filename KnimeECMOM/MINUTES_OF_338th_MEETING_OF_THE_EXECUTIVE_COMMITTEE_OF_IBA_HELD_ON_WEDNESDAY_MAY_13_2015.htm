The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1      To consider draft IBA Budget 2015-16.

                   

1.1     Mr. Syed Fahad Jawed presented the overview of the draft IBA Budget 2015-16.  He also presented the proposed budget of each profit centres, EMBA program, and Library.  The following comments / observations were made:

 
o    The Dean & Director suggested that out of the proceeds of Rs.7 million from the concert of Rahat Fatah Ali Rs.5 million be allocated to the Endowment Fund and Rs.2 million to the Scholarship fund to cater to the short fall of HEC Scholarship Fund.  He said that they have to find new donors for Scholarships.

 
o    The Registrar was advised to control utility bills specially electricity. Each building incharge should be given the target to reduce electricity bills and one of the factors of their performance evaluation would be based on savings achieved in electricity units consumption.   

o    Mr. Imran Batada was advised to reduce licensing fees on hardwares and softwares and to re-arrange and re-allocate Rs.25 million on computers and peripherals.

o    Dr. M. Shahid Qureshi was advised to step up marketing efforts to increase the number of students in EPGD Family Business program and try to curtail the expenses.

 
o    Mr. Moeid Sultan was advised to pursue the recovery of outstanding amount of Rs.6 million from Sindh Government on account of services rendered by CED and send someone from Finance Department to Sindh Government to pursue the case.

 
o    The Dean & Director suggested that the 30% of the Consultancy Fees earned by the faculty memebrs should be reflected in the budget.  (The available information on Consultancy Projects should be documented and reflected in the budget.)  Dr. Qazi Masood Ahmed was advised to get a survey done on the running consultancy projects and report to the Finance Department. The information should also be made available on the CBER-IBA Website as it will have positive impact on HEC’s ranking of IBA.

 
o    The Dean & Director suggested that the budget of MBA (Tax Management) should be shown separately for the sake of transparency.  He clarified that the revenues from MBA (Tax Management) and EMBA should be shown separately.  Similarly the proportionate expenses for each program should be shown separately. However, the students of both the programs will attend the classes together if the course requirement is the same.

 
o    It was suggested that creation of departmental libraries can be considered at a later stage.

  

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
