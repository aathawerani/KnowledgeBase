MINUTES OF 264TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 9, 2013

 

The 264th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, September 9, 2013 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                                            Acting Director

2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                                                      Associate Dean, FBA

3.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                                         Registrar

4.      Mr. Mobin Khalili                                                              Controller of Examinations

5.      Engr. Rehanul Ambia Riaz                                                  Director Projects

6.      Mr. Moeid Sultan                                                             Director Finance

7.      Zafar A. Siddiqui                                                             Director CED

8.      Mr. Imran Batada                                                            Acting Head of ICT

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No. 1      Status Report from:

 

Director Projects on time bound projects.

 

1.1       Completion of additional works and operation of Students’ Centre project till August 30, 2013: The Director Projects reported that all actions have been successfully completed.

 

1.2       The generator of Data Centre to be used for Students’ Centre till such time the power is received from KESC: The Director Projects reported that procurement of DG set for Data Centre is in advance stages as BOQ item. He said that increase in cost (Rs.1.2 million) will have to be paid because of price increases by the Manufacturers. The procurement of the DG set was kept on hold due to re-engineering and later on Design changes and hold on the decision on Data Centre.  He said that till July 2013 they were not aware that the Data Centre will not be made at the Main Campus. He said that they would not pay the contractors their OH and Profit on incremental portion of the cost. He said that after negotiation with the authorised dealers management (SM Jaffer) they have been able to get only Rs.50,000 rebate. He said that market prices were also checked for other brands. They were also found equal or more. He said that the Project Managers (Nespak) and Electrical Consultants were fully involved in the whole process and have recommended the final purchase price. He clarified that the increase in cost will not impact the project cost as savings of about Rs.4.00 million are expected to be realized from other technical decisions. He presented the following price adjustment details:

 

-          Quoted BOQ price on 29/2/2012                     Rs.4,250,000.00

-          Latest quoted price on 15/8/2013                   Rs.5,497,311.00

-          Difference             =                                  (Rs.1,247,311.00)

 

He said that the following are reasons for the increase in price:

 

1)         Change in income tax by 1% :  Impact:                      Rs.     87,311/-

 

2)         Price increment from FG Wilson

Price increment on basic cost from Principal

in 1.5 years Rs.4,900,000 - 3,740,000   =       Rs.1,160,000/- ( i.e. 31.0%)

 

3)         20% OH and Profit on difference in cost

Rs.232,000. ( NOT being paid to Contractors). Impact is 0.0%

 

Overall impact on Project       =          1.0%

 

Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know as to why there is almost 30% increase in the basic price. The Director Projects said that the price increased over a period of 1½ years during this period international prices have increased.  He said that he had verified the rate from other sources also.

 

Mr. Imran Batada said that as they are moving to cloud, there will be no server at Main Campus. Some of the servers would be moved to City Campus and some will move to the place from where they would obtain the services. The Registrar said that earlier it was planned to have a control room to monitor the system of fire panel at the data centre.  He suggested that the Control Room be shifted to the Admin. Building. The Director Projects said that he would do that.  

 

1.3       The project management work of the Students’ Centre to be taken care of by Mr. Amin Ashraf Qureshi: The Director Projects reported that action was completed and notice was served to the Project Management in time. However, Project Managers Services were used for the testing and commissioning of HVAC plant as its installation was done under their supervision as Projects department did not have the HVAC expertise.  He said that they have not paid anything to the Project Managers.

 

1.4       The Committee allowed time extension for construction works for Sports Ground and ancillary works at Main Campus till August 31, 2013: The Director Projects reported that actions have been taken. All the sports facilities are ready for handover. He said that Mr. Asad Ilyas has said that he would take over all the facilities and the Registrar would dedicate a team who will look after it.

 

Status Reports from Associate Deans:

 

1.5       Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that he would present it in the next EC meeting when the Dean & Director would also be present.

 

DECISION

 

1.6       The up-dates given above were noted and approved.

 

1.7       The Executive Committee approved the increase in the cost of the generator for Data Centre based on the reasons explained by the Director Projects and advised that that the Generator be procured at an early date to make the Students’ Centre fully functional.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
