Mr. Kumail Raza Hemani, Program Office Manager, and Mr. Adnan Hameed, H.R. Manager attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

 

Item No. 1      Briefing from Mr. Kumail Raza Hemani, Manager Program Office and IRC with regard to his visit to Schools in Dubai.

 

1.1       Mr. Kumail Raza Hemani, Manager Program Office and IRC reported that he visited three schools; one in Dubai and two in Abu Dhabi. He met the Students Counsellor / Career Advisors of those schools and then he had meetings with the students of the graduating batch. He said that the students were looking for universities abroad as all of them were well off and nobody asked for financial assistance. Many questions in relation to the academic programs, their linkages and collaborations especially in relation to Babson were asked by the students. He said that after group discussions, four or five students belonging to Pakistan and India came to him. Their main concern was the security situation in Karachi.  One of their suggestions was to lower down the SAT Score or conduct admission test in Dubai also.  He said that he did not have meetings with the parents of the students.

 

1.2       The Dean & Director said that IBA is better and cheaper than the universities those students would be planning to go. He said that the students should be told that we have financial assistance also.  He asked Mr. Imran Batada to work on on-line admission tests.  Mr. Batada said that it can be done but a large number of work stations would be required for that. The Registrar said that it can be done on a fixed date, time and place. However, he said that the disadvantage of on-line admission is that if a student uses unfair means, we cannot control that. The Dean & Director said that we can use our alumni chapter to organize it. Dr. Sayeed Ghani said that tests may be taken on different times so we will have to develop data base of questions. The Dean & Director asked Mr. Batada to computerise the existing questions data bank. To a question the Dean & Director said that we cannot have partnership with British Council in Dubai due to conflict of interest as they would be interested in sending the students to the universities in U.K. whereas we would like them to come to IBA. He asked the Registrar to have a meeting with the Alumni Chapter in Dubai in this regard.

 

DECISION

 

1.3       The Committee noted the up-dates as given by Mr. Kumail Raza.

 

ACTION

 

1.4       Mr. Imran Batada would work on on-line admission tests and computerise the existing questions data bank.

 

1.5       The Registrar would have a meeting with the Alumni Chapter in Dubai and discuss the possibility of arranging on-line admission tests.

Graduate Program Office:

 

3.2       Mr. Adnan Hameed said that in the previous meeting the recommendation for up-gradation of the position of Senior Assistant (Evening MBA Program, Main Campus) was not accepted because it was reported that there are only 50 students enrolled in the Evening Program at the Main Campus. He said that Dr. Nasir Afghan has reported that the MBA Evening Program, Main Campus is currently managing approximately 225 students (50 students enrolled and 150+ from other programs enrolled at Main Campus. 

 

The Dean & Director said that for Morning Program there is a separate office which does all the work. The students just come and take classes. He said that if the work load is increased next year, we will revisit this case after seeing the enrolment of Fall 2013 and Spring 2014.

 

DECISION

 

3.3       The Committee decided to revisit this case if the workload is increased next year.

 

Undergraduate Program Office:

 

3.4       Mr. Adnan Hameed informed the Committee that last year the Executive Committee had approved up-gradation of two Assistant level positions (one for Main and one for City Campus) to Executive level but the selection on Main Campus position was deferred by the Promotion Committee. 

 

DECISION

 

3.5       The Committee observed that since the up-gradation of the position of Assistant at the undergraduate program office at Main Campus has already been approved by the EC last year, the H.R. may initiate the process of up-gradation of this position. 

 

Library:

 

3.6       Mr. Adnan Hameed informed the Committee that the Librarian has recommended promotion of Assistant Librarian (I) in Job Band 1(i) to Assistant Librarian (II) Job Band 1(ii).  He said that the incumbent Ms. Nusrat Jabeen has been working in our Library since 2004. She is a qualified Library Science professional and handles technical services in Collection Services Unit. The proposal is to acknowledge seniority of the incumbent and streamline the structure through internal equity.

 

DECISION

 

3.7       The Committee approved the recommendation for promotion of Ms. Nusrat Jabeen from Assistant Librarian (i) to Assistant Librarian (ii). As it is a seniority case, the incumbent will be promoted directly without going through the process of promotion / up-gradation.  

 

Centre for Executive Education:

 

3.8       Mr. Adnan Hameed reported that Mirza Izhar Husain, Director CEE has recommended that the position of Assistant Manager (Business Development) – CEE be up-gradated to the level of Manager (Business Development). He said that as Mirza Izhar had to go to Islamabad, so he could not join the EC meeting. He said that the justifications for up-gradation as given by Mirza Izhar are that the incumbent played a vital role in IT related assignments such as CEE portal design, on-line testing, on-line surveys, etc. and successfully getting collaborations with internationally recognized bodies such as PMI(USA) & AHRD (USA). He informed the Committee that the incumbent was given cash award of Rs.30,000 in 2012.   

 

3.9       The Dean & Director said that he has plans to transform the Business English & Skills Development program and reorganize it under the name of “Ardeshir Cowasjee Centre”. It may either be administered independently or we may use Assistant Manager Business English & Skills Development Program. There may be two arms of this centre; Business English for outsiders and Writing Centre for our students specially the Social Sciences students because for them command over written English is very necessary. The Writing Centre will be working under a faculty and it will be taken away from the CEE. Part-time teachers will become Program Directors for CDC, Alumni Affairs, Public Affairs, and there will be managers for each program. He said that for CDC Mr. Leon Menezes will be the Program Director. However, he said that he is in the thinking stage and would welcome any idea from the EC.

 

DECISION

 

3.10     The Committee deferred the recommendation in light of the briefing given by the Dean & Director as noted in 3.9 above.

 

ACTION

 

3.11     Mr. Adnan Hameed would request Mirza Izhar Husain to talk to the Dean & Director and discuss the idea of transformation of Business English Centre.

 

ICT Department:

 

3.12     Mr. Imran Batada said that he has recommended that the incumbent working on the position of Database Administrator be promoted to the rank of Senior Data Base Administrator. He said that the incumbent has been working at ICT for the last three years.

 

3.13     The Dean & Director said that they would consider such cases after the incumbents have completed five years of service at IBA.

 

3.14     Mr. Imran Batada said that his second recommendation is for the up-gradation of the position of Senior Web Graphics Designer in J3(ii) to Senior Web Specialist in Job Band 1(ii).  Mr. Adnan Hameed informed the Committee that the incumbent was promoted to the next job band step in 2012.  

 

3.15     The Dean & Director said that since the incumbent has been promoted last year, we cannot consider the recommendation now.

 

DECISION

 

3.16     The Committee did not consider the recommendations for promotion / up-gradation of the two positions of ICT Department as they were premature.

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
