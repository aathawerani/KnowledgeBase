Item No. 6      To consider  and approve release of 10% Security Deposit to M/s. UNISYS for Data Storage.

 

6.1       The Director Finance reported that the vendor who had supplied EMC storage systems to IBA has requested for release of 10% security deposit and the Director ICT has also recommended to release the 10% Security Deposit on the basis of the following:

 

The Final Acceptance Test (FAT) could not be conducted till date due to lack of availability of Data Centre, being the responsibility of the IBA, and the equipment is being installed at a temporary location and is working flawlessly since last nine months.

 

6.2       However, the Director Finance reported that CPC in its meeting held on June 20, 2011 has given the decision for the release of security deposit of Rs.7.4 million subject to the following:

 

o   Furnish Bank Guarantee of ‘A’ rated bank only for a period approved by EXCO.

 

o   EXCO is requested to ask the concerned to provide holistic time-line & schedule of work to complete this project since its delay causes the risk both in terms of monetary & kind to the IBA

 

6.3       The Director Finance reported that the above CPC decision was made when the ICT Deptt.  referred the case to the CPC to change the terms of the contract which was signed between the vendor and the IBA, Karachi.

 

6.4       The Director Finance further reported that there is a performance bond which is in place for the period till 2013 but it only fulfils one of the conditions of the contract which should not be treated as collateral to release the security deposit amounting to Rs.7.4 million. In the light of the above he suggested the following to release the security deposit:

 

Either, as per the terms of contract PAT & FAT is signed and provided to IBA

OR

The bank guarantee is provided by the vendor amounting to Rs.7.4 million for a period acceptable to IBA as also advised by the CPC.

 

6.5       The Director Projects said that if the Director ICT is satisfied then he should sign the FAT so that 10% could be released to the vendor who is at no fault.  Moreover, he said that IBA has got Performance Bond valued at 10% of contract price which is valid for three years.

Item No. 8      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)      Provision of Pergola Structure for Academic & Admin Blocks at City Campus

 

8.1       Mr. Umer Makhdumi reported that the bridge canopy (Pergola structure) containing polycarbonated sheets over a length of around 275 Rft of Academic Block corridor was part of original refurbishment plan for Academic Block, City Campus. However, in order to economize the project, the plan for pergola was shelved by IBA management thereby saving the cost. He said that in the aftermath of recent rains, particularly after the rough spell of September 06, 2011, the necessity of providing and installing of pergola structure has been strongly felt so as to minimize water penetration into first floor as well as ground floor rooms.  The dirt and droppings of birds will also be greatly reduced as a result of installation of this canopy over Academic Block. Similarly, he said that a horizontal aluminium ribbed structure, projecting around 6.0 feet from the wall, is required for Admin Block at certain portions, at a height of around 12.0 ft from floor level, again to minimize chances of rain water entry into Ground Floor offices. He said that the tentative cost estimates will be sent for EC’s approval at a later stage after consultations with Architects and project managers. He requested the Executive Committee to give principle approval for the execution of aforementioned tasks of Academic Block pergola and ribbed horizontal aluminium structure for Admin Block.

 

 

DECISION

 

8.2       The Committee in principle approved the above mentioned proposal.
