MINUTES OF 307TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, AUGUST 4, 2014

The 307th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, August 4, 2014 at 9:30 a.m. at Breakout Room, Aman-CED Building, IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

1. Dr. Mohammad Nishat Associate Dean, FBA
2. Dr. Sayeed Ghani Associate Dean, FCS
3. Capt. (R) Ahmed Zaheer, PN Registrar
4. Mr. Moeid Sultan Director Finance
5. Engr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz Director Projects
6. Mr. Imran Batada Head of ICT Department
7. Mr. Adnan Hameed Head of HR

 

Mr. Ahmed Adeel Zia, Program Manager attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:   

 

Item No.1       To consider and approve the draft agreement for Greenhouse Project.

 

1.1       The Registrar said that the proposal for the Greenhouse Project at North Nazimabad Plot has already been approved by the EC in its 302nd meeting held on June 23, 2014. He informed the Committee that he got the required area of around 400 yards cleaned from shrubs and bushes. He further informed that he got the proprietary of IBA reinforced by redoing all the sign boards. He said that he had sent the draft agreement for the use of IBA land at North Nazimabad, by the IBA Centre for Entrepreneurship Development for Greenhouse Project to the EC for their perusal and comments, if any.  He requested the EC members to give their comments on the draft agreement. He clarified that the premises would be provided for the project to the CED as an extension of the CED without charging any rent. However, the utilities charges would be paid from the project’s fund. 

 

1.2       Dr. Sayeed Ghani wanted to know if Deutsche Bank – the sponsor of the project – will have any objection if, for any reason, the project is closed before the stipulated period.  Mr. Ahmed Adeel Zia clarified that Deutsche Bank want one cycle of crop to be completed.

 

1.3       Dr. Mohammad Nishat reiterated the earlier decision of EC that IBA can have a classroom or couple of classrooms and start outreach programs for the intermediate students of government colleges of surrounding areas such as New Karachi, North Karachi, F.B Area, Nazimabad etc., and can also start summer program for the catchment areas – students of Government schools, and colleges can be provided training there. The Registrar said that it is an extension of academic activities of the CED.        Mr. Moeid Sultan suggested that the plot should be named as “Entrepreneurial Research Site”.  The EC agreed with Mr. Moeid Sultan.

 

1.4       Dr. Mohammad Nishat suggested that the revenue to be generated through this project should be shared for the students’ scholarships. The Registrar suggested that commencing from the second crop 10% of the gross revenue be deposited to the IBA Students’ Welfare Fund.  

 

1.5       The Registrar to a question clarified that there is no financial involvement of IBA except as an intermediary.  Mr. Moeid Sultan said that the students may be advised to submit technical activity report at the end of project to CED / financial report to the Finance Department for information purpose only.

 

DECISION

 

1.6       The Committee approved the draft agreement for Greenhouse Project along with the suggestions noted above.  The Registrar would incorporate the suggestions noted above in the agreement.

 

Item No. 2      To consider request from the Mess Staff for Ramazan Allowance.

                          

2.1       Mr. Adnan Hameed reported that the mess staff have requested for Ramazan Allowance as is given to the Security Staff for not getting any relaxation in duty hours during Ramazan.  He said that IBA pays Ramazan Allowance of Rs.5000/- to the Supervisor and Rs.4,500/- to the Security Guards.  He recommended that the same amount should be approved for the mess staff (cooks, bearers etc.)  He clarified that mess staff are not paid any overtime. 

 

2.2       Mr. Adnan Hameed said that the Paish-e-Imam has also requested for the Ramazan Allowance as he has to lead Taraveeh Prayers. The Registrar said that for leading Taraveeh prayers the Paish-e-Imam is separately paid.     

 

DECISION

       

2.2       The Committee approved the proposed Ramazan Allowance to the Mess Staff only.

 

Item No. 3      Reporting Items:

 

a)         Implementation of Lecture Recording System.

 

3.1       Mr. Imran Batada reported that implementation of Lecture Recording System has been started. He said that the Registrar has allocated two rooms at Tabba Building, and three rooms have been allocated at the City Campus. He said that they would launch it for the up-coming semester.  He requested that faculty should be requested to volunteer to edit the recorded lectures for up-loading on the IBA Website.   

 

b)         Smart Board.

 

3.2       Mr. Imran Batada informed the Committee that they have started the process of procurement of five Smart Boards which would hopefully be received by the next week. He said that ideally the Lecture Recording System and the Smart Board should be together. However, the EC may decide if those are to be placed together or separately.

 

3.3       The Registrar observed that the Smart Board would cover the White Board which is extensively used by faculty.  Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested that three Smart Boards should be placed together with the Lecture Recording System (LRS) and two should be independent. The Registrar suggested that two Smart Boards along with LRS should be placed at FCS building, and one each at CEE, Tabba Building, and Aman-CED Building. 

 

3.4       Dr. Sayeed Ghani suggested that LRS and SB should be properly launched and for that Program offices and chairpersons should be involved. He suggested that an email to all chairpersons highlighting the features of LRS and SB should be sent by the Registrar in his capacity as Facilities Manager and it should be further enforced by the Associate Deans.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

3.5       The Committee decided that two Smart Boards along with LRS should be placed at FCS building, and one each at CEE, Tabba Building, and Aman-CED Building.

 

3.6       The Registrar would send an email to all chairpersons highlighting the features of LRS and SB which would further be enforced by the Associate Deans.

 

c)         Video Conferencing Mobile Equipment.

 

3.7       Mr. Imran Batada reported that Video Conferencing Mobile equipment has been received which have been temporarily kept at Academic Block.  He requested that a room either at Tabba or Aman-CED Building be provided for placing the equipment. He said that at City Campus they have the requisite space.  The Registrar said that he would look into it and find out suitable space for placing the equipment.

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
