Dr. Shakil Khoja, Mr. Abdul Rehman, Mr. Sharaf Husain, and Syed Wajeeh Zaidi attended the meeting on special invitation.

The following items were discussed:

 

Item No.1      To consider and approve the award of work for construction of Gate No.4 at Main Campus.
 

1.1     Mr. Abdul Rehman said that an advertisement for Single Stage two envelop bidding for construction of Gate No.4 was floated in daily newspapers on May 10, 2015.  He said that only the following two bidders submitted their bids. Scrutiny of their documents was done by the Consultant M/s Najmi Bilgrami Collaborative Private Limited (NBCL).

1.    M/s Samad Khan Babar
2.  M/s Aikes Private Limited

1.2     The technical bids were opened on June 12, 2015 in front of IBA officials and   Contractor’s representative. The technical bids were sent to M/s NBCL for their evaluation. As per technical evaluation both the contractors obtained the qualifying marks and stand technically qualified for the above subject work.

1.3     The technical evaluation was presented in Technical Evaluation Committee (Projects). The Committee approved the Technical Evaluation Report of M/s NBCL. The same was presented in Procurement Committee for their approval to open the financial bids. The Procurement Committee approved the financial bid opening.

1.4     Financial Bids were opened in front of IBA officials and contractor’s representative. The        amount of bids announced at the time of bidding was as follows:

 1.    M/s Smad Khan Babar             Rs.6,225,472/-
 2.   M/s Aikes                            Rs.5,640,122/-

1.5     Mr. Abdul Rehman reported that the financial bids were sent to Consultant M/s NBCL for scrutiny and verification who submitted their report and recommended M/s Aikes for award of work at a cost of Rs.5,640,122/- The case was presented in Procurement Committee on September 5, 2015 which recommended the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s Aikes at a cost of Rs.5,640,122/-

         

1.6     Mr. Abdul Rehman requested the Executive Committee to approve the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s Aikes at a cost of Rs.5,640,122/-

 

1.7     The Dean & Director observed that the cost was very high. Mr. Abdul Rehman said that earlier estimate was of Rs.10 million.  He said that Rs.3 million has been provided for this project in the budget. The Dean & Director asked as to how much time it would take to complete the project.  Mr. Abdul Rehman said that it would take 8 months time from the commencement date of the project.  The Dean & Director said that the balance amount can be paid from next year’s budget.

 

1.8     The Registrar suggested that an accurate and detailed time line should be obtained from the contractor. He further said that before the convocation a road from gate No.3 should be ready. He further said that during the execution of the project, proper security arrangement should be made.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

1.9     The Committee approved the award of work to the lowest bidder M/s Aikes at a cost of Rs.5,640,122/-

 

1.10    Mr. Abdul Rehman would obtain from the Contractor a detailed time line for completion of the project.

 

Item No.2     To consider the MNL1 Lab issue.

 

2.1     Mr. Sharaf Hussain said that there are complaints from faculty and students that most of the machines at MNL1 Lab are very slow.  He said that it is because they are using single user account, every student does not have individual account. Another issue is of security that if a student saves his data another student can have access and change or delete it because the same password and the same identity is used.

 

2.2     The Dean & Director said that it is very dangerous to have one common password for all those attending the lab.  Mr. Imran Batada clarified that it is only for logging in purpose and if the students work on ERP or LMS they use their own identity and password and that is secure.

 

2.3     Dr. Shakil Khoja said that there could be a security breach as anybody can make anonymous email using that login.  He said that students should have their own profile; there should not be general log in and general password. Mr. Wajeeh said that for him un-known devices are of more concern; usually a student carries two to three devices and if a student uses tab or smart phone the user cannot be identified.  Mr. Imran Batada said that currently they have given right on all devices but in future they would make a policy whereby only one device at a time can be used. He said that they are procuring a device which would be able to identify the user who logs in the system. Mr. Wajeeh said that they are working on individual profile starting with FCS students.  The Dean & Director instructed that by Spring 2016 all students should have their own roaming profiles, ids and passwords.

 

2.4     Mr. Imran Batada said that the issue of slow system performance needs to be resolved. The Dean & Director asked as to how performance can be enhanced. Mr. Wajeeh said that they are up-grading the labs. Dr. Shakil Khoja said that the two labs MNL1 and CCL are very old and they should be up-graded.  Mr. Imran Batada said that they have allocated amount in the budget for the up-gradation of these labs. He hoped that by January 2016 the labs would have been up-gradated.

 

2.5     Mr. Wajeeh Zaidi proposed that as a pilot project for two identified FCS courses they can create roaming profile and obtain the feedback of faculty and students.  Dr. Shakil Khoja said that the problem would not be resolved with this solution.  He suggested that we should have control on the system and there should not be generic uses. Mr. Imran Batada said that when they purchase the device they would present a policy to the EC for their approval.        

 

DECISION &ACTION

 

2.6     The Committee decided that for the FCS students, as a pilot project, roaming profile be created and at the same time feedback of the faculty and students would be obtained.

 

2.7     Mr. Wajeeh Zaidi and Mr. Imran Batada would implement the pilot project.

 

Item No.3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

                       a)           Proposal for charging the students for printing.
  

3.1     Dr. Shakil Khoja suggested that in order to reduce printing cost and to increase the uses of LMS, students should be charged some amount for printing.  He further said that for faculty also we can think of allowing certain number of pages in a semester.  Mr. Abdul Wajed said that students bring their own pages and are allowed to print 20 pages per day but it is not strictly checked.  He further suggested that for printing that does not require high tech computers some space outside the labs should be provided.

 

3.2     The Dean & Director said that A&FC has also shown concern on the high cost of printing and stationery. He said that IBA has made hefty investment on LMS but it is not fully used. He said that he would discuss this issue in the Academic Leadership Team (ALT) meeting.  Mr. Imran Batada suggested that we should also allot some quota for printing to staff also.   The Dean & Director said that we should have the printing data of students, faculty and staff and identify where the consumption is high. The Registrar advised Mr. Sharaf Husain to obtain data of how much printing was done and toners used in three months time in each lab.

 

DECISION & ACTION

 

3.3     The Committee requested Mr. Sharaf Hussain to obtain and present to the EC data of three months printing at each lab and its cost on toners.

 

 b)           Photographs of office bearers on the Portal.
       

3.4     The Dean & Director suggested that photographs of all office bearers of the students societies as well as all students should be available on the Portal.  Mr. Imran Batada said that photographs of students and faculty are available in the ERP.  

 

There being no other issue, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
