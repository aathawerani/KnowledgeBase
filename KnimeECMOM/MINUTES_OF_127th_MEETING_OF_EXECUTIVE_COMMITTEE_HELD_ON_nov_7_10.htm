MINUTES OF THE 127TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA HELD ON MONDAY, NOVEMBER 8, 2010

1.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                             Associate Dean

2.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                   Associate Dean

5.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                   Director ICT

6.      Ms. Ayesha A. Menai                               Director H.R.

 

Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah, Chairman, Library Committee attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following items were discussed:      

 

 

Item No. 1      To consider Status Reports on EC decisions:

 

1.1              The Committee considered the status reports on the EC decisions presented by the following:

 

DIRECTOR HR:

 

1.2              The Director H.R. with the Director ICT to work for enhancing the capacity of the existing ICT staff and to identify those who are trainable: The Director H.R. reported that she held meetings with Mr. Imran Batada and Mr. M. The Director ICT reported that he has started to provide training but a lot of training is needed.

REGISTRAR

1.12          Preparation of Maintenance Schedule: The Registrar stated that the preparation of maintenance manual is in hand.

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair. 

 

a)      Follow up on the decisions taken at the Director IBA meetings with IBA Staff.

 

3.1       Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah, Chairman, Library Committee presented the following status of the decisions related to Library taken at the meetings of the staff with Director IBA:

 

3.2       Libraries of both campuses to have trained front office staff, who would be able to respond to queries of students, faculty & staff: Partially Done. Morning sections of both the libraries have qualified front staff but afternoon sections of both the libraries have no such staff because of the shortage of trained staff.

 

3.3       Head Librarian will compile data on the use of library at IBA:  The Head Librarian has started this. It is in process.

 

3.4       Library will work on the concept of “Information Brokerage / Clearing House”:  This has been done. Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah informed the Committee that library staff have been trained in various disciplines and have been assigned as facilitator with the researchers/faculty members in searching latest research articles. He presented the name of the library staff, the researchers/faculty members and the subjects/areas of research.    

 

3.5       Library to order only new editions of books and journals:  This has been done. Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah stated that only new editions of books and journals are being ordered. He presented details of the books / journals ordered from August to October 2010. He said that request for old editions will be presented to the Library Committee for approval.

 

            The Director Projects suggested for departmental library particularly for Projects and Maintenance Department. However, Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah said that if the books are for the use of students then it can be procured and it will have to be in the library. 

 

            The Committee advised the Director Projects to procure the required books from Projects’ budget and in this case the Projects Department would be responsible for the maintenance of the books.  The Library can facilitate in procurement of the books.    

 

3.6       Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah stated that out of 23 library staff members, there are only 8 staff members who are properly qualified in Library Science. Out of 15 non-qualified staff members most of the staff members were transferred to library because they had not been performing their duties properly in their department.  He suggested that these staff members may be sent in batches to get library science qualification so that they become useful for library.  He said that library requires 16 qualified staff members with existing messengers and other lower staff to run the library properly. He said that they may surrender six non-qualified staff members if they get 8 additional properly qualified staff members.  

 

3.7       The Director H.R. requested Dr. Ahmed Ali Shah to nominate those library staff who need training in library science. She did not agree on hiring additional staff because the Director is not in favour of increasing headcount.

 

3.8       Dr. Muhammad Nishat suggested to hire interns (students of Library Sciences) from Karachi University. The members agreed with Dr. Nishat.

 

DECISION:

 

3.9       The up-dates along with comments given above were noted and approved.

 

 

a)      Rescheduling /re-conducting of classes that were scheduled on November 6, 2010 because of low attendance.

 

3.10     The Registrar informed the EC members that on account of burial of late Dr. Imran Farooq on Saturday, November 6, 2010, the attendance of both students and staff was very low. However, the IBA remained open and a few classes were held with low attendance. The Comprehensive Examination was also held in evening hours. Nevertheless, some faculty members have requested that a decision be taken as to whether it would be treated as a working day or observed as holiday particularly from the point of view of students’ attendance. The Registrar requested the Committee members to give a decision.

 

 

DECISION

 

3.11     Owing to the peculiar circumstances that prevailed on Saturday, November 6, 2010, the Committee decided to request all faculty members to reschedule / re-conduct the classes that were scheduled or held on November 6, 2010 preferably on next Sunday at a time convenient to them.

 

3.12     The Committee further decided that in order to avoid such situations in future, IBA should introduce an SMS Alert system for use by the faculty.

 

ACTION

 

3.13     Dr. Muhammad Nishat, Acting Director would request all faculty members to reschedule / re-conduct the classes that were scheduled or held on November 6, 2010 on next Sunday at a time convenient to them.

 

3.14     The Director ICT would arrange to introduce an SMS Alert system for use by the faculty.   

   

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
