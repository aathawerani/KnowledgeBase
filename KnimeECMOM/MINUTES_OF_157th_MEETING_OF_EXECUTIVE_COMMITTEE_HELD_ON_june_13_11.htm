MINUTES OF 157TH MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF IBA

HELD ON MONDAY, JUNE 13, 2011

 

The 157th meeting of the Executive Committee of IBA was held on Monday, June 13, 2011 at 9:30 a.m. at IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

1.      Dr. Ishrat Husain                                    Dean & Director

2.      Dr. Muhammad Nishat                             Associate Dean

3.      Dr. Sayeed Ghani                                   Associate Dean

4.      Capt. (Retd.) Ahmed Zaheer PN                Registrar

5.      Mr. Ali Taha                                          Director Finance

6.      Mr. Zafar A. Siddiqui                               Director CED

7.      Dr. Arshad Siddiqi                                   Director ICT

 

Dr. Nasir Touheed, Coordinator Testing attended the meeting on special invitation.

 

The following agenda items were discussed:    

 

Item No. 1      To consider and approve designing of external development works – IP based CCTV System.

 

1.1         The Director Projects in his email had stated that as a part of the designing of external development works, a meeting was held between M/s. S. Mehboob & Company (SMC), Consulting Engineers and IBA on 30th May, 2011 in the office of the Registrar to decide about the CCTV system. As per requirement of ICT, the Consultants M/s. SMC have recommended the CCTV to be IP based on the network of Fiber Optic Cable. The Director Projects stated that the Registrar was not in favor of IP based CCTV. So there was the point of difference, which needed to be resolved in the EC.  He requested the EC to decide if the CCTV system is to be based on conventional communication system or it should be IP based.

 

1.2       The Registrar in reply to the email of the Director Projects clarified that the meeting in the Registrar Office was held on his request followed by a visit of the Consultants and IBA security personnel to neighboring HEJ complex for seeing the 260 non-IP based surveillance network currently deployed at the HEJ complex. The Consultants were shown this as a specimen of what the Registrar Office had in mind for IBA.

 

1.3       The Registrar said that he was not in favor of IP based cameras because they are very costly.  He said that the non-IP based solution would cost around Rs.1 million whereas the IP-based solution would cost around Rs.3 million.

 

1.4       The Dean & Director wanted to know as to what would happen to the existing cameras already installed.

 

1.5       The Director ICT said that if they want to use RFID then the CCTV has to be IP based.  He clarified that the existing cameras would not fit into the ERP so it would become useless. 

 

1.6       The Dean & Director said that if the Director ICT wants IP based CCTV for security reasons, then he may install one camera at the entrance of each campus.

 

1.7       The Registrar said that he had seen an ad in which it was mentioned that 4 IP based WI FI cameras are available for Rs.95,000/- he suggested to get that and only selected cameras be connected to the nodes.

 

DECISION

 

1.8       On the advice of the Registrar it was decided that the Registrar, the Director Projects and the Director ICT would have a meeting and discuss the various options and report the outcome to the EC.

 

 

Item No. 2      Progress Report on Data Centre Project.

 

2.1       The Director ICT reported that they have prepared the Purchase Agreement and have sent it to the Director Projects to include their items but because of his illness, the Director Projects is unable to do that.

 

2.2       The Dean and Director observed that if the Director Projects is ill, his number two should do it.    

 

 

Item No. 3      Any other item with the permission of the Chair.

 

a)         To consider and approve revised rates for testing related activities.

 

3.1       Dr. Nasir Touheed presented a proposal for revision in rates for testing related activities which suggested around 10% increase in rates.

 

3.2       The Dean & Director analyzed the trend of revenue generated through testing activities during the last three years.  He observed that the reduction in the number of candidates for MBA program, has also affected the activity of the testing department.  He suggested to use the Testing Department as a profit centre and increase its capacity utilization to a maximum level. 

 

3.3       The Dean & Director said that the Head of Testing, like head of CED, should be aware of what is the income, expenditure and what the trend is. He said that if the income is exceeding the expenditure then increase in remuneration is justified. He also suggested to make facebook of the testing services.

 

3.4       Dr. Nasir Touheed said that last year OGDC wanted IBA to conduct their recruitment test @Rs.1050/- per candidate but IBA refused to work at such a low rate.

 

3.5       The Dean & Director said that if volumes are high then they should give discount.

 

DECISION

 

3.6       The Committee approved the revision in rates for testing related activities as proposed by Dr. Nasir Touheed.

 

ACTION

 

3.7       Dr. Nasir Touheed would try to achieve maximum capacity utilization of the Testing Department as a profit centre.   

 

 

b)         Renting out the IBA Auditorium (Main Campus)

 

3.8       The Registrar said that a few IBA alumni wanted to know if IBA can give auditorium on rent.

 

3.9       The Dean & Director said that they can give the IBA Main Campus auditorium on rent but not the APWA auditorium.  He suggested that one person should be given the responsibility of keeping track of the availability of the auditorium.

 

 

c)         One year’s Master Program.

 

3.10     The Dean & Director requested Dr. Muhammad Nishat to discuss with department chairs about one year’s Master Program.  He also requested Dr. Sayeed Ghani to discuss and design one year’s Master Program in the Academic Committee and present the recommendation of the Academic Committee before the Academic Board.   

 

There being no other issues, the meeting was concluded with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
