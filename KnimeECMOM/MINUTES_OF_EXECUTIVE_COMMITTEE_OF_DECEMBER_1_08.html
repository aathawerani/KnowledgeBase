MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE IBA HELD ON DECEMBER 1, 2008 (TWENTY FOURTH MEETING)

 

The 24th meeting of the Executive Committee of the IBA was held on Monday, December 1, 2008 at 9:30 a.m. at the IBA Main Campus. The following were present:

 

  1. Dr. Ishrat Husain                                Director
  2. Dr. Muhammad Nishat                         Professor
  3. Dr. Sayeed Ghani                               Chairman, CS & MIS
  4. Mr. Ali Taha                                      Director Finance
  5. Mr. Rehan-ul-Ambia Riaz                      Director Projects
  6. Dr. Arshad B. Siddiqi                           Director I.T.

The following items were discussed:

Item No. 1      To consider note dated November 29, 2008 from Director Finance regarding Online Charges to Si3

1.1              The Director Finance in his note of November 29, 2008 stated that the IBA opened an e-merchant bank account with Citibank in May 2008, mainly for the purpose to facilitate students / applicants to pay IBA fees ONLINE. The online payment system was supported by Si3. This system was launched by the approval of A&FC, specially for the applicants for IBA admissions tests in May / June 2008. The interface was developed by IBA-MIS team that worked well for IBA admission tests and an amount of Rs.110,631 was received from Online payments.  He stated that since that time the system remained in-active but incurring expenses as minimum monthly charges of Rs.4,950/- p.m. were charged by Si3. He said that till date an expense of Rs.77,879/- has been accumulated as Fee Collection Charges and an additional amount of Rs.20,000 would also be due as annual charges from Citibank for maintaining this facility. (The total expenses would be Rs.97,879/- against collection of Rs.110,631/-)

1.2              In the light of the above facts the Director Finance suggested that we should stop using ONLINE facility and thus IBA would be able to save Rs.80,000/- per year.

DECISION

1.3              The Committee decided that instead of stopping this facility the IBA should go for more automation and students should also be provided the facility of making payment of fees by using ONLINE facility.

ACTION

1.4              The Finance Department in coordination with I.T. Department would facilitate the students to pay their fees for Spring Semester and onward semesters through Online payment.

Item No. 2     To consider note of November 29, 2008 from Director Finance regarding disbursement of fund of Rs.247,870/- collected from employees salary for earthquake victims.

2.1              The Director Finance informed the Committee that the employees of IBA contributed Rs.247,870/- for the earthquake victims of Balochistan. He wanted to know as to whom the collected fund should be handed over.

DECISION

2.2       The Committee advised the Director Finance to donate the fund either to Edhi Trust Foundation or the Governor Balochistan Earthquake Relief Fund.

ACTION

2.3       The Director Finance will send the cheque for Rs.247,870/- to either of the two.

Item No. 3      To consider note from Director Finance regarding line of action for recovery of fee of Rs.6.5 million due from students.

3.1       The Director Finance raised the issue of recovery of fee of Rs.6.5 million due from the students.  He requested the Committee to decide the line of action for recovery of outstanding fee from the students.

DECISION

3.2       The Committee requested the Director Finance to write to the concerned students for payment of dues and advised to send a copy of the letter to concerned Program Office for placing in concerned student’s personal file.

3.3       The Committee further decided that from now onward the Finance Department will provide comprehensive details of dues recoverable from students to concerned Program Offices for their follow up with the students. The Committee also decided that the Finance Department would provide a statement of outstanding amount to Associate Deans for their information and suitable action.

ACTION

3.4       The Director Finance would ensure that letter for recovery of dues is sent to concerned students and a copy of the letter is sent to Program Office.

3.5       Director Finance would also ensure that a statement of outstanding fee from students is provided to Associate Deans.

 

Item No. 4      To consider note from Director I.T. regarding installation of Voice Backbone Cable at IBA City Campus.

4.1              The Director I.T. presented three quotations from (1) M/s. Avaya Business Partners, (2) Orient Communication, and (3) M/s. Sony Electronics for the installation of Voice Backbone Cable at IBA City Campus which, according to him, was damaged due to the neglect of the Contractor.

4.2              The Director Projects stated that neither the Contractor has accepted the responsibility for the damage of cable nor has it been established that the cable has been damaged by the Contractor as such the Contractor is not willing to bear the cost of installation of Voice Backbone Cable.   

DECISION

4.3       The Committee decided to award the job of installation of voice Backbone Cable at IBA City Campus to the lowest bidder i.e. M/s. Avaya Business Partner who have quoted Rs.183,695/- including all taxes.

ACTION

4.4       The Director I.T. will provide details / specifications of the work to the Purchase Officer for issuing Work Order to M/s. Avaya Business Partner for the installation of voice Backbone Cable at IBA City Campus.

Item No.  5     Reporting Items:

a)      Monthly Progress Report in Brief

5.1              The Director Projects presented Monthly Progress Report in brief for the month of November 2008 which was noted by the Committee.

There being no other issues, the meeting terminated with a vote of thanks to the Chair.
